List Of Top Jewelry Stores In Indianapolis References

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In search of the perfect piece of jewelry? Look no further than Indianapolis! The city boasts some of the top jewelry stores in the country, offering a wide range of styles and options to suit any taste and budget.

When it comes to finding the right jewelry store, many people struggle with knowing where to start. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to try and narrow down your choices to just a few. Additionally, many people worry about getting ripped off or ending up with a subpar piece of jewelry.

So, what should you look for when choosing a top jewelry store in Indianapolis? Ideally, you want to find a store that has a good reputation, knowledgeable staff, and a wide selection of high-quality jewelry. It's also important to consider factors like pricing, return policies, and any additional services or perks that the store may offer.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the top jewelry stores in Indianapolis and what sets them apart from the rest. Whether you're in the market for an engagement ring, a special occasion piece, or just something to treat yourself, these stores have got you covered.

Reis-Nichols

As one of the oldest and most respected jewelry stores in Indianapolis, Reis-Nichols is a top choice for many shoppers. The store offers a wide range of high-end jewelry brands, including Rolex, David Yurman, and Mikimoto, as well as their own exclusive Reis-Nichols collections. They also provide services like custom design and jewelry repair, making them a one-stop-shop for all your jewelry needs.

Moyer Fine Jewelers

Located in Carmel, Moyer Fine Jewelers is another top choice for those looking for high-quality jewelry in Indianapolis. The store offers a range of luxury brands like Cartier, John Hardy, and Roberto Coin, as well as their own Moyer Fine Jewelers designs. They also offer services like custom design and jewelry repair, as well as financing options to help make your purchase more affordable.

Distinctive Diamonds

If you're looking for an engagement ring or wedding band, Distinctive Diamonds is a great place to start. The store specializes in diamond jewelry and offers a wide range of engagement ring styles, including vintage-inspired and contemporary designs. They also offer financing options and a lifetime diamond upgrade program, making it easy to find the perfect ring for your budget and preferences.

Koehn & Koehn Jewelers

For those who prefer a more personalized shopping experience, Koehn & Koehn Jewelers is a great option. The family-owned store has been serving the Indianapolis area since 1957 and is known for their exceptional customer service and attention to detail. They offer a range of high-quality jewelry brands, as well as custom design and heirloom restoration services.

Question and Answer

Q: Do these stores offer financing options?

A: Yes, all of the stores mentioned in this article offer financing options to help make your purchase more affordable.

Q: Can I get my jewelry appraised at these stores?

A: Yes, all of the stores mentioned in this article offer jewelry appraisal services.

Q: Do these stores offer custom design services?

A: Yes, all of the stores mentioned in this article offer custom design services for those looking for a more personalized piece of jewelry.

Q: Do these stores offer any additional services or perks?

A: Yes, many of these stores offer additional services like jewelry repair, cleaning, and maintenance, as well as perks like lifetime diamond upgrades and financing options.

Conclusion of Top Jewelry Stores in Indianapolis

Whether you're in the market for an engagement ring, a special occasion piece, or just something to treat yourself, Indianapolis has a wide range of top jewelry stores to choose from. By considering factors like reputation, selection, and services offered, you can find a store that meets your needs and helps you find the perfect piece of jewelry.
