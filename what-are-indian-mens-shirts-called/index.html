What Are Indian Men s Shirts Called

Written by Frank Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India is known for its rich culture and traditional clothing. Indian men's shirts are no exception. They are unique in design, texture, and color. However, many people are unaware of what these shirts are called. In this tutorial blog post, we will explore the answer to the question, "What are Indian men's shirts called?"

For those who are not familiar with Indian clothing, it can be challenging to know what to look for when searching for Indian men's shirts. Moreover, the terminology may be confusing, and it can be challenging to find the right type of shirt that fits your needs, occasion, and style.

The answer to the question, "What are Indian men's shirts called?" is Kurta. A kurta is a traditional Indian shirt worn by men. It is loose-fitting, long-sleeved, and typically made of cotton or silk. Kurta has a collarless design and may have a button placket or a slit neckline. It is often paired with loose-fitting pants called pajamas or churidar.

In summary, Indian men's shirts are called Kurta. They are traditional shirts that have a unique design and are made of cotton or silk. Kurta is often paired with loose-fitting pants called pajamas or churidar.

What is Kurta?

Kurta is a popular traditional Indian shirt that is worn by men. It is comfortable, versatile, and stylish. Kurta comes in various designs, colors, and patterns, making it suitable for different occasions, seasons, and styles. I remember my grandfather wearing a Kurta on special occasions and festivals. It was a symbol of tradition and culture.

Types of Kurta

There are various types of Kurta that you can choose from:

Straight Kurta

A straight kurta is a basic design that is simple and elegant. It has a straight cut and is suitable for casual or formal occasions.

How to Wear a Straight Kurta?

You can wear a straight kurta with jeans or chinos for a casual look. For a formal look, pair it with pajamas or churidar.

Angrakha Kurta

Angrakha Kurta has a unique design that is inspired by the traditional Rajasthani dress. It has a wrap-around style and is secured by a tie or a button. It is suitable for festivals or weddings.

How to Wear Angrakha Kurta?

Angrakha Kurta looks best when paired with churidar or pajamas. It can be worn with or without a jacket.

Pathani Kurta

Pathani Kurta is a popular design that is inspired by the traditional dress of the Pathan community. It has a loose-fitting design and is suitable for casual or formal occasions.

How to Wear Pathani Kurta?

Pathani Kurta looks great when paired with jeans or chinos. For a formal look, pair it with pajamas or churidar.

Why Should You Wear Kurta?

Kurta is a comfortable and stylish shirt that is suitable for different occasions. It is perfect for festivals, weddings, casual outings, or formal events. Kurta comes in various designs and colors, making it easy to find the perfect match for your style and personality. Wearing a Kurta is a great way to embrace Indian culture and tradition.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the difference between Kurta and Shirt?

A. Kurta is a traditional Indian shirt that has a unique design and is made of cotton or silk. It is loose-fitting, long-sleeved, and typically collarless. On the other hand, a shirt is a western-style shirt that is usually made of cotton or polyester.

Q. Can I wear Kurta to work?

A. Kurta is suitable for casual or formal occasions, making it perfect for work. You can pair it with pajamas or churidar for a formal look or jeans/chinos for a casual look.

Q. What is the best fabric for Kurta?

A. Cotton and silk are the best fabrics for Kurta. They are comfortable, breathable, and suitable for different occasions.

Q. How do I choose the right Kurta?

A. When choosing a Kurta, consider the occasion, style, and comfort. Look for a design that suits your personality and body type. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and style.

Kurta is a traditional Indian shirt that is popular among men. It is comfortable, stylish, and suitable for different occasions.
