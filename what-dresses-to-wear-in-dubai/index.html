What Dresses To Wear In Dubai Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning a trip to Dubai and wondering what dresses to pack? Dubai is a modern and cosmopolitan city, but it's also a Muslim country with conservative clothing standards. As a visitor, it's important to respect the local culture and dress appropriately. In this guide, we'll show you what dresses to wear in Dubai and how to stay stylish while staying modest.

If you're not familiar with the local culture and customs, it can be challenging to figure out what dresses to wear in Dubai. You don't want to offend anyone, but you also want to feel comfortable and stylish. The good news is that there are plenty of options that are both modest and fashionable. It's all about finding the right balance.

So, what dresses should you pack for your trip to Dubai? The most important thing to keep in mind is to dress modestly. This means covering your shoulders, knees, and cleavage. You don't have to wear a burqa or an abaya (the long black dress worn by some Muslim women), but you should avoid anything too revealing or tight.

Maxi Dresses

Maxi dresses are a perfect choice for Dubai's warm weather. They're comfortable, stylish, and most importantly, modest. They cover your legs and arms while still allowing you to stay cool. You can choose from a variety of styles, including flowy, bohemian, or structured. Pair your maxi dress with sandals or flats for a chic and effortless look.

Midi Dresses

Midi dresses are another great option for Dubai. They're longer than a typical knee-length dress, but shorter than a maxi dress. Midi dresses come in a variety of styles, from casual to dressy, so you can find one that suits your needs. You can pair your midi dress with heels or flats, depending on the occasion.

Long-Sleeved Dresses

If you're looking for something a little more covered up, a long-sleeved dress is a great choice. You can find long-sleeved dresses in a variety of styles, from casual to formal. They're perfect for slightly cooler weather or for air-conditioned indoor spaces. Pair your long-sleeved dress with flats or heels, depending on the occasion.

Kaftan Dresses

Kaftan dresses are a traditional Middle Eastern garment that has become popular in the fashion world. They're loose, flowy, and perfect for Dubai's warm weather. Kaftans come in a variety of styles, from simple to embellished, so you can find one that fits your personal style. You can pair your kaftan dress with sandals or flats for a casual look, or dress it up with heels for a more formal occasion.

Q&A

Q: Can I wear shorts in Dubai?

A: While shorts are technically allowed, they're not considered appropriate in many public places, especially for women. It's best to opt for longer pants or skirts instead.

Q: Can I wear a bikini to the beach?

A: Yes, you can wear a bikini to the beach, but it's best to choose a more modest style. Avoid anything too revealing or skimpy, and cover up with a sarong or cover-up when you're not in the water.

Q: Can I wear sleeveless tops in Dubai?

A: Sleeveless tops are generally not recommended, especially for women. It's best to choose tops with sleeves or bring a lightweight jacket or cardigan to wear over your top.

Q: Can I wear high heels in Dubai?

A: Yes, you can wear high heels in Dubai, but keep in mind that you may be doing a lot of walking, so comfortable shoes are recommended. It's also a good idea to bring a pair of flats or sandals for when your feet need a break.

Conclusion of What Dresses to Wear in Dubai

When it comes to what dresses to wear in Dubai, it's all about finding the right balance between style and modesty. Choose dresses that cover your shoulders, knees, and cleavage, and avoid anything too revealing or tight. Maxi dresses, midi dresses, long-sleeved dresses, and kaftan dresses are all great options that will keep you cool, comfortable, and stylish during your trip to Dubai. Remember to always respect the local culture and customs, and you'll have a wonderful and memorable trip.
