Who is the Top Richest Boxer in the World 2021?

As of 2021, the top richest boxer in the world is Floyd Mayweather Jr. with a net worth of $450 million. Mayweather has earned his wealth through a combination of his successful boxing career and his savvy business ventures. He has won 50 fights, including 27 knockouts, and has never been defeated in a professional fight. In addition to his boxing career, Mayweather has also made a significant amount of money through endorsements, sponsorships, and his own promotions company, Mayweather Promotions.

Mayweather's success can be attributed to his incredible work ethic and dedication to his craft. He has always been known for his rigorous training regimens and his ability to outwork his opponents in the ring. He has also been able to capitalize on his success by investing in real estate, businesses, and other ventures that have helped him build his wealth.

The Rise of Anthony Joshua

Anthony Joshua is a British boxer who has quickly risen to become one of the top boxers in the world. He currently holds three of the four major world titles in the heavyweight division, and his net worth is estimated to be around $80 million. Joshua's success can be attributed to his incredible talent and dedication to his craft.
