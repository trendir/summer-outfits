How Much Do Product Designers Make In India

Written by Eveline Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you curious about how much product designers make in India? It's a common question and a valid concern for those considering a career in design. As a product designer, you want to be compensated fairly for your skills and experience. In this article, we'll explore the average salary for product designers in India, factors that impact salary, and how to negotiate a higher salary.

Product design is a highly sought-after profession that requires creativity, technical skills, and a deep understanding of user experience. However, the pay scale for product designers in India can vary widely depending on factors such as industry, company size, location, and experience.

According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a product designer in India is ₹1,151,251 per year. However, salaries can range from ₹413,000 to ₹2,687,000 per year, depending on the factors mentioned above.

In summary, the factors that impact how much product designers make in India include industry, company size, location, and experience. It's important to research these factors and negotiate your salary accordingly.

Factors that Impact Product Designer Salaries in India

One factor that can impact product designer salaries in India is the industry they work in. For example, product designers working in the software industry may earn more than those working in manufacturing. Company size can also make a difference, as larger companies may be able to offer higher salaries than smaller ones.

Location is another important factor to consider. Product designers working in major cities like Bangalore, Mumbai, and Delhi may earn more than those working in smaller cities or rural areas. Experience is also a significant factor, as more experienced product designers are likely to earn higher salaries than those just starting out.

Negotiating a Higher Salary as a Product Designer in India

If you're a product designer in India and you're looking to negotiate a higher salary, there are a few things you can do. First, research the average salary for your position and experience level in your industry and location. This will give you an idea of what you can realistically expect to earn.

Next, be prepared to make a case for why you deserve a higher salary. Outline your skills and experience, and be specific about the value you bring to the company. Finally, be willing to negotiate and compromise. Your employer may not be able to meet your salary demands, but they may be able to offer other benefits or incentives.

Additional Tips for Product Designers in India

Aside from negotiating your salary, there are other steps you can take to increase your earning potential as a product designer in India. Consider expanding your skillset by learning new design software or taking courses in related fields like user experience or web design. Networking is also important, as it can help you connect with potential employers and clients.

Personal Experience

As a product designer working in Bangalore, I have found that salaries can vary widely depending on the company and industry. While some companies may offer salaries on the lower end of the scale, others are willing to pay top dollar for experienced designers with in-demand skills. It's important to do your research, negotiate effectively, and continue to develop your skills to increase your earning potential.
