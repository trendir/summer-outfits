Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between basic tees and performance tees?

A: Basic tees are made from a standard cotton material that is soft and comfortable. Performance tees are made from a moisture-wicking material that is designed to keep you cool and dry during physical activity.

Q: Are wholesale t shirts more affordable than retail t shirts?

A: Yes, wholesale t shirts are typically more affordable than retail t shirts because you are buying in bulk.

Q: Can I order custom wholesale t shirts?

A: Yes, many wholesale t shirt brands offer custom printing services. You can add your own design or logo to the shirts for a personalized touch.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale t shirts?

A: The minimum order quantity for wholesale t shirts varies depending on the brand and the supplier. However, most brands require a minimum order of at least 12 shirts.
