What Shoes To Wear With A Black Maxi Dress

Written by Bardi May 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect shoes to wear with your black maxi dress? Do you want to complete your look but don't know where to start? Finding the right shoes can be a challenge, especially when it comes to a black maxi dress. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

The key to finding the perfect shoes to wear with a black maxi dress is to consider the occasion and the style of the dress. You want to choose shoes that complement the dress without overpowering it. You also want to make sure that the shoes are comfortable enough to wear for an extended period of time.

When it comes to choosing shoes to wear with a black maxi dress, there are a few options that work well. You can go for a classic look with a pair of black heels or sandals. You can also opt for a pop of color with a pair of red or blue heels. Another option is to choose a pair of statement shoes with bold prints or embellishments.

In summary, when it comes to what shoes to wear with a black maxi dress, keep in mind the occasion and the style of the dress. Choose shoes that complement the dress without overpowering it, and make sure they are comfortable enough to wear for an extended period of time.

Classic Black Heels or Sandals

When in doubt, a classic pair of black heels or sandals is always a safe option to wear with a black maxi dress. Black shoes complement the dress without taking away from the overall look. Whether you opt for a strappy sandal or a closed-toe pump, black shoes are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Personally, I love pairing my black maxi dress with a pair of black strappy heels. They add a touch of elegance to the dress and elongate my legs. Plus, they go with just about everything in my wardrobe!

Pop of Color

If you're feeling bold, a pop of color can add some excitement to your black maxi dress. Red or blue heels are a great option to make a statement while still keeping the focus on the dress. Just make sure the color you choose complements the dress and doesn't clash with it.

I recently wore a pair of red heels with my black maxi dress to a wedding, and I received so many compliments on my outfit. The pop of color added some fun to the overall look and made me feel confident and stylish.

Statement Shoes

If you really want to make a statement, consider wearing a pair of shoes with bold prints or embellishments. This can include animal print, metallic accents, or even glitter. Just make sure the shoes don't overpower the dress and keep the rest of your accessories minimal.

Personally, I love wearing my black maxi dress with a pair of leopard print heels. They add some personality to the outfit and make it stand out. Plus, they're comfortable enough to wear all night long.

Comfort is Key

When it comes down to it, comfort is the most important factor when choosing shoes to wear with a black maxi dress. Make sure the shoes you choose are comfortable enough to wear for an extended period of time. This is especially important if you'll be standing or walking for long periods of time.

One tip I have is to break in your shoes before the event. Wear them around the house for a few hours to make sure they're comfortable and won't cause any blisters or sore feet.

Conclusion of What Shoes to Wear with a Black Maxi Dress

When it comes to what shoes to wear with a black maxi dress, there are a few options that work well. Classic black heels or sandals are always a safe option, while a pop of color can add some excitement to the outfit. Statement shoes with bold prints or embellishments can make a statement, but make sure they don't overpower the dress. And most importantly, make sure the shoes you choose are comfortable enough to wear for an extended period of time.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear flats with a black maxi dress?

A: Yes, you can wear flats with a black maxi dress. Just make sure they complement the dress and don't make it look too casual.

Q: What type of sandals should I wear with a black maxi dress?

A: Strappy sandals or gladiator sandals are a great option to wear with a black maxi dress. They add some interest to the outfit while still keeping it casual.

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a black maxi dress?

A: You can wear sneakers with a black maxi dress, but it's important to choose the right type of sneakers. Go for a pair of white or black sneakers that are clean and minimalistic.

Q: Is it okay to wear wedges with a black maxi dress?

A: Yes, wedges are a great option to wear with a black maxi dress. They add some height and elongate your legs while still being comfortable to wear.
