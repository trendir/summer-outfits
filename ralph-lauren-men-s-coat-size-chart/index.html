How to Measure for the Ralph Lauren Men's Coat Size Chart

Measuring for the Ralph Lauren Men's Coat Size Chart is simple. You will need a measuring tape and someone to assist you. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Chest

Wrap the measuring tape around the widest part of your chest, under your armpits, and over your shoulder blades. Make sure that the tape is snug but not tight.

Step 2: Measure Your Waist

Wrap the measuring tape around your natural waistline, which is usually an inch or two above your belly button. Again, make sure that the tape is snug but not tight.

Step 3: Measure Your Sleeve Length

Start at the center back of your neck and measure across your shoulder and down to your wrist. Make sure that your arm is relaxed at your side when taking this measurement.

Tips for Using the Ralph Lauren Men's Coat Size Chart

Here are a few tips for using the Ralph Lauren Men's Coat Size Chart:

  • Measure yourself or have someone else do it for you. This ensures accurate measurements.
  • Take your measurements in inches.
  • When in doubt, size up. It's easier to make a larger coat fit than a smaller one.
  • Refer to the size chart for each specific style of coat. Different styles may have different fits.
