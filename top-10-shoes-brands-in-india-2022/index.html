Question and Answer

Q: How can I choose the right size of shoes?

A: It is important to measure your feet before buying shoes to ensure that you get the right size. You can do this by tracing your feet on a piece of paper and measuring the length and width. It is also a good idea to try on shoes in-store to ensure a proper fit.

Q: How often should I replace my shoes?

A: It is recommended to replace your shoes every 300-500 miles or every 6-8 months, whichever comes first. However, this can vary depending on the type of shoe and how often you wear them.

Q: How can I maintain the quality of my shoes?

A: To maintain the quality of your shoes, it is important to clean them regularly and store them properly. Avoid wearing them in extreme weather conditions and rotate your shoes to prevent excessive wear and tear.

Q: Can I wear sports shoes for everyday wear?

A: Yes, you can wear sports shoes for everyday wear, as long as they are comfortable and provide the necessary support to your feet. However, it is important to choose the right type of sports shoe for your needs.
