High End Men s Dress Shoe Brands A Guide To Finding The Perfect Pair

Are you tired of buying cheap dress shoes that fall apart after just a few wears? Do you want to invest in a high-quality pair of shoes that will last for years and make you look and feel your best? If so, then it's time to start exploring the world of high end men's dress shoe brands.

Many men make the mistake of skimping on their dress shoes, thinking that they're just an afterthought to their overall outfit. However, your shoes are one of the most important parts of your wardrobe, and they can make or break your entire look. Not only that, but wearing cheap, uncomfortable shoes can also cause pain and discomfort, leading to long-term foot problems.

If you want to look and feel your best, then it's worth investing in a high end men's dress shoe brand. These shoes are made from the highest quality materials, are designed for both style and comfort, and will last for years with proper care and maintenance.

In this article, we'll take a look at some of the top high end men's dress shoe brands on the market today, and explore what makes them so special. Whether you're looking for a classic oxford, a sleek loafer, or a stylish boot, there's a high end brand that's perfect for you.

Allen Edmonds

My personal favorite high end men's dress shoe brand is Allen Edmonds. This American company has been around since 1922, and is known for its timeless designs, exceptional craftsmanship, and high-quality materials.

I first discovered Allen Edmonds when I was looking for a new pair of dress shoes for a job interview. I had always worn cheap, uncomfortable shoes in the past, and didn't realize what a difference a quality pair of shoes could make. When I tried on my first pair of Allen Edmonds, I was blown away by how comfortable they were. Not only that, but they looked amazing and made me feel confident and polished.

What sets Allen Edmonds apart from other high end men's dress shoe brands is their commitment to quality and craftsmanship. Each shoe is made by hand in the United States, using only the finest materials, including full-grain leather and cork footbeds. They also offer a wide range of sizes and widths, so you can find a shoe that fits your foot perfectly.

Church's

Another top high end men's dress shoe brand is Church's, a British company that has been around since 1873. Church's is known for its classic, elegant designs, and its commitment to using only the finest materials.

I first discovered Church's when I was living in London, and was immediately drawn to their sleek, sophisticated oxfords. I purchased my first pair and was blown away by how comfortable they were, even after wearing them all day. The leather was soft and supple, and the shoes were clearly made to last.

One thing that sets Church's apart from other high end men's dress shoe brands is their attention to detail. Each shoe is handcrafted in England, using traditional techniques that have been passed down through generations. They also offer a wide range of styles and colors, so you can find a shoe that fits your personal style.

John Lobb

For the ultimate in luxury and craftsmanship, look no further than John Lobb. This British company has been around since 1866, and is known for its impeccable attention to detail and use of the finest materials.

I first discovered John Lobb when I was shopping for a pair of dress shoes for my wedding. I wanted something truly special and luxurious, and John Lobb did not disappoint. The shoes were handcrafted from the finest leather, and featured intricate details that were truly breathtaking.

What sets John Lobb apart from other high end men's dress shoe brands is their commitment to excellence. Each shoe is made by hand, taking over 190 steps to complete. They also offer a bespoke service, where you can have a shoe made specifically for your foot, ensuring the perfect fit.

How to Care for Your High End Men's Dress Shoes

Once you've invested in a pair of high end men's dress shoes, it's important to take care of them properly to ensure they last for years. Here are some tips for caring for your shoes:

  • Invest in a good quality shoe horn to help you put your shoes on without damaging the backs.
  • Use shoe trees to help your shoes maintain their shape and prevent creases.
  • Clean your shoes regularly with a soft brush and mild soap, and condition them with leather conditioner to keep the leather supple.
  • Avoid wearing the same pair of shoes two days in a row, as this can cause the leather to stretch and lose its shape.
  • Rotate your shoes regularly to give them time to rest and recover between wears.

Conclusion of High End Men's Dress Shoe Brands

If you're looking for a high quality, stylish, and comfortable pair of dress shoes, then it's worth investing in a high end men's dress shoe brand. Whether you choose Allen Edmonds, Church's, John Lobb, or another top brand, you can be sure that you're getting a shoe that's made from the finest materials and designed to last for years.
