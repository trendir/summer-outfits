What to Wear to a Barn Wedding 2020: A Complete Guide

Barn weddings have become increasingly popular in recent years, providing a rustic and charming backdrop for couples to tie the knot. However, dressing for a barn wedding can be a challenge, with the need to balance style and comfort while also considering the practicalities of a rural setting. If you're wondering what to wear to a barn wedding in 2020, read on for our complete guide.

When it comes to dressing for a barn wedding, the key is to strike the right balance between chic and practical. You want to look stylish and put-together, but also be comfortable and able to move around easily. Additionally, you need to consider the weather and terrain of a rural setting, meaning that high heels and tight-fitting clothing may not be the best choice.

Choosing Your Outfit

When selecting your outfit for a barn wedding, it's important to choose fabrics and styles that are comfortable and practical. Opt for breathable fabrics such as cotton or linen, and choose shoes that are both stylish and sturdy, such as ankle boots or sandals with a chunky heel. Dresses and skirts are a popular choice for female guests, but make sure to choose a length that's appropriate for the setting - knee-length or midi dresses are ideal. For male guests, a smart-casual outfit consisting of chinos or dress pants paired with a button-down shirt or polo shirt is a safe bet.

Accessorizing Your Outfit

Accessories can help to elevate your barn wedding outfit, adding a touch of glamour and sophistication. However, it's important not to go overboard - choose one or two statement pieces that complement your outfit, rather than overwhelming it. For women, a simple clutch bag and a pair of statement earrings can help to dress up a simple dress, while for men, a stylish watch or a patterned tie can add a pop of color to an otherwise understated outfit.

Makeup and Hair

When it comes to makeup and hair for a barn wedding, the key is to keep it natural and effortless. Opt for a dewy, fresh-faced look with minimal eye makeup and a natural lip color. Loose waves or a simple updo are ideal for a rustic setting, while a messy bun or braids can help to keep your hair out of your face while also looking stylish.

Dressing for the Weather

As with any outdoor wedding, it's important to dress for the weather when attending a barn wedding. If the wedding is taking place in the summer, choose lightweight fabrics and breathable clothing to stay cool, and don't forget to bring a hat or sunglasses to protect yourself from the sun. In colder months, layering is key - opt for a cozy sweater or jacket that can be easily removed if you get too warm.

Personal Experience

I recently attended a barn wedding and opted for a knee-length floral dress paired with ankle boots and a denim jacket. I accessorized with a simple clutch bag and a pair of statement earrings, and kept my makeup and hair natural and effortless. The outfit was comfortable and practical, while also feeling stylish and put-together.

My partner opted for a pair of dress pants paired with a button-down shirt and a patterned tie, with ankle boots completing the look. The outfit was smart-casual and appropriate for the setting, while also allowing him to move around easily and stay comfortable throughout the day.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear high heels to a barn wedding?

A: While it's not impossible to wear high heels to a barn wedding, it's generally not recommended. The terrain of a rural setting can be uneven and unpredictable, making it difficult to walk in high heels. Opt for sturdy shoes with a chunky heel or flats instead.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a barn wedding?

A: While jeans can be dressed up for a smart-casual look, they may not be appropriate for a barn wedding. Opt for dress pants or chinos paired with a button-down shirt or polo shirt instead.

Q: Do I need to wear a hat to a barn wedding?

A: While a hat can be a stylish addition to a barn wedding outfit, it's not a necessity. Consider the weather and your personal style when deciding whether or not to wear a hat.

Q: Can I wear a maxi dress to a barn wedding?

A: While a maxi dress can be a stylish choice for a barn wedding, it's important to consider the length and style of the dress. Opt for a maxi dress with a flowing, bohemian feel, and avoid anything too tight or revealing.

Conclusion

When it comes to dressing for a barn wedding in 2020, the key is to strike the right balance between style and practicality. Choose comfortable, breathable fabrics and sturdy shoes, accessorize with statement pieces, and keep your hair and makeup natural and effortless. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to look stylish and put-together while also feeling comfortable and able to move around easily.
