Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I care for my Eddie Bauer down jacket?

It's recommended that you wash your jacket in a front-loading washing machine using cold water and a mild detergent. Tumble dry the jacket on low heat with a few tennis balls to help fluff up the down.

2. How warm is the Eddie Bauer down jacket?

The warmth of the jacket depends on the type of insulation used. Eddie Bauer down jackets are available in different insulation levels, ranging from 550-fill to 850-fill down, which provides warmth in different temperature ranges.

3. Are Eddie Bauer down jackets waterproof?

Most Eddie Bauer down jackets are water-resistant, which means they can withstand light rain or snow. However, they are not completely waterproof, and they may not hold up in heavy rain or prolonged exposure to water.

4. Can I wear my Eddie Bauer down jacket in extreme temperatures?

Eddie Bauer down jackets are designed to provide warmth in freezing temperatures. However, it's important to choose the right insulation level for the temperature range you'll be in. If you'll be in extremely cold temperatures, consider investing in a jacket with a higher fill power.
