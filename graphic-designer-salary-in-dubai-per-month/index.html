Graphic Designer Salary In Dubai Per Month A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a graphic designer based in Dubai and wondering how much you should be earning per month? Do you want to negotiate a better salary with your employer but don't know where to start? Look no further, as this comprehensive guide will provide you with all the information you need to know about graphic designer salary in Dubai per month.

The average salary for a graphic designer in Dubai is around AED 5,000 to AED 20,000 per month, depending on the level of experience, skills, and industry. However, many graphic designers may feel that their salary does not reflect their hard work and dedication, which can lead to frustration and demotivation.

So, what can you do to ensure that you are getting paid what you deserve as a graphic designer in Dubai?

Firstly, it is important to understand the factors that determine your salary, such as your level of experience, skills, education, and the industry you work in. It is also important to research the average salary for graphic designers in Dubai and compare it to what you are currently earning.

In addition, you can negotiate your salary with your employer by highlighting your achievements, skills, and contributions to the company. You can also provide evidence of your market value by researching the salaries of other graphic designers in your industry and presenting this information to your employer.

Personal Experience with Graphic Designer Salary in Dubai per Month

As a graphic designer based in Dubai, I have experienced the frustration of feeling underpaid for my work. However, I have also learned the importance of advocating for myself and negotiating my salary with my employer.

I have found that highlighting my achievements and contributions to the company, as well as providing evidence of my market value, has helped me to negotiate a better salary. It is also important to have an open and honest conversation with your employer about your expectations, goals, and concerns.

Market Trends for Graphic Designer Salary in Dubai per Month

The market trends for graphic designer salary in Dubai per month vary depending on the industry. For example, graphic designers in the advertising and marketing industry may earn higher salaries compared to those in the education or non-profit sector.

In addition, the demand for graphic designers in Dubai is increasing, which can lead to higher salaries and better job opportunities. However, it is important to stay up-to-date with the latest trends, technologies, and skills in the industry to remain competitive and marketable.

Factors that Determine Graphic Designer Salary in Dubai per Month

The factors that determine graphic designer salary in Dubai per month include:

- Level of experience

- Skills and expertise

- Education and qualifications

- Industry and company

- Market demand and trends

How to Negotiate a Better Graphic Designer Salary in Dubai per Month

To negotiate a better graphic designer salary in Dubai per month, you can follow these steps:

1. Research the average salary for graphic designers in your industry and compare it to what you are currently earning.

2. Highlight your achievements, skills, and contributions to the company.

3. Provide evidence of your market value by researching the salaries of other graphic designers in your industry and presenting this information to your employer.

4. Have an open and honest conversation with your employer about your expectations, goals, and concerns.

Conclusion of Graphic Designer Salary in Dubai per Month

Overall, graphic designer salary in Dubai per month can vary depending on various factors such as experience, skills, education, and industry. However, by advocating for yourself and negotiating your salary with your employer, you can ensure that you are getting paid what you deserve as a graphic designer in Dubai.
