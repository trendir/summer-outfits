Where To Shop For Men s Shorts The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is here and it's time to update your wardrobe with some stylish shorts! But where can you find the best place to shop for men's shorts? Don't worry, we've got you covered. Read on to discover our top picks for the best places to shop for men's shorts.

When it comes to shopping for men's shorts, there are a few pain points that come to mind. One of the biggest is finding the right fit. Men come in all shapes and sizes, and it can be difficult to find shorts that fit just right. Another pain point is finding shorts that are both stylish and comfortable. No one wants to sacrifice style for comfort, or vice versa. Lastly, it can be overwhelming to navigate through all the different stores and brands, trying to find the perfect pair of shorts.

So, what is the best place to shop for men's shorts? The answer may depend on your personal style and budget, but we've narrowed down the top choices for you to consider.

Here are our top picks for the best place to shop for men's shorts:

1. Nordstrom

If you're looking for a high-end shopping experience, Nordstrom is the place to go. They offer a wide variety of men's shorts from top brands like Nike, Bonobos, and Patagonia. You'll find everything from classic chino shorts to trendy printed shorts. Plus, Nordstrom has a great return policy, so if you don't love your purchase, you can easily return it.

2. J.Crew

J.Crew is a great option for those looking for classic, preppy styles. They offer a wide range of shorts in various fabrics and colors, from cotton chino shorts to lightweight linen shorts. J.Crew also offers a variety of fits, including slim and regular, so you can find the perfect fit for your body type.

3. ASOS

If you're on a budget, ASOS is a great place to shop for men's shorts. They offer a wide selection of affordable shorts in various styles, including cargo shorts, denim shorts, and athletic shorts. ASOS also offers free shipping and returns, which makes it easy to try out different styles and sizes without any risk.

4. Amazon

Amazon may not be the first place you think of when it comes to shopping for men's shorts, but they actually have a great selection of affordable shorts from top brands like Levi's and Dockers. Plus, with Amazon Prime, you can get free two-day shipping on many items, which is a huge bonus.

5. Your Local Boutique

Don't forget to check out your local boutique for unique and stylish shorts. You may be able to find a one-of-a-kind pair of shorts that no one else has. Plus, shopping locally supports small businesses in your community.

How to Choose the Best Place to Shop for Men's Shorts

When it comes to choosing the best place to shop for men's shorts, there are a few factors to consider. First, think about your personal style and what type of shorts you're looking for. Do you want classic chino shorts or something more trendy? Second, consider your budget. Some stores, like Nordstrom, may have higher price points, while others, like ASOS, offer more affordable options. Lastly, think about the store's return policy. If you're not sure about a purchase, it's always good to have the option to return or exchange it.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some popular styles of men's shorts?

A: Some popular styles of men's shorts include chino shorts, cargo shorts, athletic shorts, and denim shorts.

Q: What brands offer high-quality men's shorts?

A: Some brands that offer high-quality men's shorts include Nike, Bonobos, Patagonia, J.Crew, and Levi's.

Q: How can I find the right fit for men's shorts?

A: It's important to know your measurements and pay attention to the fit guide provided by the store. Some stores, like J.Crew, offer multiple fits, so you can find the perfect fit for your body type.

Q: What should I look for in a good pair of men's shorts?

A: Look for shorts that are comfortable, stylish, and made from high-quality materials. It's also important to consider the fit and the length of the shorts.

Conclusion of Best Place to Shop for Men's Shorts

When it comes to shopping for men's shorts, there are plenty of options out there. Whether you're looking for high-end designer shorts or affordable options, there's a store out there for you. Consider your personal style, budget, and the store's return policy when making your decision. With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect pair of shorts for summer!
