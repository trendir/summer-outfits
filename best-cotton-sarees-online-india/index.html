How to Care for Your Cotton Saree

Caring for your cotton saree is crucial to ensure its longevity and quality. Here are a few tips to help you care for your cotton saree:

  • Hand wash the saree with a mild detergent
  • Do not wring or twist the saree while washing
  • Dry the saree in shade and iron it while it is still slightly damp
  • Store the saree in a cool, dry place to prevent mildew and discoloration
