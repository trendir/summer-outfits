What To Wear To A Traditional Nigerian Wedding A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Attending a traditional Nigerian wedding can be both exciting and overwhelming at the same time. There are so many things to consider, including what to wear. You want to look your best, but also respect the culture and traditions of the wedding. In this post, we will guide you on what to wear to a traditional Nigerian wedding, so you can feel confident and comfortable.

When it comes to attending a traditional Nigerian wedding, it's important to dress appropriately. The wedding is usually a very colorful and vibrant event, so you want to make sure your outfit matches the energy of the occasion. Additionally, there are certain cultural traditions that should be respected and reflected in your attire.

The traditional attire for a Nigerian wedding is the Ankara fabric, which is a vibrant and colorful fabric that is usually worn as a headwrap, dress, or skirt. Men usually wear a traditional agbada or dashiki. It's important to note that it's customary to wear bright and bold colors to a Nigerian wedding.

In summary, when attending a traditional Nigerian wedding, it's important to dress appropriately and respect the cultural traditions. The attire for a Nigerian wedding is usually the Ankara fabric, and bright and bold colors are customary.

What to Wear to a Traditional Nigerian Wedding: Personal Experience

As someone who has attended several traditional Nigerian weddings, I can attest to the importance of dressing appropriately. I made the mistake of wearing a simple black dress to my first Nigerian wedding and felt out of place. Since then, I have made sure to wear bright and bold colors, and usually opt for a dress made out of the Ankara fabric.

What to Wear to a Traditional Nigerian Wedding: Tips and Tricks

When selecting your outfit for a traditional Nigerian wedding, it's important to keep in mind the vibrant and colorful nature of the occasion. Here are some tips and tricks to help you look your best:

1. Wear Ankara Fabric

The Ankara fabric is the traditional fabric for Nigerian weddings. It's bright, bold, and colorful, and will help you fit in with the energy of the event. You can wear an Ankara dress, skirt, or headwrap, depending on your personal style.

2. Opt for Bright and Bold Colors

As mentioned earlier, Nigerian weddings are known for their bright and bold colors. Don't be afraid to wear a dress in a bright shade of pink or yellow. You can also incorporate these colors into your accessories, such as your shoes or jewelry.

What to Wear to a Traditional Nigerian Wedding: More Tips

Here are some more tips to help you nail your outfit for a traditional Nigerian wedding:

3. Dress Modestly

While Nigerian weddings are known for their vibrant and colorful attire, it's important to dress modestly. Avoid wearing anything too revealing or tight-fitting.

4. Consider the Venue

When selecting your outfit, consider the venue of the wedding. If the wedding is taking place in a church or mosque, you may want to opt for a more conservative outfit. If the wedding is taking place outdoors, consider wearing comfortable shoes and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.

What to Wear to a Traditional Nigerian Wedding: Final Thoughts

Attending a traditional Nigerian wedding can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these tips and tricks, you can ensure that you look your best and respect the cultural traditions of the wedding. Remember to wear bright and bold colors, incorporate the Ankara fabric into your outfit, and dress modestly. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to turn heads at the wedding!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a white dress to a Nigerian wedding?
A: It's best to avoid wearing white to a Nigerian wedding, as it is traditionally associated with mourning.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a Nigerian wedding?
A: Yes, you can wear a hat to a Nigerian wedding. Just make sure it matches your outfit and complements the overall look.

Q: Can I wear jeans to a Nigerian wedding?
A: No, it's not appropriate to wear jeans to a Nigerian wedding. It's best to dress up and wear something more formal.

Q: Can I wear flats to a Nigerian wedding?
A: It's best to wear heels or dressy sandals to a Nigerian wedding. Flats may be too casual for the occasion.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Traditional Nigerian Wedding

Attending a traditional Nigerian wedding can be an exciting and fun experience. By following the dress code and cultural traditions, you can ensure that you look your best and show respect to the couple getting married. Remember to wear bright and bold colors, incorporate the Ankara fabric into your outfit, dress modestly, and avoid wearing white or jeans. With these tips in mind, you're sure to make a great impression at the wedding!
