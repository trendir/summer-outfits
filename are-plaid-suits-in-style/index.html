Are Plaid Suits In Style A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline May 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Plaid suits have been a fashion staple for decades, but are they still in style? In this guide, we'll explore the current trends and provide tips for incorporating plaid suits into your wardrobe.

Many people are hesitant to wear plaid suits because they fear they will look outdated or out of place. However, plaid suits can be a stylish and versatile addition to any wardrobe when worn correctly.

The short answer is yes, plaid suits are still in style. In fact, they have recently experienced a resurgence in popularity. Many designers have included plaid suits in their collections, and they have been spotted on celebrities and influencers alike.

To rock a plaid suit in style, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, choose a suit that fits well and flatters your body type. Second, opt for subtle patterns and colors that are easy to mix and match. Third, accessorize with simple pieces to keep the focus on the suit.

The Benefits of Wearing Plaid Suits

Plaid suits offer a range of benefits, including:

  • Versatility: Plaid suits can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.
  • Timelessness: Plaid suits have been a fashion staple for decades and will always be in style.
  • Individuality: Wearing a plaid suit sets you apart from the crowd and shows that you have a unique sense of style.

Personal Experience

I recently attended a wedding where the dress code called for a suit and tie. Instead of opting for a traditional black or navy suit, I decided to wear a plaid suit. I received countless compliments on my outfit and felt confident and stylish throughout the event.

Tips for Wearing Plaid Suits

If you're new to wearing plaid suits, consider these tips:

  • Stick to subtle patterns and colors.
  • Pair with simple accessories.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as wool or tweed.

How to Dress Up a Plaid Suit

To dress up a plaid suit, pair it with a crisp white dress shirt, a solid tie, and dress shoes. Avoid bold accessories or patterns that may compete with the suit.

How to Dress Down a Plaid Suit

To dress down a plaid suit, pair it with a casual shirt, such as a denim or chambray shirt, and sneakers or loafers. You can also roll up the sleeves of the shirt for a more relaxed look.

Final Thoughts

Plaid suits are a timeless and versatile addition to any wardrobe. By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can rock a plaid suit in style and make a statement wherever you go.

Question and Answer

Q: Can plaid suits be worn in the workplace?

A: Yes, plaid suits can be worn in the workplace when paired with a dress shirt and dress shoes. Stick to subtle patterns and colors for a more professional look.

Q: Can plaid suits be worn in the summer?

A: Yes, plaid suits can be worn in the summer when made from lightweight fabrics such as linen or cotton.

Q: Are plaid suits only for men?

A: No, plaid suits can be worn by anyone regardless of gender.

Q: How do I choose the right plaid suit for my body type?

A: Choose a suit that fits well and flatters your body type. If you're unsure, consult with a tailor or sales associate for guidance.

Conclusion of Are Plaid Suits in Style

Plaid suits are in style and offer a range of benefits for both men and women. By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can rock a plaid suit in style and make a statement wherever you go.
