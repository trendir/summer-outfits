South Indian Boy Names Starting With K and Their Significance

Karthik: This is a popular name in South India, and it means 'son of Lord Shiva.' Parents who are devotees of Lord Shiva often choose this name for their baby boy.

Krishna: This name is derived from Lord Krishna, who is a popular deity in Hindu mythology. The name means 'dark' or 'black,' and it is a perfect name for a baby boy with dark skin.

Kiran: This name means 'ray of light' or 'sunbeam' in Sanskrit. Parents who want to give their baby boy a name that signifies brightness and positivity often choose this name.

Kishore: A Unique South Indian Boy Name Starting With K

Kishore is a unique south Indian boy name starting with K. The name means 'young' or 'youthful,' and it is perfect for parents who want to give their baby boy a name that signifies youthfulness and energy. I have a friend who named her son Kishore, and everyone in our friend circle loved the name. It is easy to pronounce, and it has a positive connotation.

Kamal: A Meaningful South Indian Boy Name Starting With K

Kamal is a meaningful south Indian boy name starting with K. The name means 'lotus' in Sanskrit, and it is a perfect name for parents who want to give their baby boy a name that signifies purity and beauty. I know a family who named their baby boy Kamal, and they love the name. It is easy to pronounce, and it has a positive meaning.
