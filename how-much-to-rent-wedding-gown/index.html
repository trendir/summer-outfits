How Much Does It Cost To Rent A Wedding Gown

Written by William Aug 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Planning a wedding is an exciting time, but it can also be stressful. One of the biggest decisions a bride has to make is what to wear on her big day. While some brides opt to purchase a wedding gown, others may prefer to rent one. One of the most common questions brides have is, "how much does it cost to rent a wedding gown?"

The cost of renting a wedding gown can vary depending on a number of factors, including the designer, the style, and the location of the rental shop. However, on average, renting a wedding gown typically costs between $200 and $2,000.

In general, renting a wedding gown is a more affordable option than purchasing one outright. Not only can it save you money, but it also allows you to wear a designer gown that may have been otherwise out of your budget. Additionally, renting a gown means you won't have to worry about what to do with it after the wedding.

My Personal Experience with Renting a Wedding Gown

When I was planning my wedding, I knew I wanted to wear a designer gown, but I couldn't justify spending thousands of dollars on a dress I would only wear once. I decided to look into renting a gown instead. I found a local bridal shop that offered a wide selection of designer gowns for rent, and I was able to find the perfect dress for a fraction of the cost of purchasing it.

Not only was renting a gown more affordable, but it also made the whole process much easier. The rental shop took care of alterations, cleaning, and even provided a garment bag for me to transport the dress in. It was a stress-free experience that allowed me to focus on other aspects of the wedding planning process.

Tips for Renting a Wedding Gown

If you're considering renting a wedding gown, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

1. Start Your Search Early

Just like purchasing a wedding gown, it's important to start your search for a rental gown early. This will give you plenty of time to find the perfect dress and make any necessary alterations.

2. Read the Contract Carefully

Before signing a contract with a rental shop, make sure you read it carefully. Pay attention to the rental period, any fees for late returns, and the condition the gown should be returned in.

Conclusion

Renting a wedding gown can be a great option for brides who want to wear a designer gown without breaking the bank. With a little research and preparation, you can find the perfect dress and have a stress-free wedding day.

Question and Answer

Q: Can you alter a rental wedding gown?

A: Yes, most rental shops offer alterations to ensure the perfect fit for your big day.

Q: Do I need to clean the rental gown before returning it?

A: No, most rental shops will take care of cleaning the gown for you.

Q: What happens if I damage the rental gown?

A: You will likely be charged a fee for any damages to the gown. Make sure you read the rental contract carefully to understand your responsibilities.

Q: Can I rent accessories to go with my rental gown?

A: Yes, many rental shops offer a variety of accessories to complete your wedding day look.
