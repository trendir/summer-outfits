High End Plus Size Clothing Brands Where To Shop For Stylish Clothes

Are you tired of shopping for plus size clothing only to find frumpy, unflattering options? It can be frustrating when you want to dress stylishly, but the fashion industry seems to ignore your needs. The good news is that there are high end plus size clothing brands that cater to women of all shapes and sizes. In this blog post, we'll explore some of the best options on the market.

When it comes to shopping for plus size clothing, there are a few pain points that many women experience. First, there's the issue of limited options. Many brands only offer a small selection of plus size clothing, leaving shoppers with few choices. Then there's the issue of fit. Plus size clothing often isn't designed with curves in mind, which can lead to ill-fitting garments. Finally, there's the issue of style. Many plus size clothing options are outdated or simply not fashionable.

The target of high end plus size clothing brands is to provide stylish, well-fitting options for women of all sizes. These brands often use high-quality materials and offer a wider range of sizes than traditional clothing brands. They also prioritize fashion, offering on-trend pieces that flatter a variety of body types.

In summary, high end plus size clothing brands aim to provide women with stylish, well-fitting options that are on-trend and made with high-quality materials. These brands address the pain points of limited options, poor fit, and outdated styles that many plus size women experience.

Universal Standard

One of the most popular high end plus size clothing brands is Universal Standard. This brand offers a wide range of sizes, from 00 to 40, and uses high-quality materials to create its garments. I personally love Universal Standard because their clothing is both fashionable and comfortable. I've purchased several pieces from them, including a wrap dress and a pair of jeans, and have been impressed with the fit and quality of both.

Eloquii

Eloquii is another popular high end plus size clothing brand. They offer a wide range of sizes, from 14 to 28, and have an extensive selection of on-trend pieces. I recently purchased a jumpsuit from Eloquii and was thrilled with the fit and quality. The brand also offers a variety of workwear options, which can be hard to find in plus sizes.

Fenty

Rihanna's Fenty brand has made waves in the fashion industry for its inclusive sizing and stylish designs. While the brand doesn't offer a full range of plus sizes yet, they do offer a few pieces in sizes up to 3X. I recently purchased a Fenty dress in a size 2X and was impressed with the fit and quality. I hope the brand continues to expand its plus size offerings in the future.

How to Shop for High End Plus Size Clothing Brands

If you're looking to shop for high end plus size clothing brands, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, pay attention to sizing. Many high end brands offer a wider range of sizes than traditional brands, so be sure to check their size charts before making a purchase. Second, consider the style of the brand. Some high end brands are more trendy, while others are more classic. Choose a brand that aligns with your personal style. Finally, don't be afraid to invest in high quality pieces. While high end clothing may be more expensive, it's often worth it in the long run for the quality and longevity of the garment.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high end plus size clothing brands more expensive than traditional clothing brands?

A: Yes, high end plus size clothing brands can be more expensive than traditional clothing brands. However, the quality and longevity of the garment often make it worth the investment.

Q: Do high end plus size clothing brands offer a wider range of sizes?

A: Yes, many high end plus size clothing brands offer a wider range of sizes than traditional clothing brands. Some brands offer sizes up to 6X.

Q: Can I find workwear options at high end plus size clothing brands?

A: Yes, many high end plus size clothing brands offer workwear options. Eloquii, for example, has a wide range of work-appropriate pieces.

Q: Do high end plus size clothing brands offer on-trend pieces?

A: Yes, many high end plus size clothing brands prioritize fashion and offer on-trend pieces. Universal Standard and Fenty are two brands that are known for their stylish designs.

Conclusion of High End Plus Size Clothing Brands

Shopping for plus size clothing can be frustrating, but high end plus size clothing brands provide a solution. These brands offer a wider range of sizes, high-quality materials, and on-trend designs. When shopping for high end plus size clothing, pay attention to sizing, style, and quality, and don't be afraid to invest in pieces that will last. With options like Universal Standard, Eloquii, and Fenty, there's no reason why plus size women can't dress stylishly and confidently.
