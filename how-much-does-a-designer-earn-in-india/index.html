How Much Does A Designer Earn In India

Written by Robby Jul 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming a designer in India? Do you want to know how much a designer earns in India? If yes, then you are in the right place. The salary of a designer in India is a topic of concern for many people.

Designers are creative professionals who use their artistic skills to create visual designs that meet the needs of their clients. They work in various fields, including graphic design, web design, fashion design, and product design. However, the salary of a designer in India varies depending on several factors.

The salary of a designer in India depends on various factors such as experience, skillset, industry, and location. According to Payscale, the average salary of a designer in India is around INR 3,00,000 to INR 8,00,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on the industry and location. For example, a designer working in Bangalore or Mumbai can earn more than a designer working in a smaller city.

In conclusion, the salary of a designer in India is determined by several factors such as experience, skillset, industry, and location. However, on average, a designer in India can earn around INR 3,00,000 to INR 8,00,000 per year.

My Personal Experience:

As a designer working in India, I can say that the salary varies greatly depending on the industry and location. In my experience, designers working in the IT industry tend to earn more than designers working in other fields. Also, the city in which you work plays a significant role in determining your salary. For example, designers working in Bangalore tend to earn more than designers working in other cities.

Factors affecting Designer's Salary in India:

Experience:

Experience is a crucial factor that determines the salary of a designer in India. As you gain more experience in the field, your salary tends to increase. A designer with 5-10 years of experience can earn around INR 8,00,000 per year, while a designer with less than 2 years of experience can earn around INR 2,50,000 per year.

Skillset:

The skillset of a designer also plays a significant role in determining their salary. Designers with a diverse skillset tend to earn more than designers with limited skills. For example, a designer who can work on both web and graphic design can earn more than a designer who only specializes in one field.

Location:

The city in which you work is another essential factor that determines your salary as a designer in India. Designers working in metropolitan cities such as Mumbai, Bangalore, and Delhi tend to earn more than designers working in smaller cities.

Industry:

The industry in which you work also plays a crucial role in determining your salary as a designer in India. Designers working in the IT industry tend to earn more than designers working in other industries such as fashion or product design.

Question and Answer:

Q. How much does a designer earn in India with 2 years of experience?

A. A designer with less than 2 years of experience can earn around INR 2,50,000 per year in India.

Q. What is the average salary of a designer in India?

A. According to Payscale, the average salary of a designer in India is around INR 3,00,000 to INR 8,00,000 per year.

Q. Do designers working in metropolitan cities earn more than designers working in smaller cities?

A. Yes, designers working in metropolitan cities such as Mumbai, Bangalore, and Delhi tend to earn more than designers working in smaller cities.

Q. Which industry pays more to designers in India?

A. The IT industry tends to pay more to designers in India compared to other industries such as fashion or product design.

Conclusion of How much does a designer earn in India:

The salary of a designer in India is determined by several factors such as experience, skillset, industry, and location. On average, a designer in India can earn around INR 3,00,000 to INR 8,00,000 per year. However, the salary can vary depending on the industry and location. Designers working in the IT industry and metropolitan cities tend to earn more than designers working in other industries and smaller cities.
