What Was The Biggest Boxing Fight Ever

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Boxing is a sport that has been around for centuries, and it has produced some of the most iconic and memorable moments in sports history. One question that many people ask is, what was the biggest boxing fight ever? The answer to this question is not as simple as it may seem, as there have been many great fights throughout the years that could all be considered the biggest.

When it comes to boxing, there are a few pain points that come to mind. One of these is the physical toll that the sport takes on the fighters. Another is the intense pressure that comes with competing at such a high level. And of course, there is the sheer excitement and drama that comes with watching two skilled fighters go head to head.

The biggest boxing fight ever is a subjective topic, and it largely depends on who you ask. However, many experts and fans alike would agree that the 1971 fight between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier, dubbed the "Fight of the Century," is one of the biggest fights in boxing history.

So what makes this fight so special? For one, it was the first time that two undefeated heavyweight champions had faced off in the ring. It was also a highly anticipated bout, with both fighters having a fierce rivalry that had been building for years. On top of that, the fight itself was an absolute classic, with both fighters trading blows and putting on an incredible show for the fans in attendance.

In summary, the biggest boxing fight ever is a topic that is up for debate. However, many experts would agree that the 1971 fight between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier is one of the most iconic and memorable fights in boxing history.

What Was the Biggest Boxing Fight Ever: The Fight of the Century

I remember watching the Fight of the Century with my dad when I was a kid. We were both huge boxing fans, and we had been eagerly anticipating this fight for months. I remember the excitement in the air as we sat down in front of the TV, waiting for the fighters to make their entrances.

As soon as the bell rang, it was clear that this was going to be an incredible fight. Ali and Frazier both came out swinging, and it was clear that they were both in top form. The fight went back and forth, with both fighters landing some incredible blows.

One of the most memorable moments of the fight came in the 15th round, when Frazier landed a devastating left hook that sent Ali to the canvas. Ali managed to get back up, but he was clearly hurt and struggling to stay on his feet. In the end, Frazier was declared the winner by unanimous decision.

Looking back on the Fight of the Century, it's clear why it is considered one of the biggest boxing fights ever. It had everything you could want in a boxing match: two undefeated champions facing off, a fierce rivalry, and an incredible display of skill and heart from both fighters.

What Was the Biggest Boxing Fight Ever: The Thrilla in Manila

Another fight that is often mentioned when discussing the biggest boxing fights ever is the 1975 bout between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier, known as the Thrilla in Manila. This fight was the third and final meeting between the two fighters, and it was an absolute war.

Like the Fight of the Century, the Thrilla in Manila was a highly anticipated bout that lived up to the hype. Ali and Frazier both gave it their all, and the fight was incredibly close throughout. In the end, Ali emerged victorious after Frazier's corner threw in the towel before the start of the 15th round.

What makes the Thrilla in Manila such a memorable fight is the sheer intensity of it. Both fighters were pushed to their limits, and the fight was brutal and grueling from start to finish. It was a true test of skill, heart, and endurance, and it is still talked about today as one of the greatest boxing matches ever.

What Makes a Boxing Fight Great?

When it comes to what makes a boxing fight great, there are a few key factors to consider. First and foremost, the fighters themselves need to be skilled and talented. A great fight is one where both fighters are evenly matched and capable of putting on an incredible show.

Another important factor is the level of anticipation and excitement leading up to the fight. Great fights are ones that fans have been eagerly anticipating for weeks or even months, and that generate a lot of buzz and excitement in the media and among fans.

Finally, a great fight is one that is memorable and leaves a lasting impression on those who watch it. Whether it's because of the incredible skill displayed by the fighters, the drama and excitement of the bout, or the sheer intensity of the competition, a great fight is one that people will be talking about for years to come.

How to Watch the Greatest Boxing Fights Ever

If you're a boxing fan looking to watch some of the greatest fights in the sport's history, there are a few ways to do so. One option is to check out your local library or video store and see if they have any classic boxing matches available for rent or purchase.

Another option is to look online for streaming services that offer classic fights. Many streaming platforms, such as YouTube and ESPN+, have a wide selection of classic boxing matches available for viewing.

Finally, you can also check out some of the many books and documentaries that have been written about boxing's greatest fights. These can give you an in-depth look at some of the most memorable moments in the sport's history, and help you gain a deeper appreciation for the skill, heart, and dedication of the fighters who have made boxing such an incredible sport.

Question and Answer

A: Joe Frazier won the Fight of the Century by unanimous decision.

Q: What made the Thrilla in Manila such a great fight?

Conclusion of What Was the Biggest Boxing Fight Ever

Boxing has produced some of the most memorable moments in sports history, and the biggest boxing fight ever is a topic that is up for debate. However, many experts and fans alike would agree that the 1971 fight between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier, known as the Fight of the Century, is one of the most iconic and memorable fights in boxing history. Whether you're a longtime fan of the sport or a newcomer looking to learn more, watching some of the greatest fights in boxing history is a great way to appreciate the incredible skill, heart, and dedication of the fighters who have made boxing such a beloved sport.
