Mens Trouser Sizes in France: Understanding the System

When it comes to mens trouser sizes in France, the sizing system is based on the measurement of the waist in centimeters. The sizes range from 36 to 60, with each size corresponding to a specific waist measurement. For example, a size 36 corresponds to a waist measurement of 71cm, while a size 60 corresponds to a waist measurement of 117cm.

When shopping for trousers in France, it's important to know your waist measurement in centimeters. This will help you determine your size and find the perfect fit. If you're unsure about your waist measurement, you can use a tape measure to measure around your waist at the narrowest point, which is usually just above the belly button.
