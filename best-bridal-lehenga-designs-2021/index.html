What are the Latest Trends in Bridal Lehenga Designs 2021?

As we enter 2021, there are several trends in bridal lehenga designs that are gaining popularity. One of the biggest trends this year is the use of pastel colors. Soft shades of pink, mint green, and lavender are becoming increasingly popular among brides who want a more subtle look. Another trend that we're seeing is the use of metallic embroidery. Gold and silver threadwork is being used to create intricate designs that add a touch of glamour to the bridal lehenga.

When it comes to styles, A-line lehengas and mermaid lehengas are still in fashion. However, we're also seeing more brides opt for the traditional circular lehenga. This style is great for brides who want a more traditional look, and it's also perfect for those who want to add volume to their outfit.

How to Choose the Best Bridal Lehenga for Your Body Type?

Choosing the right bridal lehenga for your body type is essential to ensure that you look your best on your big day. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect lehenga:

For Hourglass Body Type:

If you have an hourglass figure, you can opt for a fitted lehenga that accentuates your curves. A mermaid lehenga is a great option for this body type as it hugs your curves in all the right places.

How to Accessorize?

When it comes to accessories, you can opt for a statement necklace and earrings to draw attention to your neckline. A maang tikka or a mathapatti can also complement your outfit and add a touch of elegance.

For Pear-Shaped Body Type:

If you have a pear-shaped body, you can opt for an A-line lehenga that flares out from the waist. This will help balance out your proportions and create a more symmetrical look.

How to Accessorize?

When it comes to accessories, you can opt for a long necklace that draws attention away from your hips. You can also wear statement earrings or a maang tikka to add some drama to your outfit.

For Apple-Shaped Body Type:

If you have an apple-shaped body, you can opt for a lehenga with an empire waistline. This style will help create the illusion of a smaller waistline and draw attention to your upper body.

How to Accessorize?

When it comes to accessories, you can opt for a choker necklace that draws attention to your neckline. You can also wear statement earrings or a maang tikka to add some sparkle to your outfit.

Question and Answer:

Q: What colors are popular for bridal lehengas in 2021?

A: Soft shades of pink, mint green, and lavender are becoming increasingly popular among brides this year.

Q: What styles of bridal lehengas are in fashion this year?

A: A-line lehengas, mermaid lehengas, and circular lehengas are all in fashion this year.

Q: How can I choose the right bridal lehenga for my body type?

A: You can choose a fitted lehenga for an hourglass figure, an A-line lehenga for a pear-shaped body, and a lehenga with an empire waistline for an apple-shaped body.

Q: What accessories should I wear with my bridal lehenga?

A: You can wear a statement necklace, earrings, mathapatti, or maang tikka to complement your outfit.

Conclusion of Best Bridal Lehenga Designs 2021:

Choosing the best bridal lehenga design can be a daunting task, but with this guide, you'll have a better understanding of the latest trends and styles for 2021. Remember to choose a design that suits your body type and personal style, and accessorize accordingly. With the right bridal lehenga, you'll look stunning on your big day!
