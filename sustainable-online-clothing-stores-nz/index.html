Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a brand is truly sustainable?

A: Look for brands that are transparent about their production processes and materials. They should be able to provide information about where their materials come from, how they are produced, and what steps they are taking to reduce their environmental impact.

Q: Are sustainable clothing brands more expensive?

A: While some sustainable clothing brands can be more expensive than fast fashion brands, there are also many affordable options available. Shopping at sustainable online clothing stores NZ can also make sustainable fashion more accessible and affordable.

Q: How can I reduce my environmental impact when it comes to fashion?

A: There are a number of ways to reduce your environmental impact when it comes to fashion. One of the best ways is to shop for sustainable and eco-friendly clothing. You can also try to reduce your overall consumption by buying fewer items and choosing high-quality pieces that will last longer.

A: In addition to shopping for sustainable clothing, you can also support sustainable fashion by buying second-hand clothing, repairing and mending your clothes, and recycling or repurposing old clothing.
