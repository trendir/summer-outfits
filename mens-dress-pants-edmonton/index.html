Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between slim-fit and straight-leg dress pants?

A: Slim-fit dress pants are more form-fitting and tapered towards the ankle, while straight-leg dress pants have a more relaxed fit and are the same width from the thigh to the ankle.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a pair of mens dress pants in Edmonton?

A: The price of mens dress pants in Edmonton can vary depending on the brand and the material. However, you can expect to spend anywhere from $50 to $500.

Q: Can I wear dress pants casually?

A: Yes, you can wear dress pants casually by pairing them with a t-shirt or a casual button-up shirt. However, it's essential to make sure that the dress pants are not too formal for the occasion.

Q: How often should I wash my mens dress pants?
