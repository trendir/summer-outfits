Best Quality Formal Shirts In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of wasting money on formal shirts that don't last long or don't fit well? Do you want to find the best quality formal shirts in India that are durable, comfortable, and stylish? Look no further! In this guide, we will explore the top brands and features to look for when buying formal shirts in India.

When it comes to formal shirts, there are several pain points that people commonly face. Some of these include poor quality materials, uncomfortable fits, lack of variety in sizes and colors, and high prices. Finding a shirt that ticks all the boxes can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available in the market.

The target of this guide is to help you find the best quality formal shirts in India that suit your preferences and budget. Whether you're looking for a shirt for a job interview, a wedding, or a business meeting, we've got you covered.

In summary, this guide will cover the top brands and features to look for when buying formal shirts in India. We will also share some personal experiences and tips to help you make an informed decision.

Types of Formal Shirts

Before we dive into the best quality formal shirts in India, let's first understand the different types of formal shirts available.

1. Dress Shirts: These are the most formal type of shirts that come in plain or striped patterns. They are usually made of cotton or linen.

2. Oxford Shirts: These are a versatile option that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions. They have a thicker weave and a button-down collar.

3. Poplin Shirts: These are lightweight and breathable shirts that are perfect for summers. They have a plain weave and a smooth texture.

Best Quality Formal Shirts in India: Top Brands

1. Van Heusen: Van Heusen is a popular brand that offers a wide range of formal shirts for men. They are known for their high-quality fabrics and modern designs. Their shirts are available in a variety of fits and sizes.

2. Arrow: Arrow is another well-known brand that offers premium formal shirts for men. They are known for their classic designs and impeccable quality. Their shirts are available in a range of colors and patterns.

Features to Look for in Best Quality Formal Shirts in India

1. Fabric: Look for shirts made of high-quality fabrics such as cotton or linen. These fabrics are breathable, durable, and comfortable to wear.

2. Fit: Make sure the shirt fits well and is tailored to your body type. A well-fitted shirt not only looks good but also feels comfortable.

3. Collar: The collar of a formal shirt should be stiff and well-structured. It should also fit well around the neck without being too tight or too loose.

How to Care for Formal Shirts in India

1. Wash shirts in cold water to prevent shrinkage.

2. Avoid using bleach or strong detergents as they can damage the fabric.

3. Iron shirts on a low heat setting to prevent damage to the fabric.

Personal Experience

As someone who has bought several formal shirts over the years, I have learned the importance of investing in high-quality shirts that last long and look good. My go-to brands for formal shirts in India are Van Heusen and Arrow. I prefer shirts made of cotton as they are breathable and comfortable to wear even for long hours. I also make sure to get my shirts tailored to my body type for a perfect fit.

FAQs

Q. Can formal shirts be worn for casual occasions?
A. Yes, some types of formal shirts such as Oxford and Poplin shirts can be worn for casual occasions as well.

Q. How often should I wash my formal shirts?
A. It is recommended to wash formal shirts after every wear to maintain their quality and appearance.

Q. How do I know if a formal shirt fits me well?
A. A well-fitted shirt should be snug around the shoulders, chest, and waist, without being too tight or too loose.

Q. Are formal shirts expensive?
A. The price of formal shirts varies depending on the brand, quality, and design. However, investing in a high-quality formal shirt is worth it in the long run.

Conclusion of Best Quality Formal Shirts in India

When it comes to finding the best quality formal shirts in India, it's important to consider factors such as fabric, fit, and brand. By choosing a high-quality shirt that fits well and is made of durable fabric, you can ensure that you look and feel confident for any formal occasion. Don't forget to take care of your shirts by washing them properly and ironing them on a low heat setting. With these tips in mind, you can find the perfect formal shirt that suits your style and budget.
