Best Dress Brands of 2020: Your Ultimate Guide to Looking Fabulous

When it comes to finding the perfect dress, it can be a daunting task. With so many brands out there, it can be overwhelming to sift through the options and find the perfect fit. Whether you're searching for a special occasion dress or something casual for everyday wear, it's important to know which brands are worth your investment. In this article, we'll take a deep dive into the best dress brands of 2020 and help you find your next wardrobe staple.

So, what should you look for in a dress brand? Quality fabrics, attention to detail, and a variety of styles are all important factors to consider. You want to invest in a brand that will stand the test of time and provide you with pieces that you'll love for years to come. With that in mind, let's take a closer look at the best dress brands of 2020.

Overall, the best dress brands of 2020 include Reformation, Zimmermann, Free People, Aritzia, and Self-Portrait. These brands offer a wide range of styles, from bohemian to sophisticated, and use high-quality fabrics to create pieces that are both comfortable and stylish.

Zimmermann

Zimmermann is an Australian brand that is known for its feminine and romantic designs. While their pieces can be on the pricier side, they are worth the investment for special occasions.

Free People

Free People is a bohemian brand that offers a range of dresses, from flowy maxi dresses to edgy mini dresses. Their pieces are perfect for festivals and outdoor events, and are made from comfortable fabrics like cotton and rayon. I love their embroidered dresses, which add a unique touch to any outfit.

Aritzia

Aritzia is a Canadian brand that offers a mix of minimalist and statement dresses. Their pieces are perfect for everyday wear, and are made from high-quality fabrics like silk and linen. I personally love their slip dresses, which can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Self-Portrait

Self-Portrait is a brand that is known for its intricate lace designs and feminine silhouettes. Their dresses are perfect for weddings and other formal events, and are made from high-quality fabrics like silk and chiffon. While their pieces can be on the pricier side, they are worth the investment for a special occasion.

How to Choose the Best Dress Brand for You

When it comes to choosing the best dress brand for you, it's important to consider your personal style and needs. Do you prefer bohemian or minimalist styles? Are you looking for a dress for a special occasion or everyday wear? By answering these questions, you can narrow down your options and find a brand that works for you.

Tips for Styling Your Dresses

Once you've found the perfect dress, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling your dresses:

  • Pair a maxi dress with sandals and a denim jacket for a casual look.
  • Dress up a midi dress with heels and statement earrings for a formal occasion.
  • Layer a slip dress over a t-shirt for an on-trend look.
  • Add a belt to a flowy dress to cinch in your waist and create definition.
