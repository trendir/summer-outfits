Discovering The Best Costumes Movies A Guide To Great Style On The Silver Screen

Written by Frank Sep 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of watching movies with boring costumes? Do you want to explore the world of fashion through the lens of film? If so, you're in the right place. In this post, we'll explore some of the best costumes movies of all time, and why they're worth watching. From intricate period pieces to modern-day masterpieces, these films offer a glimpse into the artistry of costume design.

Costumes are an integral part of any film, helping to create a sense of time and place, establish character, and add to the overall aesthetic of the movie. Good costumes can make a good movie great, and bad costumes can ruin an otherwise excellent film. Whether you're a fashion enthusiast or just appreciate great cinema, finding the best costumes movies is a must.

So what makes a great costume movie? While everyone has their preferences, the best costumes movies tend to be those that are visually stunning and well-crafted. They should also be able to transport the viewer to another time and place, whether that's the Elizabethan era or the streets of New York City. Ideally, the costumes should also play a role in the story, helping to shape the characters and their arcs throughout the film.

In this post, we'll explore some of the best costumes movies of all time, and why they're worth watching. From classic Hollywood to contemporary cinema, these films offer a masterclass in costume design and fashion history. So without further ado, let's dive in!

The Great Gatsby (2013)

When it comes to modern-day costume epics, The Great Gatsby is hard to beat. Directed by Baz Luhrmann, this adaptation of F. Scott Fitzgerald's classic novel is a visual feast for the eyes, with costumes that are both extravagant and historically accurate. From the flapper dresses to the tailored suits, every piece of clothing in the film is designed to transport the viewer to the roaring twenties.

Personally, I was blown away by the level of detail that went into the costumes for The Great Gatsby. The beading, embroidery, and lacework are exquisite, and every piece of jewelry is perfectly chosen to complement the outfits. The costumes also play an important role in the story, helping to establish the characters and their social status. Overall, The Great Gatsby is a must-watch for anyone interested in fashion or film.

Marie Antoinette (2006)

For a trip back in time to the opulent court of Versailles, look no further than Sofia Coppola's Marie Antoinette. This film is a feast for the eyes, with costumes that are both luxurious and historically accurate. From the elaborate gowns to the towering wigs, every detail of the costumes is designed to transport the viewer to the 18th century.

Personally, I love the way that the costumes in Marie Antoinette help to establish the character of the queen herself. As she becomes more isolated and out of touch with the people of France, her clothing becomes more extravagant and outlandish. The costumes also help to establish the social hierarchy of the court, with each character dressed in a way that reflects their status. Overall, Marie Antoinette is a must-watch for anyone interested in the history of fashion.

Mad Men (2007-2015)

While Mad Men is technically a TV show, it's worth mentioning here because of its incredible attention to detail when it comes to costume design. Set in the 1960s, the show is a masterclass in mid-century fashion, with every outfit perfectly tailored to the character wearing it. From the sharp suits worn by Don Draper to the colorful dresses worn by Joan Holloway, every piece of clothing in Mad Men is a work of art.

Personally, I love the way that Mad Men uses fashion to establish the character of each person on the show. From Peggy's transformation from a meek secretary to a powerful ad executive to Joan's struggle to be taken seriously in a male-dominated workplace, their clothing is a reflection of their journey throughout the series. Overall, Mad Men is a must-watch for anyone interested in fashion or the history of advertising.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy (2001-2003)

While The Lord of the Rings is primarily known for its epic battles and sweeping landscapes, the costumes in these films are equally impressive. Designed by Ngila Dickson and Richard Taylor, the clothing worn by the characters in The Lord of the Rings is both beautiful and functional, with each outfit perfectly suited to the character wearing it. From the elven cloaks to the armor worn by the Rohirrim, every piece of clothing in the films helps to establish the world of Middle Earth.

Personally, I love the way that the costumes in The Lord of the Rings help to establish the different cultures and races in the world of the films. From the elegant robes worn by the elves to the rough-hewn leathers worn by the dwarves, every outfit reflects the unique characteristics and history of the people wearing it. Overall, The Lord of the Rings is a must-watch for anyone interested in fantasy or costume design.

When it comes to finding the best costumes movies, it's all about finding films that transport you to another time and place. Whether you're interested in history, fantasy, or modern-day fashion, there's a movie out there that will capture your imagination. From The Great Gatsby to Lord of the Rings, these films offer a masterclass in costume design and fashion history. So why not grab some popcorn, sit back, and enjoy the show?
