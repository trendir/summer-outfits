Difference Between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit And Classic Fit Everything You Need To Know

Written by Bardi Nov 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're in the market for a new shirt, you may have come across the terms Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit. While both options may seem similar, there are some key differences between the two that can affect how the shirt fits and feels. In this post, we'll break down the difference between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit so you can make an informed decision on which one is right for you.

Pain Points of Difference Between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit

When it comes to buying clothes, fit is everything. A poorly fitting shirt can make you look and feel uncomfortable, while a well-fitting shirt can boost your confidence and make you feel great. In the case of Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit, it's important to understand the differences so you can choose the fit that works best for your body type and personal style.

What is the Difference Between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit?

The main difference between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit is in the cut of the shirt. Classic Fit shirts have a relaxed fit that is designed to provide maximum comfort, while Custom Fit shirts have a more tailored cut that is designed to hug the body and provide a slimmer look. Custom Fit shirts also have a slightly shorter length and smaller armholes than Classic Fit shirts.

Another key difference between the two is the fabric. Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit shirts are made with a blend of cotton and elastane, which provides a slight stretch to the fabric and allows for a more form-fitting cut. Classic Fit shirts, on the other hand, are made with 100% cotton and have a looser, more relaxed feel.

Pros and Cons of Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit

When it comes to deciding between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit, it's important to weigh the pros and cons of each option. Here's a quick breakdown:

Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit

Pros:

  • Form-fitting cut that provides a slimmer look
  • Shorter length and smaller armholes for a more tailored feel
  • Provides a slight stretch for added comfort

Cons:

  • May not be as comfortable as Classic Fit for some individuals
  • May not be ideal for larger body types

Tommy Hilfiger Classic Fit

Pros:

  • Relaxed fit that provides maximum comfort
  • Ideal for larger body types or those who prefer a looser fit
  • 100% cotton fabric is breathable and comfortable

Cons:

  • May not provide as slim of a look as Custom Fit
  • May not be ideal for those who prefer a more tailored look

Which One Should You Choose?

Ultimately, the choice between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit comes down to personal preference. If you prefer a more form-fitting look, then Custom Fit may be the way to go. If you prioritize comfort and a looser fit, then Classic Fit may be the better option. It's also worth considering your body type and personal style when making your decision.

Question and Answer

Q: Are Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit shirts only for slim individuals?

A: While Custom Fit shirts are designed to provide a more tailored look, they can still work for individuals of all body types. It's all about finding the fit that works best for you.

Q: Are Tommy Hilfiger Classic Fit shirts baggy?

A: Classic Fit shirts are designed to provide a relaxed, comfortable fit. While they may not be as form-fitting as Custom Fit shirts, they should not be overly baggy.

Q: Can you dress up Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit shirts?

A: Both Custom Fit and Classic Fit shirts can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Custom Fit shirts may be better suited for formal occasions, while Classic Fit shirts may be better for casual settings.

Q: How do I know which size to order for Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit shirts?

A: It's important to consult the size chart provided by Tommy Hilfiger to ensure you select the right size for your body type. If you're unsure, you can always order multiple sizes and return the ones that don't fit.

Conclusion of Difference Between Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit and Classic Fit

When it comes to Tommy Hilfiger Custom Fit vs. Classic Fit, there is no right or wrong choice. It all comes down to personal preference and what works best for your body type and style. By understanding the differences between the two, you can make an informed decision and find the perfect shirt for you.
