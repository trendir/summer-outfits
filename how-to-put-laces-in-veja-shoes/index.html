How to Put Laces in Veja Shoes: A Step-by-Step Guide

Veja shoes are a popular choice for those who value style and sustainability. However, putting laces in Veja shoes can be a bit tricky, especially for those who are new to the brand. If you're struggling to figure out how to put laces in Veja shoes, you're not alone. Many people find it difficult to get the laces just right.

If you're wondering how to put laces in Veja shoes, don't worry. It's actually quite simple once you know the steps. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of putting laces in Veja shoes so you can get the perfect fit every time.

To summarize, in this post, we'll cover:

  • The basics of how to put laces in Veja shoes
  • Tips and tricks for getting the perfect fit
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • Answers to frequently asked questions about putting laces in Veja shoes

Step 1: Prepare Your Shoes and Laces

Before you start putting laces in your Veja shoes, you'll need to make sure they're ready. Start by unlacing your shoes completely and removing any old laces. Then, take a look at your new laces. Veja shoes typically come with two sets of laces, so choose the set that you want to use.

Once you have your laces picked out, make sure they're the right length for your shoes. You can do this by comparing them to your old laces or by measuring them against the length of your shoe. If they're too long, you can cut them to size with a pair of scissors.

Step 2: Thread Your Laces

Now it's time to thread your laces through the eyelets of your shoes. Start by inserting one end of the lace into the bottom eyelet of your shoe. Then, thread it across to the opposite eyelet and insert it through. Pull the lace tight so there's no slack.

Next, take the other end of the lace and insert it into the opposite bottom eyelet. Thread it across to the other eyelet and insert it through. Again, pull the lace tight so there's no slack.

Tips:

- Start with the bottom eyelets and work your way up.

- Make sure the laces are even on both sides.

Step 3: Cross Your Laces

Now that both ends of your laces are threaded through the bottom eyelets, it's time to start crossing them. Take the right lace and cross it over the left lace. Insert it into the next eyelet on the left side of the shoe.

Then, take the left lace and cross it over the right lace. Insert it into the next eyelet on the right side of the shoe.

Keep crossing your laces until you reach the top eyelets. Make sure you're pulling the laces tight as you go.

Common mistakes to avoid:

- Don't skip any eyelets. Make sure you're crossing your laces through every eyelet.

- Don't twist your laces. Make sure they lie flat against your shoe.

Step 4: Tie Your Laces

Once you've threaded your laces through all of the eyelets, it's time to tie them. Start by tying a basic knot at the top of your laces. You can use a standard knot or a double knot, depending on your preference.

If you have extra lace, you can tuck it into your shoe or tie a bow.

FAQs

Q: How tight should I tie my Veja shoes?

A: Your Veja shoes should be snug but not too tight. Make sure you can still move your toes and your foot doesn't feel constricted.

Q: Can I use any type of lace for my Veja shoes?

A: You can use any type of lace as long as it fits through the eyelets of your shoes. However, we recommend using the laces that come with your Veja shoes to ensure the best fit.

Q: How often should I replace my Veja laces?

A: It depends on how often you wear your shoes and how much wear and tear your laces experience. If your laces start to look worn or frayed, it's time to replace them.

Q: Can I wash my Veja laces?

A: Yes, you can wash your Veja laces. We recommend washing them by hand with a mild detergent and letting them air dry.

Conclusion

Putting laces in Veja shoes is a simple process that anyone can do. By following the steps we've outlined in this post, you'll be able to get the perfect fit every time.
