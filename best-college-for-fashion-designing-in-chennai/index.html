Best College For Fashion Designing In Chennai

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career in fashion designing? Do you want to learn about the best colleges for fashion designing in Chennai? Look no further, as this post has got you covered!

Choosing the right college for fashion designing can be a daunting task, as there are many factors to consider such as the reputation of the college, the quality of education, the faculty, the facilities, and the placement opportunities. Moreover, with so many colleges offering fashion designing courses in Chennai, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one.

So, what is the best college for fashion designing in Chennai? The answer is National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) Chennai. NIFT is a premier institute of fashion designing in India, with campuses in various cities including Chennai. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in fashion designing, fashion communication, textile design, and fashion technology.

In summary, NIFT Chennai is the best college for fashion designing in Chennai due to its reputation, quality of education, experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and excellent placement opportunities.

Target of Best College for Fashion Designing in Chennai

I personally had a great experience studying at NIFT Chennai. The faculty members were knowledgeable and supportive, and the curriculum was well-structured and industry-relevant. The campus had all the necessary facilities such as a library, design studios, computer labs, and a well-equipped auditorium. Additionally, the college had tie-ups with leading fashion companies, which provided us with the opportunity to work on live projects and intern with them.

Placement Opportunities at NIFT Chennai

One of the key advantages of studying at NIFT Chennai is the excellent placement opportunities it provides. The college has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in securing internships and jobs in leading fashion companies such as H&M, Zara, Levi's, and Fabindia. Moreover, the college has a good track record of placing students in reputed fashion companies with attractive salary packages.

Curriculum at NIFT Chennai

The curriculum at NIFT Chennai is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the fashion industry, its trends, and its demands. The courses are a mix of theory and practical sessions, which help students gain hands-on experience in designing, pattern-making, draping, and garment construction. Additionally, the college also provides opportunities for students to participate in national and international fashion events, which helps them gain exposure and build their portfolio.

Admission Process at NIFT Chennai

The admission process at NIFT Chennai is based on a written entrance exam followed by a situation test and personal interview. The entrance exam tests the candidate's knowledge in General Ability Test (GAT) and Creative Ability Test (CAT). The situation test evaluates the candidate's ability to create and present a 3D model based on a given theme. The personal interview assesses the candidate's creativity, communication skills, and overall personality.

Conclusion of Best College for Fashion Designing in Chennai

In conclusion, NIFT Chennai is the best college for fashion designing in Chennai due to its reputation, quality of education, experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and excellent placement opportunities. It provides students with a platform to showcase their creativity and talent and helps them build a successful career in the fashion industry. So, if you are interested in pursuing a career in fashion designing, NIFT Chennai is the college for you!
