Where To Sell Second Hand Clothes In Melbourne A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of having a closet full of clothes you no longer wear? Do you want to get rid of them and make some extra cash? If you live in Melbourne, you're in luck because there are plenty of options for selling your second-hand clothes. However, it can be overwhelming to know where to start and what options are available. In this guide, we'll explore the different places you can sell your clothes, the pros and cons of each option, and tips for getting the most money out of your items.

If you're looking to sell your second-hand clothes, there are plenty of options available in Melbourne. From online marketplaces to physical stores, there's something for everyone. The best option for you will depend on your preferences, the type of clothes you're selling, and how much time and effort you want to put into the process.

Overall, the most popular places to sell your second-hand clothes in Melbourne include online marketplaces such as eBay and Facebook Marketplace, consignment stores like Round She Goes, and local markets like the Rose St. Artists' Market. Each of these options has its own pros and cons, which we'll explore in more detail below.

Online Marketplaces

Online marketplaces like eBay and Facebook Marketplace are great options for selling your second-hand clothes in Melbourne. They allow you to reach a wide audience, and you can sell your items from the comfort of your own home. However, they do require some effort on your part, as you'll need to take photos of your items, write descriptions, and handle shipping.

Personally, I've had great success selling my clothes on Facebook Marketplace. I find it easy to use and the local focus means that I can often sell my items quickly. When using online marketplaces, it's important to take good photos of your items and write detailed descriptions. This will help your items stand out and make them more appealing to potential buyers.

Consignment Stores

Consignment stores like Round She Goes are another popular option for selling second-hand clothing in Melbourne. These stores will sell your items for you and take a percentage of the sale price as their fee. This is a great option if you don't want to deal with the hassle of selling your items yourself, but it does mean that you'll make less money overall.

Personally, I've had mixed experiences with consignment stores. While they can be a convenient option, I've found that they often take a large percentage of the sale price, leaving me with very little money. It's important to do your research and find a consignment store that has a fair fee structure and a good reputation.

Local Markets

Local markets like the Rose St. Artists' Market are a fun and unique option for selling your second-hand clothes in Melbourne. These markets allow you to sell your items in person, which can be a great way to connect with buyers and get a sense of what sells well. However, they do require more effort on your part, as you'll need to set up a stall and be present for the duration of the market.

I've had a great time selling my clothes at local markets. It's a fun way to spend a weekend, and I always come away with some extra cash. However, it can be tiring to set up and manage a stall for a whole day, so it's important to be prepared and bring plenty of snacks and water!

Tips for Selling Your Second-Hand Clothes

No matter where you choose to sell your second-hand clothes in Melbourne, there are a few tips that can help you get the most money for your items:

  • Take good photos: Clear, well-lit photos will help your items stand out and attract buyers.
  • Write detailed descriptions: Be honest about the condition of your items and include as much detail as possible.
  • Price your items competitively: Do some research to see what similar items are selling for, and price accordingly.
  • Be responsive to buyers: Answer questions quickly and be polite and professional in your communications.

Question and Answer

Q: How much money can I expect to make from selling my second-hand clothes?

A: The amount of money you can make will depend on the quality and condition of your items, as well as the selling method you choose. On average, you can expect to make anywhere from a few dollars to a few hundred dollars.

Q: What types of clothes sell best?

A: In general, clothes that are in good condition, on-trend, and from popular brands will sell best. However, there is a market for all types of clothes, so don't be afraid to try selling items that you might not think are in demand.

Q: How do I handle shipping if I sell my items online?

A: Most online marketplaces will have tools to help you calculate shipping costs and print shipping labels. It's important to package your items carefully to avoid damage in transit.

Q: Is it worth selling my second-hand clothes at a local market?

A: If you enjoy the experience of selling in person and want to connect with buyers directly, then selling at a local market can be a great option. However, it does require more effort than other selling methods and may not be worth it if you're short on time.

Conclusion of Where to Sell Second Hand Clothes in Melbourne

Selling your second-hand clothes in Melbourne can be a great way to declutter your closet and make some extra cash. Whether you choose to sell online, through a consignment store, or at a local market, there are plenty of options available. By following these tips and doing your research, you can maximize your profits and make the most of your unwanted clothes.
