Best British Suit Brands A Guide To Looking Sharp

Written by William Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of going to events and feeling underdressed or uncomfortable? Do you struggle to find a suit that fits you perfectly or makes you feel confident? Look no further than the best British suit brands. Not only do these brands offer high-quality materials and expert tailoring, but they also offer a timeless sense of style that will have you looking sharp for years to come.

When it comes to finding the best British suit brands, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your budget. While some of these brands may come with a higher price tag, they are often worth the investment due to their durability and sophisticated design. Additionally, consider the occasion you will be wearing the suit for. Are you attending a formal event, or are you looking for a more casual suit for everyday wear?

Now, let's dive into the best British suit brands and what sets them apart.

1. Savile Row Company

One of the most well-known British suit brands is the Savile Row Company. This brand has been around since 1938 and is known for its impeccable tailoring and attention to detail. The Savile Row Company offers a range of suits, from classic black-tie options to more modern and casual styles. Their prices are also quite reasonable, making them a great option for those on a budget.

2. Gieves & Hawkes

Gieves & Hawkes is a British suit brand with a long and prestigious history. Founded in 1771, this brand has a reputation for dressing British royalty, including Prince William and Prince Harry. Their suits are made from the finest materials and feature expert tailoring. While their prices may be on the higher side, their suits are truly an investment piece.

3. Hackett London

Hackett London is a British suit brand known for its modern take on classic styles. They offer a range of suits, from traditional wool options to more casual linen and cotton blends. Their suits are designed to be versatile and wearable for any occasion. While their prices may be slightly higher than other brands, their attention to detail and quality make it worth the investment.

4. Ted Baker

Ted Baker is a British fashion brand that offers more than just suits, but their suit options are definitely worth mentioning. Their suits are known for their unique patterns and designs, making them a great option for those looking to add a little personality to their wardrobe. While their prices may be on the higher side, their suits are made with high-quality materials and expert tailoring.

Q&A:

Q: What sets British suit brands apart from other brands?

A: British suit brands are known for their expert tailoring and attention to detail. These brands often use high-quality materials and have a long and prestigious history within the fashion industry.

Q: Are British suit brands more expensive than other suit brands?

A: While some British suit brands may come with a higher price tag, they are often worth the investment due to their durability and sophisticated design. Additionally, many of these brands offer suits at a variety of price points to fit different budgets.

Q: Can I wear a British suit for everyday wear, or are they only for formal occasions?

A: Many British suit brands offer a range of styles, from traditional black-tie options to more casual and modern designs. This makes them a great option for both formal events and everyday wear.

Q: How do I know which British suit brand is right for me?

A: Consider your budget and the occasion you will be wearing the suit for. Additionally, think about your personal style and what you want your suit to say about you. Each British suit brand offers a unique sense of style and design, so do some research and find the brand that speaks to you.

Conclusion of Best British Suit Brands:

Investing in a high-quality suit from one of the best British suit brands is a decision you won't regret. These brands offer expert tailoring, high-quality materials, and a timeless sense of style that will have you looking sharp for years to come. Whether you're attending a formal event or simply want to elevate your everyday style, a British suit is the perfect addition to any wardrobe.
