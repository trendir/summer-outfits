Top 10 International Fashion Stylists

Written by Daniel Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you struggle with putting together fashionable outfits? Are you looking for inspiration from some of the most talented fashion stylists in the world? Look no further than our list of the top 10 international fashion stylists!

When it comes to fashion, it can be difficult to know where to start. With so many trends, styles, and designers to choose from, it's easy to become overwhelmed. That's where fashion stylists come in – they can help you navigate the world of fashion and create a look that's uniquely you.

The target of our list of top 10 international fashion stylists is to inspire you with the work of some of the most talented and influential fashion stylists in the industry. Whether you're looking for new ideas, want to stay up-to-date on the latest trends, or simply admire the art of fashion styling, there's something for everyone on this list.

In this article, we'll summarize the main points about each of the top 10 international fashion stylists and related keywords, without explicitly using subheadings. Then, we'll dive deeper into each stylist's work, starting with:

Alexandra Mandelkorn

Alexandra Mandelkorn is a Los Angeles-based fashion stylist who has worked with some of the biggest names in Hollywood, including Brie Larson, Laura Harrier, and Awkwafina. Her style is eclectic and unique, combining vintage pieces with modern elements to create a look that's both timeless and contemporary.

Mandelkorn's work has been featured in publications like Vogue, InStyle, and Harper's Bazaar, and she has collaborated with brands like Gucci and Louis Vuitton. Her ability to mix and match different styles and colors is truly impressive, and she's definitely one to watch in the world of fashion styling.

Katie Grand

Katie Grand is a British fashion stylist and editor who has worked on some of the most iconic fashion campaigns and editorials of the past few decades. She's been the editor-in-chief of both LOVE and POP magazines, and has styled shoots for brands like Prada, Miu Miu, and Marc Jacobs.

Grand's unique style is characterized by bold colors, playful prints, and unexpected accessories. She's not afraid to take risks and push boundaries, and her work is always exciting and fresh.

Edward Enninful

Edward Enninful is a Ghanaian-British fashion stylist and editor who has been making waves in the fashion world since the 1990s. He's worked for publications like i-D and Vogue, and has styled some of the biggest celebrities in the world, including Naomi Campbell, Rihanna, and Beyoncé.

Enninful's style is all about creating a sense of drama and glamour, with bold colors, flowing fabrics, and statement accessories. He's known for his ability to elevate even the simplest of outfits with his unique touch.

Anna Dello Russo

Anna Dello Russo is an Italian fashion editor and stylist who is known for her extravagant, over-the-top style. She's worked for publications like Vogue Italia and collaborated with brands like H&M and Macy's.

Dello Russo's style is all about making a statement, with bold colors, eye-catching accessories, and plenty of sparkle. She's not afraid to take risks and push boundaries, and her work is always exciting and unexpected.

Question and Answer

Q: What is a fashion stylist?

A: A fashion stylist is someone who works with clients to create outfits and looks that are both fashionable and functional.

Q: What skills does a fashion stylist need?

A: A fashion stylist needs to have a strong sense of style, an eye for detail, and excellent communication skills. They also need to have a good understanding of fashion trends and be able to work under pressure.

Q: How do I become a fashion stylist?

A: To become a fashion stylist, you'll need to gain experience in the fashion industry by interning or assisting established stylists. You'll also need to build a strong portfolio of your work and network with other professionals in the industry.

Q: What is the average salary for a fashion stylist?

A: The salary for a fashion stylist can vary depending on their level of experience, location, and the types of clients they work with. According to Payscale, the average salary for a fashion stylist in the United States is around $50,000 per year.

Conclusion of Top 10 International Fashion Stylists

Whether you're looking for inspiration, guidance, or simply want to admire the art of fashion styling, there's something for everyone on our list of the top 10 international fashion stylists. From the bold and daring to the timeless and classic, these stylists have made their mark on the fashion world and continue to inspire us with their work.
