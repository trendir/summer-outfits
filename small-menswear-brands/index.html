Discovering Small Menswear Brands A Guide To Quality Clothing

Written by Eveline Oct 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old fashion brands? Do you want to support smaller businesses while looking stylish? Look no further than small menswear brands. These companies offer unique and high-quality clothing options that stand out from the mainstream fashion industry. In this guide, we'll explore the benefits of supporting small menswear brands and highlight some of our favorite companies.

It can be frustrating to constantly see the same clothing options from big-name brands. Not only can it feel boring, but it also makes it difficult to stand out in a crowd. Small menswear brands offer a solution to this problem. They provide customers with unique and high-quality clothing options that are not mass-produced. However, finding these brands can be challenging without proper guidance.

So, what is the target of small menswear brands? Their goal is to provide customers with an alternative to mainstream fashion. They aim to create clothing that is both stylish and functional while using high-quality materials. These brands often have a specific style or aesthetic that sets them apart from larger brands.

In this article, we will highlight some of our favorite small menswear brands and explain what makes them stand out. We will also discuss the benefits of supporting smaller businesses and why you should consider incorporating them into your wardrobe.

1. Taylor Stitch

Taylor Stitch is a San Francisco-based brand that offers a range of menswear options. Their clothing is made from high-quality materials such as Japanese denim and Italian wool. What sets Taylor Stitch apart is their commitment to sustainability. They use eco-friendly materials and have a program where customers can send in their old clothing to be recycled.

Personally, I have purchased several items from Taylor Stitch and have been impressed with the quality of their clothing. The fit and feel are unmatched, and I appreciate their dedication to sustainability.

2. Beckett Simonon

Beckett Simonon is a Colombian-based brand that specializes in leather goods. They offer a range of shoes, belts, and bags that are all made from high-quality leather. What sets them apart is their unique business model. They only produce items that have been pre-ordered, which reduces waste and allows them to offer lower prices to customers.

I have purchased several pairs of shoes from Beckett Simonon and have been impressed with the quality and affordability. Their business model is unique, and I appreciate their commitment to reducing waste.

The Benefits of Supporting Small Menswear Brands

There are many benefits to supporting small menswear brands. First and foremost, these companies offer unique and high-quality clothing options that are not mass-produced. This means that you are less likely to see someone else wearing the same outfit as you.

Additionally, many small menswear brands are committed to sustainability and ethical production practices. They use eco-friendly materials and pay fair wages to their employees. By supporting these businesses, you are contributing to a more sustainable and equitable fashion industry.

How to Find Small Menswear Brands

One of the best ways to find small menswear brands is to do your research. Look for articles and blog posts that highlight these companies. Social media can also be a great resource for discovering new brands. Follow influencers and bloggers who promote sustainable and ethical fashion.

Another option is to attend local markets and festivals. Many small menswear brands attend these events to showcase their products and connect with customers. This is a great way to see the clothing in person and support local businesses.

3. Outerknown

Outerknown is a brand founded by professional surfer Kelly Slater. They offer a range of clothing options that are inspired by surf culture. What sets Outerknown apart is their commitment to sustainability. They use eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton and recycled polyester.

I have purchased several items from Outerknown and have been impressed with the quality and style of their clothing. I appreciate their commitment to sustainability and their unique take on surf-inspired fashion.

Question and Answer

Q: Are small menswear brands more expensive than mainstream brands?

A: It depends on the brand, but many small menswear brands offer affordable options that are comparable in price to larger brands. Additionally, supporting small businesses often means supporting ethical and sustainable production practices, which can be worth the extra cost.

Q: How can I be sure that small menswear brands are using sustainable and ethical production practices?

A: Look for information on the brand's website about their production practices. Many small menswear brands are transparent about their methods and materials. Additionally, look for certifications such as Fair Trade or GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) that ensure ethical and sustainable production practices.

Q: Are small menswear brands only for niche styles?

A: No, small menswear brands offer a range of styles that can appeal to a wide variety of customers. While some brands may have a specific aesthetic, many offer classic and timeless clothing options.

Q: Can I find small menswear brands in physical stores?

A: Yes, many small menswear brands have brick-and-mortar stores or sell their clothing in boutiques. Additionally, attending local markets and festivals can be a great way to discover new brands and see the clothing in person.

Conclusion of Small Menswear Brands

Small menswear brands offer unique and high-quality clothing options that stand out from the mainstream fashion industry. These companies often have a commitment to sustainability and ethical production practices, making them a great choice for customers who want to support smaller businesses. By doing your research and attending local markets and festivals, you can discover new brands and incorporate them into your wardrobe.
