Set Designer Salary In India Everything You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much a set designer earns in India? Are you considering a career in set design but unsure about the salary prospects? You're not alone – many aspiring set designers in India have the same questions. The good news is that in this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about set designer salary in India.

One of the biggest pain points for set designers in India is the lack of reliable information about their earning potential. Many set designers are unsure about how much they should be earning, what factors affect their salary, and how they can negotiate a better pay package.

The average salary for a set designer in India is around INR 4 lakhs per annum. However, this figure can vary widely depending on several factors such as experience, location, and type of project. In this post, we'll explore these factors in detail to help you understand how much you can expect to earn as a set designer in India.

Now that we've covered the basics, let's dive deeper into the factors that affect set designer salary in India.

Experience

Experience is one of the most significant factors that influence set designer salary in India. As a fresher, you can expect to earn between INR 2-3 lakhs per annum. However, with 2-3 years of experience, you can earn up to INR 5-6 lakhs per annum. Senior set designers with over 5 years of experience can earn upwards of INR 10 lakhs per annum.

Personally, I started my career in set design as an assistant to a senior set designer. After gaining experience and honing my skills, I was able to work on my own projects and negotiate a higher salary.

Location

The location of your work can also impact your set designer salary in India. For example, set designers working in metropolitan cities like Mumbai and Delhi can earn higher salaries compared to those working in smaller cities or towns.

When I first started my career, I worked in a small town where the pay scale was significantly lower than in Mumbai. However, I was able to negotiate a better salary package when I moved to Mumbai.

Type of Project

The type of project you're working on can also affect your salary as a set designer in India. For example, working on a Bollywood movie or a big-budget advertisement campaign can earn you higher salaries compared to working on a small-scale project.

When I worked on a national ad campaign for a popular brand, I was able to negotiate a higher salary because of the size and scope of the project.

How to Negotiate Your Set Designer Salary in India

Now that you understand the factors that affect set designer salary in India, it's essential to know how to negotiate your pay package. Here are some tips:

Research

Before negotiating your salary, research the average pay scale for set designers in your location and level of experience. This information will give you a benchmark to negotiate from.

Highlight Your Experience and Skills

When negotiating your salary, highlight your experience and skills to show your value as a set designer. If you have worked on big-budget projects or have unique skills, make sure to mention them.

Be Confident

Confidence is key when negotiating your salary. Be clear about your expectations and don't be afraid to ask for what you deserve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the average salary for a set designer in India?

A: The average salary for a set designer in India is around INR 4 lakhs per annum.

Q: Do set designers in metropolitan cities earn more than those in small towns?

A: Yes, set designers in metropolitan cities like Mumbai and Delhi can earn higher salaries compared to those working in smaller cities or towns.

Q: How much can a senior set designer with 5 years of experience earn in India?

A: Senior set designers with over 5 years of experience can earn upwards of INR 10 lakhs per annum.

Q: How can I negotiate my set designer salary in India?

A: Research the average pay scale, highlight your experience and skills, and be confident when negotiating your salary.

Conclusion

Set designer salary in India can vary widely depending on several factors such as experience, location, and type of project. By understanding these factors and negotiating your pay package, you can ensure that you're earning what you deserve as a set designer in India.
