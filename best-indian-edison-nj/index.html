Discovering The Best Indian Restaurants In Edison Nj

Written by Frank Sep 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you craving some spicy and flavorful Indian food? Do you live in or near Edison, NJ? If you answered yes to both questions, then you're in luck because Edison has some of the best Indian restaurants in the state. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to try first. In this post, we'll help you discover the best Indian restaurants in Edison, NJ, and make your decision easier.

If you're a fan of Indian cuisine, you know the struggle of finding a restaurant that meets your expectations. You want authentic flavors, fresh ingredients, and a cozy ambiance. However, not all Indian restaurants deliver on these points. Some may have bland food or use frozen ingredients, while others may have a noisy and uncomfortable setting. These are the pain points that Indian food lovers face when searching for a good restaurant.

The target of the best Indian restaurants in Edison, NJ, is to provide customers with an unforgettable dining experience. They aim to satisfy your taste buds with rich and aromatic dishes that are made from scratch. They also strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where you can enjoy your meal with friends and family.

In summary, the best Indian restaurants in Edison, NJ, offer authentic and flavorful dishes, fresh ingredients, and a cozy ambiance. They aim to provide customers with an unforgettable dining experience.

Best Indian Restaurants in Edison, NJ: Spice Zone

Spice Zone is one of the best Indian restaurants in Edison, NJ, that you shouldn't miss. I've personally visited this restaurant several times, and each time I was blown away by the quality of the food and service. The restaurant has a cozy and elegant setting, with dim lighting and comfortable seating. The staff is friendly and attentive, and they'll guide you through the menu if you're unsure what to order.

Spice Zone's menu offers a wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, from appetizers to main courses. Some of my favorites include the samosas, chicken tikka masala, and garlic naan. The dishes are prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional spices that add a burst of flavor to each bite. They also have a good selection of drinks, including mango lassi and masala chai.

Best Indian Restaurants in Edison, NJ: Mithaas

Mithaas is another top-rated Indian restaurant in Edison, NJ, that's worth trying. What sets Mithaas apart from other Indian restaurants is their focus on sweets and desserts. They have a wide variety of desserts that are made in-house and are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth after a spicy meal.

Aside from desserts, Mithaas also offers a delicious range of chaat, snacks, and main dishes. Their chaat is a must-try, especially the pani puri and bhel puri. For main dishes, I recommend the dal makhani and paneer makhani. The restaurant has a bright and modern interior, with ample seating space for groups.

What Makes Indian Food Special?

Indian cuisine is known for its diverse flavors, spices, and cooking techniques. The use of aromatic herbs and spices, such as cumin, coriander, and turmeric, gives Indian dishes a unique and unforgettable taste. Moreover, Indian food is often vegetarian-friendly, with a variety of lentil and vegetable-based dishes that are healthy and delicious.

How to Make Indian Food at Home?

If you're a fan of Indian food, you might want to try making it at home. While some Indian dishes can be complex and time-consuming, others are easy to whip up in your kitchen. Start with simple recipes like dal, chana masala, or chicken curry, and build your way up to more elaborate dishes. Invest in quality spices and ingredients, and follow the recipe closely for the best results.

Best Indian Restaurants in Edison, NJ: Moghul Restaurant

Moghul Restaurant is a long-standing Indian restaurant in Edison, NJ, that's been serving customers for over 35 years. The restaurant has a classic and elegant interior, with a spacious dining area and private rooms for events. The menu offers a range of Indian and Pakistani dishes, with a focus on meat-based dishes.

I've visited Moghul Restaurant several times for family gatherings, and the food has always been exceptional. Their lamb chops and tandoori chicken are some of the best I've had, and their biryani is a crowd-pleaser. The staff is courteous and accommodating, and they'll make sure you have a memorable dining experience.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the spiciest Indian dish?

A: The spiciest Indian dish is often considered to be the vindaloo, which is a meat-based dish that originated in Goa, India. It's made with a blend of spices, vinegar, and red chilies, and is known for its fiery taste. However, you can always ask the restaurant to adjust the spice level to your liking.

Q: What is the difference between curry and masala?

A: Curry and masala are two different types of spice blends used in Indian cooking. Curry powder usually contains a mix of turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder, while masala is a blend of several spices that can vary depending on the recipe. Masala is often used to flavor specific dishes, such as tikka masala or garam masala.

Q: What is the most popular Indian dessert?

A: The most popular Indian dessert is probably the gulab jamun, which are small fried dough balls soaked in a sweet syrup. Other popular desserts include ras malai, kulfi, and jalebi.

Q: Is Indian food healthy?

A: Indian food can be healthy, especially if you opt for vegetarian dishes that are packed with nutrients and fiber. However, some Indian dishes can be high in fat and calories, especially if they're made with cream or butter. It's important to balance your meal with a variety of dishes and watch your portion sizes.

Conclusion of Best Indian Edison, NJ

If you're looking for the best Indian restaurants in Edison, NJ, you won't be disappointed with Spice Zone, Mithaas, and Moghul Restaurant. These restaurants offer authentic and flavorful dishes, fresh ingredients, and a cozy ambiance that will make your dining experience unforgettable. Whether you're a fan of meat-based dishes or vegetarian options, there's something for everyone in Indian cuisine. So, grab your friends and family, and head to one of these restaurants for a delicious and satisfying meal.
