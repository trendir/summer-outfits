How Many Types Of Shirt Collars Are There A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old shirt styles? Do you want to mix up your wardrobe with different types of shirt collars? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the different types of shirt collars and give you the ultimate guide to finding your perfect collar match.

Have you ever found yourself struggling to find the right shirt for a specific occasion? Maybe you have a job interview and want to look professional, or perhaps you are going on a date and want to impress. The collar of your shirt can make all the difference, and understanding the different types of shirt collars can help you make the right choice.

So, how many types of shirt collars are there? Well, there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as there are many different types of collars available. However, we can break them down into a few main categories.

The Classic Collars:

The classic collar is the most common type of collar and is often seen on dress shirts. It has a spread that is neither too wide nor too narrow, making it the perfect choice for any occasion. Some of the most popular classic collars include the point collar, the spread collar, and the button-down collar.

Personally, I love wearing a point collar shirt to work. It looks sharp and professional and pairs well with a tie. The spread collar, on the other hand, is more versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. The button-down collar is a great choice for a more casual look and is perfect for wearing with jeans or chinos.

The Stand Collars:

The stand collar is a type of collar that stands up and does not fold over. It is often seen on Mandarin collar shirts and Nehru jackets. The stand collar gives off a more modern and trendy vibe and is a great choice for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

Personally, I love wearing a Nehru jacket with a stand collar. It gives me a unique and stylish look that is perfect for a night out with friends.

The Band Collars:

The band collar is a type of collar that is a simple band around the neck. It is often seen on casual shirts and gives off a laid-back vibe. The band collar is a great choice for those who want to be comfortable and stylish.

I love wearing a band collar shirt on a summer day. It keeps me cool and comfortable while still looking fashionable.

The Wingtip Collars:

The wingtip collar is a type of collar that is often seen on tuxedo shirts. It has a pointed collar that folds over and resembles wings, hence the name. The wingtip collar is a great choice for formal events and adds a touch of elegance to any outfit.

Now that we have explored the different types of shirt collars, you can make an informed decision when choosing your next shirt. Remember, the collar of your shirt can make all the difference, so choose wisely!

Question and Answer:

Q: What is the most common type of collar?

A: The most common type of collar is the classic collar. It is often seen on dress shirts and has a spread that is neither too wide nor too narrow.

Q: What is the difference between the point collar and the spread collar?

A: The point collar has a narrow spread and is best suited for those with a slimmer face. The spread collar has a wider spread and is more versatile, making it a great choice for any occasion.

Q: Can I wear a stand collar shirt to a job interview?

A: It depends on the type of job you are interviewing for. A stand collar shirt gives off a more modern and trendy vibe, which may not be suitable for more conservative industries. It is best to stick with a classic collar for a job interview.

Q: What is the wingtip collar?

A: The wingtip collar is a type of collar that is often seen on tuxedo shirts. It has a pointed collar that folds over and resembles wings, adding a touch of elegance to any outfit.

Conclusion of how many types of shirt collars:

Understanding the different types of shirt collars can help you make the right choice when choosing your next shirt. Whether you prefer a classic collar, a stand collar, a band collar, or a wingtip collar, there is a collar out there for everyone. So, go ahead and mix up your wardrobe with different types of shirt collars and see which one suits you best!
