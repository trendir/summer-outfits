What Should A Man Wear To An Indian Wedding

Written by Christine May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Attending an Indian wedding can be an exciting experience, but it can also be overwhelming, especially when it comes to deciding what to wear. As a man, you want to look your best while also respecting the cultural traditions of the wedding ceremony. In this blog post, we'll guide you through what should a man wear to an Indian wedding, so you can feel confident and comfortable on the big day.

Indian weddings are known for their vibrant colors, intricate designs, and ornate details. The bride and groom, along with their families, will often wear traditional Indian clothing, which can make it difficult for guests to decide what to wear. You want to make sure you're dressed appropriately for the occasion without outshining the couple or disrespecting the culture.

When it comes to what should a man wear to an Indian wedding, it's best to opt for traditional Indian attire, such as a sherwani or a kurta pajama. A sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is usually worn over a kurta and churidar or dhoti pants. It is typically made of silk or brocade fabric and adorned with intricate embroidery or embellishments. A kurta pajama, on the other hand, is a more casual outfit that consists of a long tunic top and loose-fitting pants. It can also be made of silk or cotton fabric and come in various colors and designs.

In addition to the clothing, it's important to accessorize appropriately for an Indian wedding. Men can wear a turban, which is a traditional headpiece, or a dupatta, which is a long scarf that can be draped over the shoulders. You can also wear traditional Indian footwear, such as mojari or jutti shoes, which are made of leather and often embroidered.

To summarize what should a man wear to an Indian wedding, it's best to opt for traditional Indian attire, such as a sherwani or kurta pajama, and accessorize appropriately with a turban, dupatta, or traditional Indian footwear. It's also important to avoid wearing white or black, which are considered inauspicious colors in Indian culture.

The Significance of Indian Attire

As an Indian-American, attending weddings has been a significant part of my life. I have always appreciated the beauty and intricacy of traditional Indian clothing, and I believe it plays an essential role in honoring the culture and traditions of the wedding ceremony.

When I attended my cousin's wedding, I knew I wanted to wear something that reflected my Indian heritage while also looking stylish and modern. I opted for a navy blue sherwani with gold embroidery, paired with beige churidar pants and mojari shoes. The outfit made me feel confident and proud of my cultural identity, and I received many compliments from family and friends.

Tips for Choosing the Right Indian Attire

When choosing what to wear to an Indian wedding, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider the dress code specified on the wedding invitation. If it's a formal wedding, you may want to opt for a more formal outfit like a sherwani, while a casual wedding may call for a more relaxed outfit like a kurta pajama.

Secondly, consider the time of day and location of the wedding. If it's an outdoor wedding in the summer, you may want to opt for lighter fabrics like cotton or linen, while a winter wedding may call for heavier fabrics like silk or velvet.

Understanding the Colors of Indian Attire

Colors play a significant role in Indian culture and are often used to symbolize different emotions and beliefs. When it comes to what should a man wear to an Indian wedding, it's important to avoid wearing white or black, as they are considered inauspicious colors. Instead, opt for bright, vibrant colors like red, gold, or green, which symbolize prosperity, happiness, and good luck.

How to Accessorize for an Indian Wedding

Accessories are an essential part of any Indian outfit and can add an extra touch of elegance and sophistication. For men attending an Indian wedding, there are several accessories to consider, such as a turban, dupatta, or traditional Indian footwear.

A turban is a traditional headpiece that can be worn in a variety of ways, depending on the style and fabric. It can be made of silk or cotton and come in various colors and designs. A dupatta is a long scarf that can be draped over the shoulders and paired with a kurta pajama. It can also be made of silk or cotton and come in various colors and designs.

Mojari or jutti shoes are traditional Indian footwear that are made of leather and often embroidered. They come in various colors and designs and can add an extra touch of sophistication to any Indian outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a suit to an Indian wedding?

A: While it's not traditional Indian attire, you can wear a suit to an Indian wedding if you're more comfortable in Western clothing. However, it's important to avoid wearing white or black, which are considered inauspicious colors in Indian culture.

Q: Can I wear jeans to an Indian wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing jeans to an Indian wedding, as they are considered too casual for the occasion. Instead, opt for traditional Indian attire like a sherwani or kurta pajama.

Q: Do I need to wear a turban to an Indian wedding?

A: While it's not required, wearing a turban can be a sign of respect for Indian culture and traditions. If you're unsure about how to wear a turban, you can ask a family member or friend for assistance.

Q: What colors should I avoid wearing to an Indian wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing white or black, as they are considered inauspicious colors in Indian culture. You should also avoid wearing red, as it is the color typically worn by the bride.

Conclusion of What Should a Man Wear to an Indian Wedding

Attending an Indian wedding can be a memorable experience, and dressing appropriately shows respect for the culture and traditions of the wedding ceremony. When deciding what to wear, remember to opt for traditional Indian attire like a sherwani or kurta pajama, and accessorize appropriately with a turban, dupatta, or traditional Indian footwear. By following these guidelines, you can look your best and feel comfortable and confident on the big day.
