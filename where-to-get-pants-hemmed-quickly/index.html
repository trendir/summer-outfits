Where To Get Pants Hemmed Quickly A Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever found the perfect pair of pants, only to realize they're too long? It can be frustrating to have to wait days or even weeks for a tailor to hem them for you. If you're in a hurry and need your pants hemmed quickly, you're in the right place. In this post, we'll go over where to get pants hemmed quickly and efficiently, so you can get back to looking and feeling your best.

It can be a hassle to find a tailor who can hem your pants in a short amount of time. You might have to take time off work to make it to an appointment or wait in line for hours. It's also frustrating when you need your pants hemmed for a specific event or occasion, and the tailor can't accommodate your timeline. That's where finding a quick and reliable solution comes in handy.

Where to Get Pants Hemmed Quickly: Online Tailors

One of the best options for getting your pants hemmed quickly is to use an online tailor. Many online tailors specialize in quick and efficient alterations, and they can often have your pants hemmed and shipped back to you within a few days. Some popular online tailors include eShakti, Stitch It, and ZipFit Denim.

Personally, I've used Stitch It to hem my pants, and I was impressed with their service. They have a user-friendly website where you can select the type of alteration you need, including pant hemming. You can choose how much you want to shorten your pants, and they provide clear instructions on how to measure your inseam. Once you submit your order, they'll send you a prepaid shipping label to send your pants in. After they've hemmed your pants, they'll ship them back to you within five business days.

Where to Get Pants Hemmed Quickly: Department Stores

Another option for getting your pants hemmed quickly is to go to a department store that offers alteration services. Many department stores like Nordstrom, Macy's, and Bloomingdale's have in-house tailors who can hem your pants while you wait. This is a great option if you need your pants hemmed quickly and want to try them on before they're altered.

I've had my pants hemmed at Nordstrom before, and the process was straightforward. I brought my pants to the alterations department, and they took my measurements and pinned the pants to the correct length. While I was shopping, they hemmed my pants, and I was able to try them on before leaving the store.

Tips for Getting Your Pants Hemmed Quickly

If you're in a hurry and need your pants hemmed quickly, there are a few tips you can follow to make the process smoother. First, make sure you know your inseam measurement before you go to the tailor. This will save time and ensure that your pants are hemmed correctly. You can measure your inseam by standing with your back against a wall and measuring from the floor to your crotch.

Another tip is to call ahead and make sure the tailor can accommodate your timeline. If you're going to a department store, ask if they have an in-house tailor who can hem your pants while you wait. If you're using an online tailor, make sure to check their shipping and processing times to ensure that you'll receive your pants in time.

Conclusion of Where to Get Pants Hemmed Quickly

Getting your pants hemmed quickly can be a lifesaver when you're in a hurry. Whether you choose to use an online tailor or go to a department store, make sure to follow these tips to ensure a smooth and efficient process. With these options and tips, you'll never have to worry about ill-fitting pants again.

Question and Answer

Q: How much does it cost to get your pants hemmed?

A: The cost of getting your pants hemmed varies depending on the tailor you use and the type of alteration you need. Online tailors like Stitch It charge around $20 for pant hemming, while department stores may charge more.

Q: How long does it take to get your pants hemmed?

A: The time it takes to get your pants hemmed depends on the tailor you use and their processing times. Online tailors like eShakti and Stitch It can have your pants hemmed and shipped back to you within a few days, while department stores may offer same-day hemming services.

Q: Can you hem pants at home?

A: If you have basic sewing skills, you can hem your pants at home. There are many online tutorials and videos that can guide you through the process. However, if you want a professional and polished look, it's best to go to a tailor.

Q: How do I know if my pants need hemming?

A: Your pants should hit at the top of your shoes and break slightly at the ankle. If they're too long and bunch up at the bottom, they need to be hemmed.
