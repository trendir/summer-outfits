What Makeup To Wear With A Black Dress A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Have you ever found yourself staring at your black dress, struggling to decide what makeup to wear with it? It can be overwhelming to choose the perfect combination of colors and styles that complement your outfit. But fear not, we’re here to help you out.

When it comes to choosing makeup to wear with a black dress, there are a few pain points to consider. You want to make sure that your makeup complements your outfit, without overpowering it. You also want to choose colors that suit your skin tone and the occasion you’re attending.

The simple answer to what makeup to wear with a black dress is that it depends on your personal preferences and the occasion. However, we’ve put together a guide to help you make the best choices for your next event.

So, let’s summarize the article's main points related to what makeup to wear with a black dress and related keywords. We’ll cover the best eye makeup, lipstick, and blush colors to wear with a black dress. We’ll also give you some tips on how to match your makeup to your outfit and the occasion. Finally, we’ll answer some common questions about makeup and black dresses.

Eye Makeup to Wear with a Black Dress

When it comes to eye makeup, you want to choose colors that complement your skin tone and the occasion. If you have fair skin, you can opt for light, shimmery colors that add a pop of color to your eyes. For darker skin tones, you can choose bold, dark colors that make your eyes stand out.

Personally, I like to wear gold or bronze eyeshadow with a black dress. I find that these colors complement the black dress without overpowering it. You can also add some black eyeliner and mascara to complete the look.

Lipstick to Wear with a Black Dress

When it comes to lipstick, you want to choose colors that match your skin tone and the occasion. For a casual event, you can opt for a nude or light pink lipstick. For a formal event, you can choose a bold red or burgundy lipstick.

I prefer to wear a nude lipstick with a black dress. It creates a natural look that complements the outfit. However, if I’m attending a formal event, I’ll choose a bold red lipstick to make a statement.

Blush Colors to Wear with a Black Dress

When it comes to blush, you want to choose colors that complement your skin tone and the occasion. For fair skin tones, you can opt for light pink or peach blush. For darker skin tones, you can choose a deeper shade of pink or red.

Personally, I like to wear a light pink blush with a black dress. It adds a subtle pop of color to my cheeks without overpowering the outfit.

Matching Your Makeup to Your Outfit and Occasion

When it comes to matching your makeup to your outfit and occasion, you want to consider the color and style of your dress. If you’re wearing a simple, classic black dress, you can opt for bold, statement makeup. If you’re wearing a more elaborate dress, you can choose more subtle makeup.

For a casual event, you can opt for natural, simple makeup that complements your outfit. For a formal event, you can choose bold, statement makeup that makes a statement.

Personal Experience

Personally, I prefer to keep my makeup simple when I’m wearing a black dress. I’ll wear gold or bronze eyeshadow, a nude lipstick, and light pink blush. This creates a natural, elegant look that complements the black dress without overpowering it.

However, if I’m attending a formal event, I’ll choose a bold red lipstick and add some black eyeliner to my eyes. This creates a bold, statement look that makes me feel confident and elegant.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear bold eyeshadow with a black dress?

A: Yes, you can wear bold eyeshadow with a black dress. Just make sure that the colors complement your skin tone and the occasion.

Q: What color lipstick should I wear with a black dress?

A: The color of lipstick you wear with a black dress depends on your skin tone and the occasion. For a casual event, you can opt for a nude or light pink lipstick. For a formal event, you can choose a bold red or burgundy lipstick.

Q: How can I make my eyes stand out with a black dress?

A: You can make your eyes stand out with a black dress by wearing bold eyeshadow and black eyeliner. Just make sure that the colors complement your skin tone and the occasion.

Q: What color blush should I wear with a black dress?

A: The color of blush you wear with a black dress depends on your skin tone and the occasion. For fair skin tones, you can opt for light pink or peach blush. For darker skin tones, you can choose a deeper shade of pink or red.

Conclusion of What Makeup to Wear with a Black Dress

Choosing the right makeup to wear with a black dress can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. By considering your skin tone and the occasion, you can choose colors that complement your outfit and make you feel confident and elegant. Whether you prefer natural, simple makeup or bold, statement makeup, there’s a look that will suit your style and personality.
