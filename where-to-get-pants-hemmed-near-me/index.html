Many department stores also offer alteration services, including pant hemming. While they may not specialize in alterations like a tailor or dry cleaner would, they can still provide quality service. The Cost of Pant Hemming

The cost of pant hemming can vary depending on the type of pants you have and the level of alterations required. On average, you can expect to pay between $10 and $30 for pant hemming. However, this cost can increase if you have more complex alterations that need to be made.

How to Prepare Your Pants for Hemming

Before taking your pants to be hemmed, there are a few things you can do to ensure that the process goes smoothly. First, be sure to wash and dry your pants according to the care label instructions. This will ensure that any shrinkage has already occurred before the hemming process. In addition, be sure to bring the shoes you plan to wear with the pants to your fitting, so the tailor can hem the pants to the proper length.
