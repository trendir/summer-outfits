What To Wear To A College Bar Guys The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 26, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Heading out to a college bar can be a fun experience, but figuring out what to wear can be a daunting task. You want to look good, but also feel comfortable and fit in with the crowd. This guide will help you navigate the world of college bar fashion and ensure you're dressed to impress.

When it comes to what to wear to a college bar guys, the key is to keep it casual but put together. You don't want to look like you just rolled out of bed, but you also don't want to look like you're trying too hard. Stick to basics like jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers, but make sure they're clean and in good condition.

To elevate your look, consider adding some accessories like a watch, a hat, or a statement piece of jewelry. Just be careful not to overdo it - one or two accessories is plenty.

In summary, what to wear to a college bar guys can be broken down into a few simple rules: keep it casual but put together, stick to basics, and add some accessories to elevate your look.

Jeans and a T-Shirt

When in doubt, jeans and a t-shirt is always a safe bet for a college bar. Opt for a dark wash or black jeans and pair them with a solid colored t-shirt or a graphic tee. This is a classic look that never goes out of style and you'll fit in perfectly with the crowd.

Casual Button-Down

If you want to step it up a notch, consider a casual button-down shirt. This is a great option if you want to look a little nicer but still keep it casual. Pair a button-down with jeans and sneakers for a comfortable but stylish look.

Dress Shoes

If you want to dress up your look even more, consider swapping out your sneakers for a pair of dress shoes. This is a great option if you're going to a slightly more upscale college bar or if you just want to stand out from the crowd.

Layering is another great way to elevate your look. Consider throwing on a denim jacket, a bomber jacket, or a hoodie over your t-shirt or button-down. This not only adds some style to your outfit but also keeps you warm if the college bar is a bit chilly.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear shorts to a college bar?

A: It depends on the dress code of the specific college bar, but in general, it's best to avoid shorts. Stick to jeans or pants to ensure you're dressed appropriately.

Q: Can I wear a suit to a college bar?

A: Unless the college bar is an upscale establishment, it's best to avoid wearing a suit. Stick to casual clothing like jeans and a t-shirt to fit in with the crowd.

Q: Should I wear a belt?

A: Yes, wearing a belt is always a good idea. It not only adds some style to your outfit but also ensures your pants stay up throughout the night.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a college bar?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear a hat to a college bar. Just make sure it's not too flashy or attention-grabbing.

Conclusion of what to wear to a college bar guys

In conclusion, figuring out what to wear to a college bar guys doesn't have to be a stressful experience. Stick to casual but put together basics like jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers, and consider adding some accessories to elevate your look. With these tips in mind, you'll be dressed to impress for your next night out.
