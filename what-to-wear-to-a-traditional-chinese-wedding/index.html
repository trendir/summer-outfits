What To Wear To A Traditional Chinese Wedding

Written by Petter Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Attending a traditional Chinese wedding can be an exciting experience, but it can also be daunting for those who are not familiar with the culture. One of the biggest concerns for guests is what to wear to the wedding. The attire for a traditional Chinese wedding is different from a Western wedding, so it's important to know what is appropriate to wear to avoid any cultural faux pas.

The idea of dressing appropriately for a traditional Chinese wedding can be overwhelming. Guests may worry about not knowing the customs and traditions surrounding the wedding attire. It's important to remember that the couple getting married will appreciate the effort put into dressing appropriately for the occasion.

The attire for a traditional Chinese wedding varies depending on the region, customs, and personal preferences of the couple. However, there are some general guidelines to follow when deciding what to wear to the wedding.

When attending a traditional Chinese wedding, it's important to dress conservatively and avoid wearing anything too revealing. For women, a long dress or a qipao (Chinese dress) is appropriate. It's best to avoid wearing white or black, as these are colors associated with mourning in Chinese culture. Men should wear a suit or a traditional Chinese tunic suit (known as a zhongshan zhuang).

My Personal Experience

As a Chinese-American, I have attended several traditional Chinese weddings. For my cousin's wedding, I wore a red qipao, which is a traditional color for weddings in Chinese culture. I made sure to wear comfortable shoes, as the wedding ceremonies can be lengthy. It was important for me to dress appropriately to show my respect for the couple's traditions and to celebrate their special day.

The Importance of Colors

Colors play an important role in traditional Chinese weddings. Red is the traditional color of weddings, symbolizing happiness and good luck. However, it's important to avoid wearing white or black, as white is associated with mourning and black is considered a somber color in Chinese culture. It's also best to avoid bright colors or patterns that are too bold or attention-grabbing, as the focus should be on the couple getting married.

The Significance of the Qipao

The qipao is a traditional Chinese dress that is appropriate for women to wear to a traditional Chinese wedding. It's a form-fitting dress that typically falls below the knee and has a high collar. The dress is often made of silk or other high-quality materials and can be adorned with intricate embroidery or beading. The qipao symbolizes femininity and elegance, making it a popular choice for weddings.

Accessories to Consider

When deciding on what to wear to a traditional Chinese wedding, it's important to consider accessories. For women, it's best to keep jewelry simple and elegant. Pearls are a popular choice, as they symbolize purity and wealth in Chinese culture. Men should consider wearing a boutonniere or a simple tie clip to add a touch of sophistication to their outfit.

My Final Thoughts

Attending a traditional Chinese wedding can be an enriching cultural experience. Dressing appropriately for the occasion is a sign of respect for the couple and their customs. Remember to dress conservatively, avoid wearing white or black, and consider the significance of colors and accessories when choosing what to wear.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a cocktail dress to a traditional Chinese wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing a cocktail dress to a traditional Chinese wedding, as it may be too revealing for the occasion. A long dress or a qipao is a more appropriate choice.

Q: Is it okay for men to wear a tuxedo to a traditional Chinese wedding?

A: While a tuxedo is a formal choice, it may be too Western for a traditional Chinese wedding. A suit or a traditional Chinese tunic suit is a more appropriate choice.

Q: Can I wear black shoes to a traditional Chinese wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing black shoes to a traditional Chinese wedding, as black is a somber color in Chinese culture. Nude or neutral-colored shoes are a better choice.

Q: Can I wear a fascinator or a hat to a traditional Chinese wedding?

A: It's best to avoid wearing a fascinator or a hat to a traditional Chinese wedding, as it may be too attention-grabbing. Keep accessories simple and elegant.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Traditional Chinese Wedding

When attending a traditional Chinese wedding, it's important to dress appropriately and show respect for the couple's customs and traditions. Keep in mind the significance of colors, avoid wearing anything too revealing, and consider the appropriate attire for the occasion. By putting in the effort to dress appropriately, guests can fully immerse themselves in the cultural experience of a traditional Chinese wedding.
