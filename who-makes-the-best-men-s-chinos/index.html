How to care for your chinos

To ensure that your chinos last as long as possible, it's important to take proper care of them. Be sure to read the care instructions on the label before washing your chinos. In general, it's best to wash them in cold water and tumble dry on low heat. Avoid using bleach and fabric softener, as these can damage the fabric.
