Convert Men s Shoe Size From Au To Eu With Ease

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning to buy shoes online but unsure about the sizing measurements? Do you often get confused when it comes to converting men's shoe size from AU to EU? This can be a frustrating experience, especially when you're unable to physically try on the shoes before purchasing them. But worry not, as we've got you covered with this tutorial on how to convert men's shoe size from AU to EU.

Getting the right shoe size is crucial to ensure comfort and avoid any injuries. However, the conversion from one system to another can be a daunting task. It's not uncommon for people to end up with ill-fitting shoes due to incorrect conversions. But that's where this guide comes in handy, as we provide you with an easy and accurate method to convert men's shoe size from AU to EU.

The first thing you need to know is that both AU and EU shoe sizes are based on the length of your foot in centimeters. However, the sizing standards are different, which is why you need to convert the size to ensure a proper fit. The AU system uses whole numbers, while the EU system uses half sizes. So, if you wear an AU size 8, your EU size would be 41.5.

To convert your men's shoe size from AU to EU, simply add 31.5 to your AU size. For example, if you wear an AU size 9, then your EU size would be 40.5 (9 + 31.5 = 40.5).

Personal Experience with Men's Shoe Size Conversion from AU to EU

As someone who frequently shops for shoes online, I've had my fair share of struggles with getting the right size. I've often ordered shoes in my usual size, only to find that they either feel too tight or too loose. It wasn't until I learned about the conversion from AU to EU sizes that I was able to get the perfect fit every time.

Common Mistakes to Avoid When Converting Men's Shoe Size from AU to EU

One common mistake people make when converting men's shoe size from AU to EU is using the wrong conversion formula. It's important to remember to add 31.5 to your AU size, and not subtract it. Another mistake is assuming that all shoe brands follow the same sizing standards. Different brands may have slight variations in their sizing, so it's always best to refer to the specific brand's size chart.

Factors to Consider When Converting Men's Shoe Size from AU to EU

When converting men's shoe size from AU to EU, it's important to consider the width of your foot as well. Different brands may have varying widths for their shoes, which can affect the fit. It's always best to measure the width of your foot and refer to the brand's size chart to ensure the perfect fit.

How to Measure Your Foot for Men's Shoe Size Conversion from AU to EU

To measure your foot for men's shoe size conversion from AU to EU, you'll need a ruler, a piece of paper, and a pen. Place your foot on the paper and trace the outline of your foot. Measure the length of your foot from the heel to the longest toe, and refer to the size chart of the brand you're purchasing from. To measure the width, place the ruler across the widest part of your foot and refer to the width measurement on the size chart.

Conclusion of Men's Shoe Size Conversion from AU to EU

Converting men's shoe size from AU to EU doesn't have to be a difficult task. By following the simple formula of adding 31.5 to your AU size, you can ensure a comfortable and proper fit every time. Remember to consider the width of your foot and refer to the specific brand's size chart for the best results. Happy shoe shopping!
