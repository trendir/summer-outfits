Spring Wedding Outfit Inspiration Look Your Best On The Big Day

Written by Aprilia May 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you attending a spring wedding this year and struggling to find the perfect outfit? Don't worry, we've got you covered! Spring weddings can be tricky to dress for, as the weather can be unpredictable and you want to strike the right balance between formal and relaxed. In this blog post, we'll provide you with some inspiration and tips to help you look your best on the big day.

Spring weddings can be a challenge when it comes to outfit inspiration. You want to look stylish and appropriate for the occasion, but you also don't want to freeze if the weather turns chilly. Plus, you don't want to upstage the bride and groom! It can be overwhelming to find an outfit that ticks all the boxes, but with some guidance, you can pull it off.

The target of this blog post is to provide you with spring wedding outfit inspiration that is appropriate, stylish, and comfortable. We want to help you choose an outfit that will make you feel confident and beautiful while also respecting the dress code and the couple's special day.

In this blog post, we'll cover a range of outfit ideas that are perfect for a spring wedding, from dresses to jumpsuits to separates. We'll also provide tips on how to accessorize your outfit and what makeup looks work well for a spring wedding. By the end of this post, you'll have plenty of inspiration to create the perfect spring wedding outfit.

Outfit Inspiration: The Dress

When it comes to spring weddings, dresses are always a popular choice. They're feminine, elegant, and perfect for the season. If you're looking for a dress that is appropriate for a wedding, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, avoid anything too short, tight, or revealing. You want to look classy and refined, not like you're going to a nightclub. Secondly, consider the color and print of the dress. While you don't have to wear pastels or florals, they are a classic choice for spring weddings.

For my cousin's spring wedding last year, I wore a knee-length pastel pink dress with lace detailing. I paired it with nude heels and a clutch bag, and kept my hair and makeup simple with loose waves and a natural look. I felt comfortable and stylish all day, and received lots of compliments on my outfit.

Outfit Inspiration: The Jumpsuit

If dresses aren't your thing, or you're looking for something a bit more unique, consider a jumpsuit. Jumpsuits are a great option for a spring wedding, as they're comfortable, versatile, and can be dressed up or down. When choosing a jumpsuit for a wedding, look for one that is tailored and made from a luxurious fabric, such as silk or satin. Avoid anything too casual or sporty, as it won't be appropriate for the occasion.

For my friend's spring wedding, I wore a navy blue jumpsuit with a cinched waist and wide-leg trousers. I paired it with silver heels and statement earrings, and kept my hair and makeup simple with a sleek ponytail and a bold lip. I felt elegant and sophisticated all day, and was able to dance the night away without worrying about my dress riding up!

Outfit Inspiration: Separates

If you're feeling creative, consider wearing separates to a spring wedding. This is a great way to mix and match different pieces to create a unique and stylish outfit. When choosing separates, look for pieces that are made from luxurious fabrics, such as silk or chiffon, and that have a cohesive color scheme or print. You want your outfit to look intentional, not like you just grabbed the first pieces you saw in your closet.

For a spring wedding last year, I wore a floral print midi skirt with a white silk blouse and nude heels. I accessorized with gold jewelry and a clutch bag, and kept my hair and makeup simple with a low bun and a natural look. I felt feminine and chic all day, and was able to move around and dance without feeling restricted.

Tips for Accessorizing Your Spring Wedding Outfit

When it comes to accessorizing your spring wedding outfit, less is often more. You don't want to overwhelm your outfit with too many accessories or jewelry, as it can take away from the overall look. Instead, choose a few statement pieces that complement your outfit, such as a pair of earrings, a bracelet, or a clutch bag.

For my sister's spring wedding, I wore a simple silver bracelet and stud earrings, along with a small clutch bag. I kept my makeup neutral and my hair in loose waves, and felt comfortable and stylish all day.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to a spring wedding?

A: While there's no hard and fast rule about wearing black to a spring wedding, it's generally not the best choice. Black can be seen as too somber or formal for a spring wedding, which is meant to be a joyful and celebratory occasion. Instead, opt for lighter colors or pastels, or try a bold print or pattern.

Q: What kind of shoes should I wear to a spring wedding?

A: When it comes to shoes for a spring wedding, comfort is key. You'll likely be on your feet for most of the day, so choose shoes that are comfortable and easy to walk in. Avoid anything too high or too strappy, as you don't want to risk tripping or getting blisters. A classic nude or metallic heel is always a good choice, or you could try a stylish block heel or wedge for added comfort.

Q: Can I wear a fascinator to a spring wedding?

A: Fascinators are a popular choice for spring weddings, especially in the UK. They're a great way to add some personality and flair to your outfit, and can be a fun accessory to wear. However, be sure to choose a fascinator that complements your outfit and doesn't overpower it. You don't want to be remembered for your hat, but rather for your overall look.

Q: What makeup looks are appropriate for a spring wedding?

A: When it comes to makeup for a spring wedding, keep it fresh and natural. You want to look glowing and radiant, not overly made up. Opt for a natural-looking foundation, a touch of blush, and a soft, shimmery eyeshadow. Finish off the look with a glossy lip or a bold pop of color, depending on your preference.

Conclusion of Spring Wedding Outfit Inspiration

With these outfit ideas and tips, you're sure to find the perfect spring wedding outfit that makes you look and feel your best. Remember to choose an outfit that is appropriate for the occasion, but that also reflects your personal style and personality. With the right outfit and accessories, you'll be able to enjoy the wedding day in comfort and style.
