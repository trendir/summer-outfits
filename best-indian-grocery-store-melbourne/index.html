India at Home is a popular Indian grocery store in Melbourne that offers a wide range of products, including spices, snacks, sweets, and frozen foods. Spice World

Spice World is another great option for Indian groceries in Melbourne. They have a vast selection of spices, herbs, rice, lentils, and other ingredients, as well as frozen foods and sweets. The prices are reasonable, and the staff is always happy to help you find what you need.

If you are looking for a one-stop-shop for all your Indian grocery needs, Radhe Wholesale & Retail is the place to go. They have everything from fresh produce to snacks, beverages, and cooking essentials. The prices are competitive, and they offer online ordering and home delivery services.
