How to Care for Your Dress Pants

To ensure that your dress pants last for years to come, it's important to take care of them properly. Always check the care label before washing or dry cleaning your pants. If possible, hang your pants to air dry instead of using a dryer. Iron your pants on a low heat setting to avoid damaging the fabric.
