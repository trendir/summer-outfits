How To Rock An Indian Wedding Style Lehenga A Beginner s Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and extravagance, and one of the most important aspects of any Indian wedding is the bride's attire. The lehenga is a traditional Indian wedding outfit that has been around for centuries, and it continues to be a popular choice for brides today. If you're a bride-to-be who is considering wearing a lehenga on your big day, then this article is for you.

Getting the perfect Indian wedding style lehenga can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to the world of Indian fashion. You might be wondering where to start, what to look for, and how to make sure you get the perfect fit. But don't worry – we've got you covered.

What is an Indian Wedding Style Lehenga?

The lehenga is a traditional Indian outfit that consists of a long skirt, a blouse, and a dupatta (a long scarf). It is typically worn by brides at Indian weddings, but it can also be worn by guests and bridesmaids. Indian wedding style lehengas are different from regular lehengas in that they are more ornate and elaborate, with intricate embroidery, beading, and embellishments.

A typical Indian wedding style lehenga consists of three parts: the skirt, the blouse, and the dupatta. The skirt is usually made of a heavy fabric like silk or velvet, and it is often embellished with embroidery, sequins, or beads. The blouse is usually made of the same fabric as the skirt, and it is also heavily embellished. The dupatta is a long scarf that is draped over the head and shoulders, and it is also heavily embellished.

Now that you know what an Indian wedding style lehenga is, let's dive into some tips on how to choose the perfect one for your big day.

Choosing the Perfect Indian Wedding Style Lehenga

When it comes to choosing an Indian wedding style lehenga, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Know Your Budget

Indian wedding style lehengas can range from a few hundred dollars to tens of thousands of dollars, depending on the designer, the fabric, and the amount of embellishment. It's important to know your budget before you start shopping so that you don't fall in love with a lehenga that is way out of your price range.

2. Consider Your Body Type

Indian wedding style lehengas come in all shapes and sizes, so it's important to choose one that flatters your body type. If you're petite, look for a lehenga with a shorter skirt and a more fitted blouse. If you're curvy, look for a lehenga with a flared skirt and a longer blouse.

3. Choose the Right Fabric

The fabric you choose for your lehenga can make all the difference in how comfortable you feel on your wedding day. If you're getting married in the summer, choose a lighter fabric like chiffon or georgette. If you're getting married in the winter, choose a heavier fabric like velvet or silk.

4. Don't Forget the Accessories

Accessories can make or break your Indian wedding style lehenga. Make sure you choose the right jewelry, shoes, and handbag to complete your look. When it comes to jewelry, choose pieces that complement the embellishments on your lehenga.

Personal Experience with Indian Wedding Style Lehenga

As an Indian-American bride, choosing the perfect Indian wedding style lehenga was one of the most important decisions I had to make for my wedding day. I knew I wanted something traditional yet modern, and something that would flatter my body type. After weeks of searching, I finally found the perfect lehenga – a red and gold number with intricate embroidery and beading. I paired it with gold jewelry, a matching clutch, and gold sandals. When I walked down the aisle in my lehenga, I felt like a true Indian princess.

How to Style Your Indian Wedding Style Lehenga

Styling your Indian wedding style lehenga is all about finding the right balance between traditional and modern. Here are a few tips:

1. Keep Your Makeup Simple

Since your lehenga will be heavily embellished, it's best to keep your makeup simple and understated. A natural look with a bold lip is always a good choice.

2. Choose the Right Shoes

When it comes to shoes, choose something comfortable yet stylish. A pair of gold sandals or juttis (traditional Indian flats) would be perfect.

3. Add a Modern Twist

If you want to add a modern twist to your Indian wedding style lehenga, consider wearing a crop top instead of a traditional blouse, or adding a statement piece of jewelry like a choker necklace.

Question and Answer

Q. How do I know if a lehenga fits me properly?

A. A properly fitting lehenga should sit comfortably on your hips without slipping down, and the blouse should fit snugly without being too tight.

Q. Can I wear an Indian wedding style lehenga to a non-Indian wedding?

A. While Indian wedding style lehengas are traditionally worn at Indian weddings, they can also be worn to other formal events like galas or balls.

Q. How do I care for my Indian wedding style lehenga?

A. Indian wedding style lehengas should be dry cleaned after each use to keep them in good condition.

Q. Can I customize my Indian wedding style lehenga?

A. Yes, many designers offer customization options for their lehengas, including changes to the fabric, color, and embellishments.

Conclusion of Indian Wedding Style Lehenga

Choosing the perfect Indian wedding style lehenga can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect outfit for your big day. Remember to keep your budget in mind, choose the right fabric and accessories, and most importantly, choose something that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
