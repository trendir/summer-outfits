5 Great Places to Eat in Bloomington MN

Are you looking for the best places to eat in Bloomington MN? There are many restaurants in the area, but not all of them offer the same quality of food and service. 1. Hazelwood Food & Drinks

Hazelwood Food & Drinks is a casual yet upscale restaurant that offers a wide variety of dishes to suit any palate. The atmosphere is warm and inviting, with a cozy fireplace and comfortable seating. The menu features everything from classic American fare to international cuisine, with a focus on fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

I recently visited Hazelwood Food & Drinks and was blown away by the quality of the food and service. I ordered the grilled salmon with roasted vegetables and was impressed by how fresh and flavorful everything tasted. The staff was also incredibly friendly and attentive, making my dining experience one to remember.

2. Blackstone Bistro

If you're in the mood for Italian cuisine, then look no further than Blackstone Bistro. This cozy restaurant offers a wide selection of classic Italian dishes, including homemade pasta and wood-fired pizza. The atmosphere is intimate and romantic, making it the perfect spot for a date night or special occasion.

I visited Blackstone Bistro with my partner and was delighted by the quality of the food and service. We ordered the spaghetti carbonara and the margherita pizza and both dishes were delicious. The staff was also very accommodating and made us feel right at home.

3. RedRossa Napoli Pizza

If you're a pizza lover, then you won't want to miss out on RedRossa Napoli Pizza. This restaurant specializes in authentic Neapolitan-style pizza, which is made with fresh ingredients and cooked in a wood-fired oven. The atmosphere is casual and family-friendly, making it a great spot for a night out with the kids.

I recently visited RedRossa Napoli Pizza with my family and was impressed by the quality of the pizza. We ordered the margherita pizza and the pepperoni pizza, and both were delicious. The crust was crispy and flavorful, and the toppings were fresh and flavorful. The staff was also very friendly and accommodating.

4. Bloomington ChopHouse

If you're in the mood for a fine dining experience, then Bloomington ChopHouse is the place to go. This elegant restaurant offers a wide variety of upscale dishes, including steak, seafood, and pasta. The atmosphere is sophisticated and upscale, making it the perfect spot for a special occasion.

I visited Bloomington ChopHouse for my anniversary and was blown away by the quality of the food and service. We ordered the filet mignon and the lobster ravioli, and both dishes were cooked to perfection. The staff was also very attentive and made us feel like royalty.

5. Masu Sushi & Robata

For sushi lovers, Masu Sushi & Robata is a must-visit restaurant in Bloomington MN. This trendy spot offers a wide variety of sushi and Japanese-inspired dishes, including robata-grilled meats and vegetables. The atmosphere is hip and modern, making it a great spot for a night out with friends.

I recently visited Masu Sushi & Robata with a group of friends and was impressed by the quality of the sushi. We ordered a variety of rolls and nigiri, and everything was fresh and flavorful. The staff was also very friendly and knowledgeable, making our dining experience one to remember.
