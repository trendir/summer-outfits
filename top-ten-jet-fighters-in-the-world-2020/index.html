F-22 Raptor

The F-22 Raptor is a fifth-generation, single-seat, twin-engine, all-weather stealth tactical fighter aircraft developed for the United States Air Force (USAF). It is the most advanced fighter jet in the world and has a top speed of Mach 2.25. Its stealth capabilities make it nearly invisible to radar, and it can carry a wide range of advanced weaponry, including air-to-air and air-to-ground missiles. The F-22 Raptor is undoubtedly one of the most lethal jet fighters in the world.
