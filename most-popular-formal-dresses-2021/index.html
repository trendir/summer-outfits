Most Popular Formal Dresses 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect formal dress to wear to your next event? Look no further than the most popular formal dresses of 2021. Whether you're attending a wedding, a prom, or a gala, these dresses are sure to make you feel confident and stylish.

Choosing the right formal dress can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. From long gowns to cocktail dresses, there are endless styles to choose from. Not to mention, you'll want to find a dress that flatters your figure and makes you feel comfortable.

In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the most popular formal dresses of 2021. We'll discuss the latest trends, the best styles for different body types, and where to shop for these dresses.

What Are the Most Popular Formal Dresses of 2021?

The most popular formal dresses of 2021 include everything from classic ball gowns to modern slip dresses. Some of the biggest trends this year include off-the-shoulder styles, sheer fabrics, and bold prints. Here are a few of our favorite styles:

The Classic Ball Gown

A ball gown is a timeless choice for a formal event, and it's always in style. This year, we're seeing ball gowns in a range of fabrics, from tulle to satin. Look for a gown with a fitted bodice and a full skirt to create a stunning silhouette.

How to Style a Ball Gown

When it comes to styling a ball gown, keep your accessories simple. Opt for a pair of classic pumps and a statement clutch. You can also add a touch of glamour with a pair of chandelier earrings or a delicate necklace.

The Modern Slip Dress

A slip dress is a great choice if you're looking for something modern and chic. This minimalist style is perfect for a cocktail party or a semi-formal event. Look for a dress in a silky fabric with a simple silhouette.

How to Style a Slip Dress

Since a slip dress is so minimalistic, it's important to add some interest with your accessories. Consider a pair of strappy sandals and some statement earrings to dress up the look. You can also add a blazer or a leather jacket for an edgier vibe.

The Bold Print Dress

If you want to make a statement at your next event, consider a dress with a bold print. This year, we're seeing everything from animal prints to floral patterns. Look for a dress with a flattering silhouette to keep the print from overwhelming your figure.

How to Style a Bold Print Dress

When it comes to styling a bold print dress, keep your accessories simple. Opt for neutral shoes and a clutch to let the dress shine. You can also add a pop of color with your jewelry or a bright lip.

Where to Shop for the Most Popular Formal Dresses of 2021

Now that you know what to look for in a formal dress, it's time to start shopping. There are plenty of great retailers that carry the most popular formal dresses of 2021, both online and in-store.

Some of our favorite retailers for formal dresses include Nordstrom, Bloomingdale's, and Macy's. If you're looking for a more budget-friendly option, check out ASOS or Lulus. These retailers offer a wide range of formal dresses at affordable prices.

Conclusion of Most Popular Formal Dresses 2021

Choosing the perfect formal dress can be a challenge, but with the most popular formal dresses of 2021, you're sure to find something that fits your style and flatters your figure. Whether you opt for a classic ball gown or a modern slip dress, make sure to accessorize with the right shoes and jewelry to complete the look.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the most popular color for formal dresses in 2021?

A: This year, we're seeing a lot of bold, bright colors like fuchsia and emerald green, as well as classic neutrals like black and navy.

Q: What style of dress is best for a curvy figure?

A: A-line dresses are a great choice for curvy figures, as they help to balance out your proportions. Look for a dress with a fitted bodice and a flared skirt to create an hourglass silhouette.

Q: Can you wear a short dress to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear a short dress to a formal event. Look for a dress in a luxe fabric like velvet or silk, and make sure to add some glam accessories to dress up the look.

Q: What shoes should you wear with a formal dress?

A: When it comes to shoes, you'll want to choose something that complements your dress without stealing the show. Classic pumps are always a great choice, but you can also opt for strappy sandals or embellished flats.
