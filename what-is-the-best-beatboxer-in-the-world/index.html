Discovering The Best Beatboxer In The World

Written by Luffy Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Beatboxing is an art form that has been around for decades but has gained massive popularity in recent years. It is a form of vocal percussion that involves mimicking the sounds of a drum machine using just the mouth, lips, tongue, and voice. There are thousands of beatboxers around the world, and it can be challenging to determine the best beatboxer in the world.

If you are a beatboxing enthusiast, you may have wondered who the best beatboxer in the world is. You may have even searched the internet for hours trying to find the answer. The internet is filled with different opinions, and it can be challenging to determine which one is right.

The truth is, there is no one answer to the question of who the best beatboxer in the world is. The beatboxing community is vast and diverse, and there are many talented beatboxers out there. However, there are a few beatboxers who have made a name for themselves and are considered some of the best in the world.

In this article, we will explore some of the most prominent beatboxers in the world and what makes them stand out. We will also provide some tips on how to become a great beatboxer yourself.

My Personal Experience with Beatboxing

As someone who has been interested in music for as long as I can remember, I was naturally drawn to beatboxing. The first time I saw a beatboxer perform, I was blown away by the sounds they were creating using just their mouth. I knew I had to learn how to do it myself.

Over the years, I have watched countless videos of beatboxers performing and have even attended a few live shows. I have come to appreciate the art form and the skill required to be a great beatboxer.

The Best Beatboxers in the World

When it comes to the best beatboxers in the world, there are a few names that always come up. Here are some of the most prominent beatboxers in the world:

1. Tom Thum

Tom Thum is an Australian beatboxer who gained international fame after his TEDx talk went viral. He is known for his ability to create a wide range of sounds using just his mouth and voice. His beatboxing skills have landed him gigs around the world, and he has performed alongside some of the biggest names in music.

2. Reeps One

Reeps One is a British beatboxer known for his unique style and ability to create complex rhythms and melodies using just his mouth. He has won multiple beatboxing competitions and has collaborated with some of the biggest names in the music industry.

3. Alem

Alem is a French beatboxer known for his incredible speed and precision. He has won multiple beatboxing competitions and has toured extensively around the world. His beatboxing skills have landed him gigs with major brands like Red Bull and Coca-Cola.

4. Gene Shinozaki

Gene Shinozaki is an American beatboxer known for his unique sounds and techniques. He has won multiple beatboxing competitions and has performed with some of the biggest names in the music industry.

How to Become a Great Beatboxer

If you are interested in becoming a great beatboxer, here are some tips that can help:

- Watch videos of other beatboxers and learn from them.

- Practice every day and focus on mastering one sound at a time.

- Experiment with different sounds and techniques to develop your own style.

- Join a beatboxing community or attend a beatboxing workshop to learn from other beatboxers.

Question and Answer

Q: Can anyone learn to beatbox?

A: Yes, anyone can learn to beatbox with enough practice and dedication.

Q: How long does it take to become a great beatboxer?

A: It varies from person to person, but it can take several years of consistent practice to become a great beatboxer.

Q: What are some common beatboxing techniques?

A: Some common beatboxing techniques include the kick drum, snare drum, hi-hat, and bassline.

Q: Are there any famous songs that feature beatboxing?

A: Yes, there are many famous songs that feature beatboxing, such as "Where Is My Mind" by The Pixies and "Say It Ain't So" by Weezer.

Conclusion

While there may not be a definitive answer to the question of who the best beatboxer in the world is, there are certainly some beatboxers who stand out from the rest. Tom Thum, Reeps One, Alem, and Gene Shinozaki are just a few of the most prominent beatboxers in the world today. If you are interested in becoming a great beatboxer yourself, remember to practice consistently, learn from other beatboxers, and develop your own style.
