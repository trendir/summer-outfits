The Best Place For A Tailored Suit A Guide To Finding The Perfect Fit

Written by Oliver Jun 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of ill-fitting suits that don't make you feel confident? Do you have a special occasion coming up and want to look your best? If so, it's time to consider investing in a tailored suit. But where is the best place to get one? In this post, we'll explore the options and help you find the perfect fit.

When it comes to finding a tailored suit, there are a few pain points to consider. First, there's the cost. Tailored suits are typically more expensive than off-the-rack options. Second, there's the time commitment. Getting a suit tailored takes time, and you'll likely need to go in for multiple fittings. Finally, there's the fear of not getting the fit you want. If you're investing in a tailored suit, you want it to fit perfectly.

The best place for a tailored suit really depends on your specific needs. Here are a few options:

Option 1: Department Stores

If you're looking for a more affordable option, department stores like Macy's or Nordstrom offer tailored suits. While these suits may not be as high-quality as those from a custom tailor, they can still provide a great fit at a lower price point. Just be sure to do your research and find a store with a good reputation for tailoring.

Option 2: Custom Tailor

If you're looking for the ultimate, bespoke suit, a custom tailor is the way to go. These tailors will take your measurements and create a suit specifically for you. While this option is more expensive and time-consuming, the end result is a suit that fits perfectly and looks incredible.

Option 3: Online Tailors

If you're short on time, online tailors like Indochino or Black Lapel can be a great option. These companies allow you to enter your measurements online and create a suit that's tailored to your specific needs. While you won't get the same in-person experience as you would with a custom tailor, these options can still provide a great fit.

Option 4: Outlet Stores

If you're looking for a deal, outlet stores like Saks Off 5th or Brooks Brothers Outlet can offer tailored suits at a lower price point. Just be sure to do your research and make sure you're getting a high-quality suit that will last.

My Personal Experience

As someone who has gone through the process of getting a tailored suit, I highly recommend going the custom tailor route if you can afford it. While it may be more expensive and time-consuming, the end result is worth it. I felt confident and looked great at my event, and the suit has held up well over time.

Common Questions About Tailored Suits

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a tailored suit?

A: It really depends on the quality of the suit and the tailor you choose. Expect to pay anywhere from a few hundred to several thousand dollars.

Q: How many fittings will I need?

A: Typically, you'll need at least two fittings to ensure the suit fits perfectly.

Q: How long does it take to get a tailored suit?

A: Again, this depends on the tailor and the complexity of the suit. Plan on at least a few weeks for the process.

Q: What if I'm not happy with the fit?

A: Most tailors will work with you to make adjustments until you're satisfied with the fit.

Conclusion

When it comes to finding the best place for a tailored suit, it really depends on your specific needs. Whether you opt for a custom tailor or an online option, investing in a tailored suit is a great way to feel confident and look your best.
