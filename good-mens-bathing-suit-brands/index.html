Good Men s Bathing Suit Brands Find The Perfect Swimwear For You

Written by Luffy Jun 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is just around the corner, and it's time to start thinking about what to wear to the beach or pool. One of the most important things to consider is finding the perfect bathing suit. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we'll explore some of the best men's bathing suit brands and what makes them stand out.

When it comes to finding the right bathing suit, there are a few things to consider. First and foremost, you want something that fits well and is comfortable. You also want a suit that is stylish and reflects your personal taste. And of course, you want something that is high-quality and will last you for many summers to come.

So, what are some good men's bathing suit brands? Here are a few to keep in mind:

1. Orlebar Brown

Orlebar Brown is a luxury swimwear brand that is known for its tailored approach to swimwear. Each suit is crafted with precision and attention to detail, ensuring a perfect fit. The brand offers a range of styles, from classic trunks to shorter swim shorts, so there's something for everyone.

Personally, I've owned a few Orlebar Brown suits over the years and have always been impressed with the quality and fit. They are definitely on the pricier side, but I think they are worth the investment if you want a suit that will last and look great for years to come.

2. Patagonia

Patagonia is a brand that is known for its commitment to sustainability, and this extends to their swimwear offerings. Their suits are made from recycled materials and are designed with both performance and style in mind. They offer a range of styles, from board shorts to swim trunks, and their suits are perfect for everything from surfing to lounging on the beach.

I recently purchased a Patagonia board short for a surf trip, and I was really impressed with how comfortable and functional it was. It's also great to know that I'm supporting a brand that cares about the environment.

3. Birdwell Beach Britches

Birdwell Beach Britches is a classic American brand that has been making surf-inspired swimwear since the 1960s. Their suits are made in the USA and are built to last, with triple-stitched seams and durable materials. They offer a range of styles, from board shorts to shorter swim trunks, and their suits have a timeless, retro-inspired look.

I've always been a fan of Birdwell's classic surf style, and I think their suits are perfect for anyone who wants a timeless, high-quality suit that will last for years.

4. Speedo

Speedo is a brand that is synonymous with swimming, and for good reason. They offer a range of swimwear options, from classic briefs to longer swim shorts, and their suits are designed with performance in mind. Whether you're training for a race or just want a suit that will stay in place during your laps, Speedo has you covered.

While I don't personally own any Speedo suits, I know many swimmers who swear by them. If you're serious about swimming, this is definitely a brand worth considering.

Conclusion of Good Men's Bathing Suit Brands

When it comes to finding the perfect bathing suit, there are a lot of options out there. But by focusing on high-quality, stylish brands like Orlebar Brown, Patagonia, Birdwell Beach Britches, and Speedo, you can't go wrong. Whether you're looking for a classic, tailored look or something more performance-focused, there's a brand out there that will meet your needs.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying a bathing suit?

A: When buying a bathing suit, you should look for something that fits well, is comfortable, and reflects your personal style. You also want to consider the quality of the suit and whether it will last you for many summers to come.

Q: Are expensive bathing suits worth the investment?

A: While it depends on your personal budget and needs, many people find that investing in a high-quality bathing suit is worth it in the long run. These suits tend to be made from better materials and are crafted with more attention to detail, ensuring a better fit and longer lifespan.

Q: What's the difference between board shorts and swim trunks?

A: Board shorts tend to be longer and looser than swim trunks, and are often made from quick-drying materials. Swim trunks are shorter and more fitted, and are often made from stretchier materials that allow for ease of movement.

Q: How do I know if a bathing suit is the right size?

A: When trying on a bathing suit, you want to make sure that it fits snugly but not too tight. You should be able to move comfortably and freely without the suit slipping or bunching up. If you're unsure about sizing, don't hesitate to ask a salesperson or refer to the brand's sizing chart.
