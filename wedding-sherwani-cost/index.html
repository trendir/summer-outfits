Wedding Sherwani Cost What You Need To Know

Written by Luffy Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Weddings are an expensive affair, and one of the most significant expenses is buying a sherwani. A sherwani is a traditional Indian outfit worn by grooms for their wedding day. It is a symbol of elegance and sophistication and can make a groom look like a prince on his big day. However, the cost of a sherwani can often be a pain point for many grooms and their families.

The cost of a wedding sherwani can vary depending on the design, material, and craftsmanship. The cost can range from a few thousand rupees to lakhs of rupees, which can be a considerable expense for many families. Additionally, there are other expenses associated with weddings, such as venue, catering, and decorations, which can make the total cost of the wedding skyrocket.

If you are planning your wedding and are worried about the cost of a sherwani, there are a few things you can do to reduce the cost. You can opt for a simpler design, choose a less expensive material, or even rent a sherwani instead of buying one. However, it is essential to keep in mind that a sherwani is a significant part of the wedding attire and can make a big difference in the groom's overall look.

Factors Affecting Wedding Sherwani Cost

The cost of a wedding sherwani is influenced by several factors, including:

Design

The design of a sherwani plays a crucial role in determining its cost. A more intricate design with embroidery, embellishments, and intricate work will cost more than a simple, elegant design.

Material

The material used to make the sherwani also affects its cost. A sherwani made from high-quality fabric such as silk, brocade, or velvet will cost more than one made from cotton or blends.

Craftsmanship

The level of craftsmanship involved in making the sherwani also affects its cost. A sherwani made by skilled artisans with intricate work will cost more than a mass-produced one.

Brand

The brand of the sherwani also plays a role in its cost. A well-known designer brand will generally cost more than a local or lesser-known brand.

When considering the cost of a wedding sherwani, it is essential to keep these factors in mind and decide which ones you are willing to compromise on to stay within your budget.

Personal Experience with Wedding Sherwani Cost

When I got married, I knew that buying a sherwani was going to be a significant expense. I wanted a sherwani that was elegant and sophisticated, but I also had a budget to stick to. After researching several options, I decided to go for a simpler design made from a less expensive material. I was still able to find a sherwani that looked great and fit within my budget. Looking back, I am glad I made that decision as it allowed me to save money for other wedding expenses.

How to Reduce Wedding Sherwani Cost

If you are worried about the cost of a wedding sherwani, here are a few tips to help you reduce the cost:

Choose a simpler design:

Opt for a sherwani with a simpler design that does not have intricate work or embellishments. A simple, elegant design can still look great and save you money.

Choose a less expensive material:

Instead of opting for high-end materials like silk or velvet, consider materials like cotton or blends that are less expensive.

Rent a Sherwani:

You can also consider renting a sherwani instead of buying one. Renting can be a cost-effective option, especially if you do not plan on wearing the sherwani again.

FAQs on Wedding Sherwani Cost

Q. Can I negotiate the cost of a wedding sherwani?

A. Yes, you can negotiate the cost of a wedding sherwani, especially if you are buying it from a local shop. However, keep in mind that the shopkeeper also needs to make a profit, so try to be reasonable with your negotiations.

Q. How much should I expect to spend on a wedding sherwani?

A. The cost of a wedding sherwani can vary depending on the design, material, and craftsmanship. You can expect to spend anywhere from a few thousand rupees to lakhs of rupees.

Q. Is it better to buy or rent a wedding sherwani?

A. It depends on your personal preference and budget. If you plan on wearing the sherwani again, it may be better to buy it. However, if you do not plan on wearing it again, renting can be a cost-effective option.

Q. Are there any ways to get a discount on a wedding sherwani?

A. You can try negotiating with the shopkeeper or look for sales and discounts offered by the store. Additionally, you can also try buying the sherwani during off-seasons when prices may be lower.

Conclusion of Wedding Sherwani Cost

The cost of a wedding sherwani can be a significant expense for many families. However, there are ways to reduce the cost without compromising on the overall look. By considering the factors that affect the cost and making smart decisions, you can find a sherwani that fits within your budget and still looks great.
