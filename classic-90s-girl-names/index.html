Top Classic 90s Girl Names

1. Emily - This timeless name has been a popular choice for girls for decades. It means “industrious” and “striving,” making it a great option for parents who want to instill a strong work ethic in their child.

2. Ashley - This name was extremely popular in the 90s and has since become a classic. It has English origins and means “ash tree meadow.”

3. Jessica - A popular name from the 90s, Jessica means “God beholds” and has Hebrew origins. It’s a great option for parents who want a classic name with a strong meaning.

4. Sarah - This name has been a popular choice for girls for centuries, and for good reason. It means “princess” and has Hebrew origins.
