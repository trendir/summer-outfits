Traditional Indian Mens Wedding Clothing A Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and extravagance, with the bride and groom being the center of attention. While there is no denying the beauty of an Indian bride decked out in traditional attire, the same cannot be said for the groom. Men are often overlooked when it comes to wedding attire, and finding the perfect outfit can be a daunting task. However, with this guide, we hope to make the process a little easier for you.

When it comes to traditional Indian mens wedding clothing, there are a few pain points that many men face. One of the biggest is the lack of options available. Unlike women's attire, which comes in a variety of colors, styles, and fabrics, men's wedding attire is limited to a few traditional options. Additionally, many men struggle with finding the right fit, as traditional Indian clothing tends to be loose-fitting.

The target of traditional Indian mens wedding clothing is to make the groom look regal and elegant on his big day. The clothing should embody the rich cultural heritage of India and reflect the groom's personality and style.

In summary, traditional Indian mens wedding clothing is limited in options but aims to make the groom look regal and elegant on his wedding day.

Sherwani: The Classic Choice

When it comes to traditional Indian mens wedding clothing, the sherwani is the classic choice. It is a long coat-like garment that is typically worn over a kurta and churidar or pajama pants. The sherwani is usually made of rich fabrics such as silk, brocade, or velvet and is adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments.

Personally, I can attest to the beauty of the sherwani. When my cousin got married last year, I wore a sherwani that was made of silk and had gold embroidery. The outfit made me feel like a prince, and I received many compliments throughout the day.

Kurta Pyjama: The Comfortable Choice

If you're looking for something a little more comfortable, the kurta pyjama is a great option. It consists of a long tunic-style shirt (the kurta) and loose-fitting pants (the pyjama). The kurta pyjama is usually made of cotton or silk and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

At my friend's wedding, I opted for a kurta pyjama as I knew I would be dancing all night long. Not only was it comfortable, but it also looked great and allowed me to move freely on the dance floor.

What to Look for When Buying Traditional Indian Mens Wedding Clothing

When buying traditional Indian mens wedding clothing, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider the fabric. As mentioned earlier, traditional Indian clothing is often made of rich fabrics such as silk, velvet, or brocade. These fabrics not only look great but also feel luxurious to wear.

Secondly, consider the fit. Traditional Indian clothing tends to be loose-fitting, but this doesn't mean it should be ill-fitting. Make sure to get your measurements taken and find a piece that fits you well.

Lastly, consider the embellishments. Traditional Indian mens wedding clothing is often adorned with intricate embroidery, sequins, or beads. Make sure to choose a piece that has embellishments that you like and that reflect your personal style.

How to Choose the Right Accessories

Accessories can make or break an outfit, and this is especially true when it comes to traditional Indian mens wedding clothing. When choosing accessories, consider the color and style of your outfit. For example, if you're wearing a sherwani with gold embroidery, consider pairing it with gold cufflinks or a gold watch.

You may also want to consider a turban or a safa, which is a traditional Indian head covering. These can add a touch of regality to your outfit and are often worn by grooms on their wedding day.

Conclusion of Traditional Indian Mens Wedding Clothing

In conclusion, traditional Indian mens wedding clothing is limited in options but aims to make the groom look regal and elegant on his wedding day. The sherwani and kurta pyjama are two great options, and when choosing an outfit, it's important to consider the fabric, fit, and embellishments. Accessories can also add a touch of regality to your outfit and should be chosen with care.
