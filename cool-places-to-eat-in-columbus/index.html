Discover the Best Places to Eat in Columbus - A Foodie's Guide

If you're a foodie, you know that finding great places to eat can be a real challenge. With so many options out there, it can be tough to know where to start. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the coolest places to eat in Columbus.

Are you tired of eating at the same old chain restaurants? Do you want to try something new and exciting? Then keep reading to find out where the locals go to satisfy their cravings.

In this article, we'll cover some of the best places to eat in Columbus. From trendy cafes to classic diners, we've got you covered. We'll also share some of our own personal experiences so you can get a feel for what each place is like.

High Street Neighborhood

If you're looking for a trendy and vibrant area to explore, head to the High Street neighborhood. Here, you'll find a variety of cool places to eat in Columbus, including Northstar Cafe and Brassica. Both of these restaurants offer fresh and healthy options that are perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

We love Northstar Cafe for its delicious salads, sandwiches, and smoothies. The atmosphere is casual and laid-back, making it a great spot to relax and enjoy a meal. Brassica is another must-visit spot in the High Street neighborhood. This Mediterranean-inspired restaurant serves up tasty pita sandwiches, salads, and sides.

German Village

If you're in the mood for some classic comfort food, head to German Village. This charming neighborhood is home to some of the best places to eat in Columbus, including Katzinger's Delicatessen and Schmidt's Sausage Haus und Restaurant.

Katzinger's is a beloved deli that's been serving up delicious sandwiches and salads for over 30 years. We recommend trying the corned beef or pastrami on rye - it's a local favorite. Schmidt's is another must-visit spot in German Village. This German restaurant is famous for its sausages, pretzels, and cream puffs.

Short North Arts District

If you're looking for a vibrant and artsy neighborhood to explore, head to the Short North Arts District. Here, you'll find a variety of cool places to eat in Columbus, including The Pearl and Bodega.

The Pearl is a trendy gastropub that serves up delicious American fare. We recommend trying the shrimp and grits or the fried chicken sandwich. Bodega is another must-visit spot in the Short North. This casual bar and restaurant serves up tasty tacos, sandwiches, and small plates.

Italian Village

If you're a fan of Italian cuisine, head to the Italian Village neighborhood. Here, you'll find some of the best places to eat in Columbus, including Marcella's and The Market Italian Village.

Marcella's is a lively Italian restaurant that serves up delicious pasta dishes and pizzas. We recommend trying the truffle garlic bread and the spaghetti and meatballs. The Market Italian Village is another must-visit spot in this neighborhood. This Italian market and restaurant serves up fresh-made pasta dishes, sandwiches, and salads.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some of the best places to eat in Columbus?

A: Some of the best places to eat in Columbus include Northstar Cafe, Brassica, Katzinger's Delicatessen, Schmidt's Sausage Haus und Restaurant, The Pearl, Bodega, Marcella's, and The Market Italian Village.

Q: What neighborhoods should I visit to find cool places to eat in Columbus?

A: Some of the best neighborhoods to visit for cool places to eat in Columbus include the High Street neighborhood, German Village, the Short North Arts District, and the Italian Village.

Q: What types of food can I expect to find in Columbus?

A: Columbus is known for its diverse culinary scene, so you can expect to find a variety of cuisines, including American, Mediterranean, Italian, and German.

Q: Are there any vegan or vegetarian-friendly restaurants in Columbus?

A: Yes, there are several vegan and vegetarian-friendly restaurants in Columbus, including Northstar Cafe, Brassica, and Portia's Cafe.

Conclusion of Cool Places to Eat in Columbus

If you're a foodie, Columbus is the place to be. With its diverse culinary scene and trendy neighborhoods, there's always something new and exciting to try. Whether you're in the mood for classic comfort food or fresh and healthy options, you're sure to find something delicious in Columbus.
