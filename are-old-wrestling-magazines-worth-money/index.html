Are Old Wrestling Magazines Worth Money

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have a collection of old wrestling magazines gathering dust in your attic or basement? Are you wondering if they are worth any money or if it's time to finally throw them away? If so, you're not alone. Many wrestling fans and collectors have the same questions about their old magazines.

The thought of parting ways with a collection you've held onto for years can be painful. However, it's important to know the value of your collection before making any decisions.

So, are old wrestling magazines worth money? The answer is yes! Depending on the condition, age, and rarity of the magazine, it could be worth a significant amount of money to collectors.

Old wrestling magazines are not only a great source of nostalgia for fans, but they are also a piece of history. They provide a glimpse into the past and offer a unique perspective on the evolution of the sport. Collectors are willing to pay top dollar for rare and significant issues.

My Personal Experience

I have been collecting wrestling magazines for over 20 years. My collection includes everything from classic issues of Wrestling Observer to more recent publications like Pro Wrestling Illustrated. Over the years, I have sold several magazines for a significant profit.

One of my most valuable magazines is a 1985 issue of Wrestling Observer featuring an interview with Hulk Hogan. The magazine is in excellent condition and is considered a rare find among collectors. I was able to sell it for over $500!

What Makes a Magazine Valuable?

Several factors determine the value of old wrestling magazines. Here are a few things collectors look for:

Rarity

The rarer the magazine, the more valuable it is. Magazines that were only printed in limited quantities or were only available in certain regions are worth more.

Condition

The better the condition of the magazine, the more it's worth. Collectors want magazines that are free from tears, stains, and other damage.

Significance

Magazines featuring significant events or interviews with famous wrestlers are worth more. For example, magazines featuring the first-ever match between The Rock and Stone Cold Steve Austin or interviews with legends like Ric Flair are highly sought after.

How to Determine the Value of Your Collection

If you're interested in selling your old wrestling magazines, it's important to know their value. Here are a few ways to determine what your collection is worth:

Research Online

Do some research online to see what other collectors are paying for similar magazines. Check out sites like eBay and Amazon to get an idea of what your collection is worth.

Consult a Professional

If you have a large collection or are unsure of its value, consider consulting with a professional appraiser. They can give you a more accurate idea of what your collection is worth.

Grade Your Magazines

Magazine grading is a system used by collectors to determine the condition of a magazine. Grading your magazines can help you determine their value. Magazines are graded on a scale from 1 to 10, with 10 being the best condition.

Question and Answer

Q: Are all old wrestling magazines worth money?

A: No, not all old wrestling magazines are worth money. The value depends on factors like rarity, condition, and significance.

Q: Can I sell my magazines directly to collectors?

A: Yes, you can sell your magazines directly to collectors. However, it's important to do your research and make sure you're getting a fair price.

Q: Should I keep my magazines or sell them?

A: It's up to you! If you have a strong attachment to your collection or enjoy looking through them, it may be worth keeping them. However, if you're looking to make some extra money or don't have the space to store them, selling them may be a good option.

Q: What's the best way to store my magazines?

A: Magazines should be stored in a dry and cool place, away from direct sunlight. It's best to keep them in a plastic sleeve to prevent damage.

Conclusion of Are Old Wrestling Magazines Worth Money

Old wrestling magazines are a piece of history and a valuable collectible for wrestling fans. If you have a collection of old magazines, it's worth taking the time to determine their value. Whether you decide to keep them or sell them, knowing their worth can help you make an informed decision.
