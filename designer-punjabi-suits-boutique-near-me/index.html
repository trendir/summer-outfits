Question and Answer

Q. What is a Punjabi suit?

A. A Punjabi suit is a traditional outfit worn by women in the Punjab region of India and Pakistan. It consists of a long tunic called a kameez, leggings called churidar, and a scarf called a dupatta.

Q. What is the difference between a Punjabi suit and a salwar kameez?

A. A Punjabi suit is a type of salwar kameez that originated in the Punjab region. The main difference is that a Punjabi suit has a shorter tunic and a tighter fit, while a salwar kameez can have different lengths and fits.

Q. How much does a designer Punjabi suit cost?

A. The cost of a designer Punjabi suit can vary depending on the design, fabric, and workmanship. It can range from a few thousand to several lakhs of rupees.

Q. Can I wear a Punjabi suit for a formal occasion?

A. Yes, a Punjabi suit can be worn for formal occasions like weddings, parties, and festivals. You can choose a heavier fabric and embroidery for a more formal look.
