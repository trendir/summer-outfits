What Brands Ship Internationally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you ever find yourself browsing online for the perfect product, only to discover that it doesn't ship to your country? It can be frustrating and disappointing to come across this roadblock. Fortunately, many brands have recognized this issue and have started to expand their shipping options to reach customers all over the world. In this post, we'll explore what brands ship internationally and how you can take advantage of their services.

It can be challenging to find the right brand that offers international shipping, especially if you're not familiar with the e-commerce landscape. It's also common to encounter issues such as high shipping fees, long delivery times, and limited product availability. These factors can make the shopping experience less enjoyable and cause you to miss out on great deals and unique products.

Thankfully, many brands now offer international shipping, and it's becoming easier to access their products from anywhere in the world. From fashion and beauty to electronics and home goods, you can find a wide variety of brands that cater to global customers. Let's take a closer look at some of the top brands that ship internationally.

Amazon

As one of the world's largest online retailers, Amazon ships to over 100 countries worldwide. You can find practically anything on Amazon, from books and electronics to clothing and groceries. Amazon also offers several shipping options, including standard and expedited shipping, as well as Amazon Prime for faster and more affordable delivery.

ASOS

If you're into fashion, ASOS is an excellent option for international shipping. This UK-based retailer offers free standard shipping to over 190 countries, as well as express shipping for an additional fee. ASOS features a vast selection of clothing, shoes, and accessories for men and women, including exclusive brands and designer collaborations.

Apple

If you're in the market for a new iPhone, iPad, or MacBook, you'll be glad to know that Apple ships to many countries worldwide. The company offers free standard shipping on most products, with the option to upgrade to express shipping for an additional cost. Apple also provides international warranty coverage, so you can have peace of mind when purchasing their products.

My Personal Experience with International Shipping

As someone who lives in a country where many brands don't ship, I've had to rely on international shipping for many of my purchases. Over the years, I've learned a few tips and tricks to make the process smoother and more affordable.

First, I always check the brand's shipping policy and fees before making a purchase. Some brands offer free shipping over a certain threshold, while others have flat rates or vary depending on the destination. I also look for discount codes and promotions that can help offset the shipping costs.

Another thing to keep in mind is the customs fees and taxes that may apply when receiving international packages. These fees can vary depending on the country and the value of the package, so it's essential to be aware of them before making a purchase.

How to Make the Most of International Shipping

If you want to take advantage of international shipping, there are a few things you can do to make the most of it.

First, consider joining loyalty programs and newsletters from your favorite brands. Many brands offer exclusive discounts and early access to sales for their members, which can help you save money and get the best deals.

You can also use package forwarding services that allow you to ship your purchases to a US address, which then forwards the package to your international address. This can be a cost-effective option, especially if the brand doesn't offer international shipping directly.

Question and Answer

Q: Are there any restrictions on what brands can ship internationally?

A: Yes, there may be restrictions on certain products due to local laws and regulations. For example, some countries prohibit the import of certain foods, cosmetics, or electronics. It's essential to check the customs regulations of your country before making a purchase.

Q: How long does international shipping usually take?

A: It depends on the brand and the shipping method you choose. Standard shipping can take anywhere from 7-21 business days, while express shipping can take 2-5 business days. However, factors such as customs clearance and local holidays can affect the delivery time.

Q: Can I track my international shipment?

A: Yes, most brands provide tracking information for international shipments. You can usually find this information in your order confirmation email or by logging into your account on the brand's website.

Q: What should I do if my international shipment is delayed or lost?

A: If your shipment is delayed or lost, contact the brand's customer service team as soon as possible. They can provide you with updates on the status of your package and help you resolve any issues that may arise.

Conclusion of What Brands Ship Internationally

Shopping online has never been easier, thanks to the many brands that offer international shipping. Whether you're looking for fashion, beauty, electronics, or home goods, you can find a brand that caters to your needs and delivers to your country. By following the tips and tricks in this guide, you can make the most of international shipping and enjoy a seamless shopping experience.
