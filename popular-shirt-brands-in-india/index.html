Popular Shirt Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia May 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of buying shirts that don't fit well or lose their quality after a few washes? Do you struggle to find the right brand that caters to your needs? Well, you're not alone. Choosing the right shirt brand can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available in the market. In this post, we will guide you through some of the most popular shirt brands in India that offer quality, fit, and style.

When it comes to buying shirts, there are several factors to consider, such as fit, quality, style, and price. It can be frustrating to spend money on a shirt that doesn't meet your expectations. That's why it's essential to choose a reliable brand that caters to your needs and preferences.

So, what should you look for in a shirt brand? The answer is simple - a brand that offers quality, fit, style, and affordability. In India, there are several popular shirt brands that meet these criteria, and we will discuss them in detail below.

In summary, choosing the right shirt brand can make a significant difference in your overall look and comfort. It's essential to consider factors such as quality, fit, style, and price when selecting a brand. Now, let's dive into some of the most popular shirt brands in India.

1. Peter England

When it comes to formal shirts, Peter England is a popular choice among Indian men. The brand offers a wide range of formal shirts that are stylish, comfortable, and affordable. I personally love their shirts' fit and quality, and they last for a long time. Peter England also offers a variety of sizes, so you're likely to find one that fits you perfectly.

2. Van Heusen

Van Heusen is a premium shirt brand that offers a range of formal and casual shirts. Their shirts are known for their excellent quality and fit, and they offer a variety of styles to choose from. I have been a fan of Van Heusen for years, and their shirts never disappoint. However, their prices are on the higher side, so they may not be affordable for everyone.

3. Arrow

Arrow is another popular shirt brand in India that offers a range of formal and casual shirts. Their shirts are known for their quality and durability, and they offer a variety of sizes and styles. I have purchased several Arrow shirts over the years, and they have never failed to impress me. However, like Van Heusen, their prices are on the higher side.

4. Louis Philippe

If you're looking for premium, high-quality shirts, Louis Philippe is an excellent choice. The brand offers a range of formal and casual shirts that are made from the finest fabrics and tailored to perfection. Their shirts are known for their excellent quality and fit, but they come at a premium price. However, if you're willing to invest in a high-quality shirt that will last for a long time, Louis Philippe is worth considering.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some affordable shirt brands in India?

A: Some affordable shirt brands in India include Park Avenue, Raymond, and Blackberrys. These brands offer quality shirts at reasonable prices.

Q: What should I look for when buying a shirt?

A: When buying a shirt, you should consider factors such as fit, quality, style, and price. It's essential to choose a brand that offers shirts that fit well, are made from high-quality fabrics, suit your style, and are affordable.

Q: How do I know what size shirt to buy?

A: It's essential to know your measurements when buying a shirt. You should measure your chest, waist, and hips and compare them to the brand's size chart. Most brands offer a size chart that can help you determine the right size to buy.

Q: Can I buy shirts online?

A: Yes, you can buy shirts online from various e-commerce websites such as Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra. However, it's essential to check the brand's size chart and reviews before making a purchase.

Conclusion of Popular Shirt Brands in India

In conclusion, choosing the right shirt brand can make a significant difference in your overall look and comfort. In India, there are several popular shirt brands that offer quality, fit, style, and affordability. Some of the most popular shirt brands in India include Peter England, Van Heusen, Arrow, and Louis Philippe. When buying a shirt, it's essential to consider factors such as fit, quality, style, and price. With this guide, we hope you can make an informed decision when choosing a shirt brand that suits your needs and preferences.
