Brooks Brothers: The Iconic American Brand

When it comes to classic mens shirts brands, Brooks Brothers is a true icon. Founded in 1818, Brooks Brothers has been defining American style for over 200 years. Their shirts are known for their impeccable tailoring, classic patterns, and high-quality fabrics.

Personally, I've been a fan of Brooks Brothers for years. I remember buying my first shirt from them for a job interview and feeling instantly more confident and put together. Since then, I've added several more shirts to my collection and have been impressed with their consistent quality and style.

If you're looking for a classic dress shirt, I highly recommend Brooks Brothers' Non-Iron Dress Shirt. It's made from high-quality Supima cotton and features a classic fit and spread collar. It's also available in a wide range of sizes, so you can find the perfect fit.

Charles Tyrwhitt: Affordable Quality

For those on a budget, Charles Tyrwhitt is a great option for classic mens shirts. Founded in 1986, Charles Tyrwhitt is known for their affordable yet high-quality shirts. Their shirts are designed in London and made from the finest fabrics.

Personally, I've been impressed with the value that Charles Tyrwhitt offers. Their shirts are well-made and stylish, yet they won't break the bank. I also appreciate that they offer a wide range of sizes and fits, so you can find the perfect shirt for your body type.

If you're looking for a classic dress shirt that won't break the bank, I recommend the Charles Tyrwhitt Twill Shirt. It's made from 100% cotton and features a classic fit and semi-spread collar. It's also available in a wide range of colors and sizes.

Fabric Matters: Understanding the Different Types of Shirts

When shopping for classic mens shirts, it's important to consider the type of fabric used. Different fabrics can affect the look and feel of the shirt, as well as its breathability and durability. Here are some common types of shirt fabrics:

Cotton: Cotton is the most common fabric used for classic mens shirts. It's soft, breathable, and durable. There are many different types of cotton, including Supima, Egyptian, and Pima.

Linen: Linen is a lightweight and breathable fabric that is perfect for warm weather. It has a casual look and tends to wrinkle easily.

Poplin: Poplin is a lightweight and tightly woven fabric that is perfect for dress shirts. It has a smooth texture and a slight sheen.

Oxford: Oxford is a thicker and more durable fabric that is often used for casual shirts. It has a textured look and is often woven in a basketweave pattern.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Fit

When it comes to classic mens shirts, the fit is crucial. A poorly fitting shirt can make you look sloppy and unprofessional, while a well-fitted shirt can make you look sharp and put together. Here are some tips for finding the perfect fit:

  • Pay attention to the collar size. The collar should be snug but not too tight.
  • Check the sleeve length. The sleeves should end at your wrist bone.
  • Consider your body type. If you have a larger build, look for shirts with a classic fit. If you have a slimmer build, look for shirts with a slim or tailored fit.
  • Try on different sizes and styles. Don't be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect fit.

Question and Answer

Q: Can classic mens shirts be worn casually?

A: Yes, classic mens shirts can be worn casually. Look for shirts made from more casual fabrics like cotton or linen, and pair them with jeans or chinos.

Q: How do I care for my classic mens shirts?

A: Follow the care instructions on the label of your shirt. Generally, it's best to wash your shirt in cold water and hang it to dry. Avoid using bleach or fabric softener, as they can damage the fabric.

Q: How do I choose the right collar style?

A: The collar style you choose will depend on your personal preference and the occasion. A spread collar is a classic choice for dress shirts, while a button-down collar is more casual.

Q: Can I wear a patterned shirt with a patterned suit?

A: Yes, you can wear a patterned shirt with a patterned suit. However, it's important to choose patterns that complement each other. For example, you could pair a striped shirt with a pinstripe suit.

Conclusion of Classic Mens Shirts Brands

Classic mens shirts are a must-have in any wardrobe. By exploring different brands and styles, you can find the perfect shirt for any occasion. Whether you prefer the iconic style of Brooks Brothers or the affordability of Charles Tyrwhitt, there's a classic mens shirt out there that will make you look and feel your best.
