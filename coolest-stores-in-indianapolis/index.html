The Coolest Stores In Indianapolis A Shopper s Guide

Written by Robby May 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wandering aimlessly through the same old stores? Do you crave a unique shopping experience that you can't find anywhere else? Look no further than the coolest stores in Indianapolis! With a diverse range of shops that cater to every taste and interest, you're sure to find something that catches your eye.

Whether you're a fashionista on the hunt for the latest trends, a foodie searching for artisanal treats, or an art lover in need of inspiration, Indianapolis has something for you. But with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to start. That's where we come in!

In this guide, we'll highlight some of the coolest stores in Indianapolis and what makes them stand out. From eclectic boutiques to hidden gems, we've got you covered. So put on your walking shoes and get ready to discover the best shopping destinations in Indy!

Indy CD & Vinyl

As the name suggests, Indy CD & Vinyl is a music lover's paradise. This independent record store has been a staple of the Broad Ripple neighborhood for over a decade and boasts an impressive selection of new and used vinyl, CDs, and cassettes. But what sets Indy CD & Vinyl apart is their commitment to local music. They host in-store performances and signings by local musicians and sell merchandise from Indy-based bands. It's the perfect spot to discover your new favorite artist or add to your vinyl collection.

Homespun: Modern Handmade

If you're in the market for unique, handmade gifts, look no further than Homespun. This boutique in the heart of Indianapolis features goods from over 300 independent artists and makers. From jewelry to home decor to baby clothes, there's something for everyone. Homespun also hosts workshops and events, so you can learn a new craft or meet the artists behind the products.

Funky Bones

Located in the Indianapolis Museum of Art, Funky Bones is an outdoor sculpture that doubles as a pop-up shop. The sculpture itself is a whimsical take on a human skeleton, with brightly colored benches and picnic tables. But during the summer months, the benches and tables are cleared out and replaced with a rotating selection of unique gifts and souvenirs. It's a great way to support the museum and take home a one-of-a-kind item.

Mass Ave Toys

If you're shopping for a child, Mass Ave Toys is a must-visit. This independent toy store on Mass Ave is packed with classic and contemporary toys, games, and puzzles. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly, and they're happy to help you find the perfect gift for any age. And if you're feeling nostalgic, they even have a section of vintage toys that will transport you back to your childhood.

The Conclusion of Coolest Stores in Indianapolis

Indianapolis may not be known as a shopping destination like New York or Los Angeles, but that doesn't mean it lacks unique and interesting stores. From record shops to handmade boutiques, Indy has something for everyone. So next time you're in town, skip the big box stores and seek out the coolest stores in Indianapolis. You won't be disappointed!
