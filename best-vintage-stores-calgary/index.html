Discover The Best Vintage Stores In Calgary

Written by Petter Sep 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you on the hunt for unique and timeless pieces to add to your wardrobe? Do you love the thrill of the hunt for vintage treasures? Finding the best vintage stores in Calgary can be challenging, but the search is worth it. Vintage shopping is not only environmentally friendly but also allows you to express your personal style in a one-of-a-kind way.

If you're tired of the same old fast fashion brands and want to switch up your style, vintage shopping might be the perfect solution. However, finding the best vintage stores can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to the scene. You may not know where to start, what to look for, and how to determine whether you're getting a good deal or not.

So, where do you begin your search for the best vintage stores in Calgary? The answer is simple - keep reading this post, and we'll guide you through the top vintage stores in the city.

To sum it up, vintage shopping is a great way to update your wardrobe while also being environmentally conscious. However, finding the best vintage stores in Calgary can be challenging. Fortunately, we've got you covered. Keep reading to discover the top vintage stores in the city.

1. The Ruby Slipper Vintage Shoppe

Located in Inglewood, The Ruby Slipper Vintage Shoppe is a must-visit for any vintage lover. The store is filled with unique and high-quality vintage clothing, accessories, and home decor items. The staff is friendly and knowledgeable, making your shopping experience a breeze.

I remember visiting The Ruby Slipper Vintage Shoppe for the first time and feeling like I had stepped back in time. The store's decor and layout are charming and give you a glimpse into the past. I found a beautiful 1960s sundress that fit me like a glove and have been a loyal customer ever since.

2. Peacock Boutique

If you're looking for designer vintage pieces, Peacock Boutique is the place to go. The store carries a range of high-end brands, including Chanel, Prada, and Gucci. The Peacock Boutique team carefully curates their selection to ensure that every piece is authentic and in excellent condition.

While Peacock Boutique may be pricier than other vintage stores, the quality of their pieces is unmatched. I purchased a beautiful vintage Chanel handbag from their store, and it's still one of my most prized possessions.

3. SalvEdge Boutique

SalvEdge Boutique is a consignment store that carries a mix of contemporary and vintage clothing and accessories. The store is a great option if you're looking for current trends alongside vintage pieces. They have a range of items, from high-end designer pieces to more affordable vintage finds.

What I love most about SalvEdge Boutique is their commitment to sustainability. The store only accepts items that are in excellent condition, and they donate any unsold items to local charities. Plus, the prices are reasonable, making vintage shopping accessible to everyone.

4. C'est Classique

If you're a fan of vintage designer pieces, C'est Classique is another store worth checking out. Located in the heart of Kensington, the store carries a range of vintage clothing and accessories from high-end designers such as Yves Saint Laurent and Christian Dior.

While the prices may be higher than other vintage stores, the quality of their pieces is top-notch. I purchased a stunning vintage Yves Saint Laurent blazer from C'est Classique that I still wear to this day.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying vintage clothing?

A: When buying vintage clothing, it's essential to check for any damages, such as tears, stains, or holes. Also, make sure to try on the clothing to ensure that it fits properly. Vintage sizing can differ from modern sizing, so don't be afraid to ask for measurements.

Q: How do I know if a vintage piece is authentic?

A: Authentic vintage pieces will have specific design details, such as the type of stitching, buttons, or tags used. Do some research on the brand and the era the piece was made in to confirm its authenticity. Additionally, purchasing from reputable vintage stores can ensure that you're getting an authentic piece.

Q: How do I care for vintage clothing?

A: Vintage clothing requires special care to ensure that it lasts. Always read the care label and follow the instructions carefully. Avoid washing vintage pieces in the washing machine and opt for handwashing or dry cleaning. Store vintage pieces in a cool and dry place, away from direct sunlight.

Q: Can I still wear vintage clothing if it's damaged?

A: Depending on the damage, vintage clothing can often be repaired. If it's a small tear or hole, a seamstress can quickly fix it. However, if the damage is extensive, it may not be worth repairing.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Stores Calgary

Overall, vintage shopping is an excellent way to update your wardrobe while also being sustainable. Calgary has several amazing vintage stores that cater to every style and budget. Whether you're on the hunt for designer vintage pieces or affordable vintage finds, there is something for everyone. So, grab your shopping bags and head out to discover the best vintage stores in Calgary.
