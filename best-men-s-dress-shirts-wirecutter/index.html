Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a dress shirt with jeans?

A: Yes! Dress shirts can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Pairing a dress shirt with jeans can create a stylish and casual look.

Q: Do I need to wear an undershirt with my dress shirt?

A: It's up to you! Some people prefer to wear undershirts to keep sweat from showing through their dress shirt, while others find them too bulky. If you do wear an undershirt, make sure it's a neutral color that won't show through your dress shirt.

Q: How should a dress shirt fit?

A: A dress shirt should fit snugly around your neck without feeling too tight. The sleeves should be long enough to cover your wrists, but not so long that they bunch up. The body of the shirt should fit close to your torso without being too tight or too loose.

Q: Can I wear a patterned dress shirt with a suit?

A: Yes! Patterned dress shirts can add some visual interest to your outfit. Just make sure that the pattern doesn't clash with your suit or tie.
