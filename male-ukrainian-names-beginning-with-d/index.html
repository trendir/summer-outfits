Famous Male Ukrainian Names Beginning with D

One of the most popular male Ukrainian names beginning with D is Dmytro, which means "earth-lover" or "follower of Demeter," the Greek goddess of harvest. This name has been used in Ukraine since the 10th century and has been worn by many influential figures in Ukrainian history, including Dmytro Yavornytsky, a famous historian and ethnographer.

Another popular name is Denys, which means "to dedicate to Dionysus," the Greek god of wine and fertility. This name has been used in Ukraine since the Middle Ages and has been worn by many famous figures in Ukrainian culture, including Denys Sichynskyi, a renowned composer.

Meanings Behind Male Ukrainian Names Beginning with D
