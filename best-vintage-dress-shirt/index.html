The Ultimate Guide To Finding The Best Vintage Dress Shirt

Written by Aprilia Jun 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you love the classic look of a vintage dress shirt, but struggle to find one that fits just right? Are you tired of settling for shirts that don't quite live up to your expectations? Finding the perfect vintage dress shirt can be a challenge, but with the right knowledge and resources, you can find a shirt that not only looks great but feels great too.

When it comes to finding the best vintage dress shirt, it's important to consider the fit, quality, and style. The ideal vintage dress shirt should have a tailored fit, be made from high-quality materials, and have a timeless design that never goes out of style.

In this guide, we'll explore the world of vintage dress shirts and provide you with everything you need to know to find the perfect shirt for your wardrobe. From where to shop to how to care for your shirt, we've got you covered.

What to Look for in a Vintage Dress Shirt

When shopping for a vintage dress shirt, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to make sure the shirt fits properly. Vintage shirts tend to run smaller than modern shirts, so be sure to check the measurements before making a purchase. You also want to look for shirts made from high-quality materials such as cotton or silk. These fabrics not only feel great but also last longer than cheaper materials.

Another important factor to consider is the style of the shirt. Look for classic designs that never go out of style, such as solid colors, stripes, or subtle patterns. Avoid trendy designs that may look dated in a few years.

Where to Shop for Vintage Dress Shirts

There are many places to shop for vintage dress shirts, both online and in person. Online retailers such as Etsy, eBay, and Poshmark offer a wide selection of vintage shirts from various eras and styles. Local vintage or thrift stores can also be a great place to find unique and affordable vintage shirts.

One thing to keep in mind when shopping online is to read the seller's reviews and check their return policy before purchasing. This will help ensure that you are buying from a reputable seller and that you can return the shirt if it doesn't fit or meet your expectations.

Caring for Your Vintage Dress Shirt

Once you've found the perfect vintage dress shirt, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure it lasts for years to come. Always follow the care instructions on the label, and avoid washing the shirt too frequently. Hang the shirt to dry instead of using a dryer, which can cause shrinkage and damage to the fabric.

How to Style Your Vintage Dress Shirt

There are many ways to style a vintage dress shirt, depending on the occasion and your personal style. For a classic, timeless look, pair your shirt with a tailored suit and dress shoes. For a more casual look, wear your shirt with jeans and sneakers. You can also experiment with different accessories such as cufflinks, ties, and pocket squares to add personality and flair to your outfit.

The Benefits of Wearing a Vintage Dress Shirt

Wearing a vintage dress shirt has many benefits. Not only do vintage shirts have a unique and timeless style, but they are also often made from high-quality materials and have a superior fit compared to modern shirts. Vintage shirts also offer a more sustainable and eco-friendly option than buying new clothing, as you are giving new life to a pre-loved garment.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I alter a vintage dress shirt to fit me better?
A: Yes, a skilled tailor can alter a vintage dress shirt to fit you better. Just be sure to carefully consider the cost of alterations before making a purchase.

Q: How do I know if a vintage dress shirt is authentic?
A: Look for labels or tags that indicate the brand, year, and materials used to make the shirt. You can also do research on the brand and style of the shirt to determine if it is authentic.

Q: How do I store my vintage dress shirt?
A: Hang your shirt on a padded hanger in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid folding or stacking your shirt, as this can cause wrinkles and damage to the fabric.

Q: Can I wear a vintage dress shirt to a formal event?
A: Yes, a vintage dress shirt can be a great option for a formal event. Just be sure to pair it with a tailored suit and dress shoes to complete the look.

Conclusion

Finding the best vintage dress shirt requires a bit of patience and knowledge, but the end result is worth it. By considering the fit, quality, and style of the shirt, and shopping from reputable sellers, you can find a shirt that not only looks great but feels great too. With proper care and styling, your vintage dress shirt can become a staple in your wardrobe for years to come.
