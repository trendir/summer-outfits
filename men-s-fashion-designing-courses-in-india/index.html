Top Men's Fashion Designing Courses in India

One of the top men's fashion designing courses in India is the Bachelor of Design in Fashion Designing offered by the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT). This four-year undergraduate course covers various topics such as fashion illustration, garment construction, textile science, fashion marketing, and merchandising. Moreover, it provides students with an opportunity to showcase their creativity and develop their own fashion designs.

Another top men's fashion designing course in India is the Bachelor of Fashion Technology in Apparel Production offered by the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT). This four-year undergraduate course covers various topics such as garment construction, textile science, quality control, and production management. Moreover, it provides students with an opportunity to learn about the latest technologies and techniques used in apparel production.
