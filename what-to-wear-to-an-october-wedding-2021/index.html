What To Wear To An October Wedding 2021 A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Weddings in October can be a bit tricky when it comes to choosing the right outfit. You don't want to be too hot or too cold, and you want to look stylish and elegant without overshadowing the bride and groom. In this guide, we'll help you navigate the ins and outs of what to wear to an October wedding 2021.

When it comes to attending a wedding, there are a few pain points that can cause stress and anxiety. The first is not knowing what to wear, and the second is feeling uncomfortable in your outfit. We're here to help you alleviate both of these concerns and ensure that you feel confident and comfortable on the big day.

The key to dressing for an October wedding is to choose pieces that are both functional and fashionable. You want to be able to move around and dance comfortably, while still looking chic and stylish.

First and foremost, consider the weather. October can be unpredictable, so it's best to dress in layers. Opt for a dress or suit with sleeves, and bring a wrap or jacket that complements your outfit. You can always take it off if you get too warm.

In terms of colors, jewel tones and rich hues are perfect for fall weddings. Think deep greens, burgundy, navy, and burnt orange. If you're not a fan of bold colors, you can't go wrong with classic neutrals like black, white, and beige.

To add some texture and interest to your outfit, consider incorporating velvet, lace, or embroidery. These details can elevate a simple outfit and make it look more special.

Accessories are also key when it comes to completing your wedding look. A statement necklace, earrings, or clutch can add some sparkle and shine to your outfit. Just be sure not to overdo it – you don't want to steal the spotlight from the bride.

My Personal Experience with October Weddings

I attended my cousin's wedding in October a few years ago, and I was unsure of what to wear. I didn't want to be too hot or too cold, and I wanted to look stylish but not too flashy. I ended up wearing a knee-length burgundy dress with sleeves, and I paired it with black tights and ankle boots. I also brought a black wrap to keep me warm during the outdoor ceremony. For accessories, I wore simple gold hoop earrings and a black clutch. I felt comfortable and confident in my outfit, and I received a lot of compliments on my dress.

Dressing for Different Types of October Weddings

If you're attending a more formal October wedding, like a black-tie affair, you'll want to opt for a floor-length gown or a chic suit. Stick with classic colors like black or navy, and don't be afraid to add some sparkle with your accessories. For a more casual wedding, like a rustic outdoor ceremony, you can be a bit more relaxed with your outfit. A midi dress or skirt with a blouse would be perfect, and you can add some boots or booties to complete the look.

Fabric Choices for October Weddings

When it comes to fabric choices for an October wedding, you'll want to opt for materials that are both comfortable and stylish. Think silk, chiffon, velvet, and lace. These fabrics are breathable and lightweight, but they also have a luxurious feel that's perfect for a special occasion.

Makeup and Hair for October Weddings

When it comes to makeup and hair for an October wedding, you'll want to keep things simple and elegant. A classic red lip or a smokey eye can add some drama to your look, but be sure to keep the rest of your makeup minimal. For hair, a sleek updo or loose waves would be perfect. Just be sure to keep some hair ties and bobby pins on hand in case the wind picks up!

Answering Your Questions About What to Wear to an October Wedding 2021

Q: Can I wear white to an October wedding?

A: It's generally best to avoid wearing white to a wedding, as it's considered bad etiquette. Stick with other colors like jewel tones, neutrals, or bold prints.

Q: Can I wear pants to an October wedding?

A: Yes! Pantsuits and jumpsuits are a great option for a more modern and fashion-forward look. Just be sure to choose a style that's appropriate for the dress code and the venue.

Q: Can I wear open-toe shoes to an October wedding?

A: It depends on the weather and the dress code. If it's still warm and the wedding is more casual, you can definitely wear open-toe shoes. However, if it's chilly or the wedding is more formal, you may want to opt for closed-toe shoes.

Q: Can I wear a fascinator to an October wedding?

A: Fascinators can be a fun and stylish accessory for a wedding, but they're not always appropriate. If the wedding is more formal, you may want to opt for a more classic hat or headpiece instead.

Conclusion of What to Wear to an October Wedding 2021

Choosing the right outfit for an October wedding doesn't have to be stressful. By following these tips and guidelines, you can feel confident and comfortable in your look, while still looking chic and stylish. Remember to dress for the weather, choose colors and fabrics that complement the season, and don't be afraid to have fun with your accessories. Happy wedding season!
