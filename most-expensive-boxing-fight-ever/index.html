So, what is the most expensive boxing fight ever? The answer is the 2015 bout between Floyd Mayweather Jr. and Manny Pacquiao. The fight was held at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada, and was dubbed the "Fight of the Century." Despite these concerns, the future of boxing looks bright.
