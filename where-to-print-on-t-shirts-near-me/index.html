The good news is that there are many options available for printing on t-shirts, and finding the best place to get your custom t-shirts printed can be easier than you think. The key is to know what to look for and where to look.

Screen Printing

Screen printing is a popular method of printing on t-shirts, especially for large quantities or designs with few colors. Screen printing involves creating a stencil, or screen, for each color in the design, and then using a squeegee to push ink through the screen onto the fabric. Screen printing is best for designs with fewer colors, as the cost of each screen can add up quickly.

My personal experience with screen printing was when I ordered 50 t-shirts for my college club. We got them printed at a local print shop, and they turned out great. The process was very smooth, and the shirts looked professional. The only downside was that we had to order a minimum of 50 shirts, which was a bit more than we needed.

Direct-to-Garment Printing

Direct-to-garment printing is a newer method of printing on t-shirts, and it is best for designs with many colors or complex designs. Direct-to-garment printing involves using a specialized printer to print the design directly onto the fabric. This method allows for full-color designs and is great for small quantities or one-off designs.

I used direct-to-garment printing for a custom t-shirt I designed for a family reunion. I only needed one shirt, so this method was perfect for me. The colors turned out vibrant, and the details were crisp. The only downside was that the ink faded a bit after a few washes.

Heat Transfer Printing

Heat transfer printing involves printing the design onto transfer paper and then using heat to transfer the design onto the fabric. This method is best for small quantities or designs with many colors. Heat transfer printing can be done at home using an iron, or at a print shop using a heat press.

I tried heat transfer printing at home for a custom t-shirt I designed for a friend's birthday. It was easy to do, and the shirt turned out great. However, I noticed that the design started to peel after a few washes, so I would recommend using a professional heat press for a more durable result.

Vinyl Printing

Vinyl printing involves using a vinyl cutter to cut the design out of vinyl, and then using heat to transfer the vinyl onto the fabric. This method is best for small quantities or designs with few colors. Vinyl printing is great for adding names or numbers to t-shirts.

My friend used vinyl printing for her son's soccer team t-shirts. She was able to add each player's name and number to the back of the shirt, which was a nice touch. The only downside was that the vinyl started to crack after a few washes, so I would recommend using a professional print shop for a more durable result.

A: Heat transfer printing is generally the cheapest method of printing on t-shirts, especially if you do it yourself at home using an iron.

Q: What is the best method of printing on t-shirts for complex designs?

A: Direct-to-garment printing is the best method of printing on t-shirts for complex designs, as it allows for full-color designs and crisp details.
