The Best Vintage Shopping London Guide Where To Find Hidden Gems

Written by Oliver Sep 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a vintage lover, London is the city for you. With its rich history and vibrant fashion scene, London is home to some of the best vintage shops in the world. However, with so many options, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. Whether you're a seasoned vintage shopper or a newbie, this guide will help you find the best vintage shopping London has to offer.

The target of this guide is to provide vintage enthusiasts with a comprehensive list of the best vintage shops in London, including their locations, specialties, and price ranges. We understand that vintage shopping can be time-consuming and frustrating, so we've done the hard work for you.

In summary, this guide will cover the following:

  • The best vintage shops in London
  • Their locations, specialties, and price ranges
  • Personal experiences and recommendations
  • Tips and tricks for vintage shopping in London

Best Vintage Shopping London: Brick Lane Market

When it comes to vintage shopping in London, Brick Lane Market is a must-visit. Located in East London, Brick Lane Market is a vibrant and eclectic flea market that's been around for over 400 years. Here you'll find everything from vintage clothing and accessories to antiques, furniture, and more.

My personal experience at Brick Lane Market was unforgettable. The atmosphere was buzzing with energy, and the variety of vintage shops and stalls was impressive. I found a beautiful 1920s beaded dress for a steal and a pair of vintage Levi's jeans that fit like a glove. The best part? The prices were reasonable, and the vendors were friendly and knowledgeable.

At Brick Lane Market, you'll find a mix of vintage and retro clothing, from the 1920s to the 1990s. Prices vary depending on the vendor and the item, but you can expect to find some great deals if you're willing to haggle.

Best Vintage Shopping London: Beyond Retro

Beyond Retro is another must-visit vintage shop in London. With multiple locations across the city, including Brick Lane and Soho, Beyond Retro is a vintage lover's paradise. Here you'll find a vast selection of vintage and retro clothing, shoes, and accessories for men and women.

My personal experience at Beyond Retro was overwhelming in the best way possible. The store was packed with racks upon racks of vintage clothing, and I could have spent hours browsing. I ended up finding a beautiful 1950s floral dress that I still wear to this day. The prices at Beyond Retro can be a bit steep, but the quality and selection are worth it.

Best Vintage Shopping London: Rokit

Rokit is a vintage shop with multiple locations across London. They specialize in retro and vintage clothing, accessories, and homeware from the 1920s to the 1990s. Rokit is known for its high-quality vintage pieces and reasonable prices.

My personal experience at Rokit was fantastic. The store was well-organized, and the selection was impressive. I found a beautiful vintage silk scarf and a pair of 1960s sunglasses that I still wear regularly. The prices at Rokit are reasonable, and the staff is friendly and helpful.

Best Vintage Shopping London: Tips and Tricks

When it comes to vintage shopping in London, here are a few tips and tricks to keep in mind:

  • Do your research: Before heading out, do some research on vintage shops in the area and make a list of the ones you want to visit.
  • Bring cash: Many vintage shops and markets only accept cash, so make sure to have some on hand.
  • Don't be afraid to haggle: Many vintage shops and markets allow for haggling, so don't be afraid to negotiate for a better price.
  • Try everything on: Vintage sizing can be very different from modern sizing, so make sure to try everything on before purchasing.

Question and Answer:

Q: What are some other vintage shops in London worth checking out?

A: Other vintage shops worth checking out in London include Blitz London, Cenci Vintage, and The East End Thrift Store.

Q: What should I look for when vintage shopping in London?

A: Look for unique and high-quality pieces that you won't find anywhere else. Also, make sure to check for any damage or wear and tear before purchasing.

Q: How can I make the most out of my vintage shopping experience in London?

A: Take your time and enjoy the experience. Vintage shopping is all about discovering hidden gems and unique pieces, so don't rush it.

Q: How can I incorporate vintage pieces into my modern wardrobe?

A: Vintage pieces can be a great way to add personality and uniqueness to your modern wardrobe. Try pairing a vintage blouse with modern jeans or a vintage dress with modern shoes.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Shopping London

In conclusion, London is a vintage lover's paradise. With its vast selection of vintage shops and markets, you're sure to find some hidden gems. Whether you're looking for a 1920s beaded dress or a pair of vintage Levi's jeans, London has it all. So, grab some cash, do your research, and get ready for a vintage shopping adventure.
