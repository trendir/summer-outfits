Discovering Traditional Indian Clothing A Beginner s Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 · 5 min read

India is a country known for its rich culture and traditions, including its vibrant and colorful clothing. However, for those who are new to Indian fashion, understanding what traditional Indian clothing entails can be quite confusing. From sarees to kurtas, there are numerous types of clothing that are considered traditional in India. In this article, we will delve into what traditional Indian clothing is all about, and provide you with a comprehensive guide to help you get started.

When it comes to traditional Indian clothing, there are a few pain points that people often encounter. Some of the most common concerns include not knowing where to start, feeling overwhelmed by the vast array of styles and designs, and struggling to find clothing that fits well. Additionally, people may be unsure about the cultural significance of certain types of clothing, and may be hesitant to wear them for fear of cultural appropriation.

Traditional Indian clothing encompasses a wide range of styles, fabrics, and designs that are unique to different regions of the country. Some of the most popular types of traditional clothing include sarees, salwar kameez, lehenga cholis, and kurtas. Each of these styles has its own unique history and cultural significance, and is typically worn for specific occasions or events.

In summary, traditional Indian clothing is a vibrant and diverse world of fashion that is steeped in culture and history. Whether you are looking to wear traditional Indian clothing for a special occasion, or simply want to learn more about this fascinating aspect of Indian culture, there is something for everyone to discover.

What is Traditional Indian Clothing?

As mentioned earlier, traditional Indian clothing consists of a wide variety of styles and designs that are unique to different regions of the country. To provide a more in-depth understanding of what traditional Indian clothing entails, let us take a closer look at some of the most popular styles:

Sarees

Sarees are perhaps the most iconic of all traditional Indian clothing styles. They are typically made from silk or cotton, and consist of a long piece of fabric that is draped around the body in a specific manner. Sarees are typically worn for special occasions such as weddings or religious ceremonies, and are available in a wide range of colors and designs.

Salwar Kameez

Salwar kameez is another popular type of traditional Indian clothing. It consists of a long tunic top (kameez) and a pair of loose-fitting pants (salwar). The outfit is typically paired with a dupatta (a long scarf) that is draped over the head or shoulders. Salwar kameez is a versatile style that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions.

Lehenga Cholis

Lehenga cholis are a type of traditional Indian clothing that is typically worn for weddings and other formal events. The outfit consists of a long skirt (the lehenga), a fitted blouse (the choli), and a dupatta. Lehenga cholis are often heavily embroidered and embellished with beads and sequins, and are available in a wide range of colors and designs.

Kurtas

Kurtas are a type of tunic that is typically worn by both men and women. They are made from cotton or silk and are available in a wide range of colors and designs. Kurtas can be paired with leggings, churidar pants, or jeans, and are a versatile style that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions.

Indian Clothing: My Personal Experience

As an Indian-American, I grew up wearing traditional Indian clothing for special occasions such as weddings and religious ceremonies. One of my favorite styles is the saree, which I find to be both elegant and sophisticated. I also love wearing salwar kameez, which is a comfortable and versatile style that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Where to Buy Traditional Indian Clothing

If you are looking to purchase traditional Indian clothing, there are a number of options available to you. Many major cities have Indian clothing stores that specialize in traditional clothing, and there are also a number of online retailers that offer a wide selection of styles and designs. Some popular online retailers include Utsav Fashion, Indian Cloth Store, and Cbazaar.

Question and Answer

Q: Can non-Indians wear traditional Indian clothing?

A: Yes, anyone can wear traditional Indian clothing! However, it is important to be respectful of the cultural significance of the clothing and to avoid appropriating it.

Q: How do I know what size to get when buying traditional Indian clothing?

A: Traditional Indian clothing sizes can be quite different from Western sizes. It is best to refer to the size chart provided by the retailer or to consult with a tailor to ensure that you get the right fit.

Q: How do I care for my traditional Indian clothing?

A: The care instructions for traditional Indian clothing can vary depending on the fabric and design. In general, it is best to follow the care instructions provided by the retailer and to avoid machine washing or drying.

Q: What should I wear with a saree?

A: Sarees are typically paired with a blouse and petticoat. The blouse can be either short-sleeved or long-sleeved, and can be made from a variety of fabrics. The petticoat is worn underneath the saree to provide support and shape.

Conclusion of What is Traditional Indian Clothing

In conclusion, traditional Indian clothing is a diverse and colorful world of fashion that is steeped in culture and history. Whether you are looking to wear traditional Indian clothing for a special occasion or simply want to learn more about this fascinating aspect of Indian culture, there is something for everyone to discover. With a little bit of research and some experimentation, you can find the perfect traditional Indian clothing style that suits your personality and fashion sense.
