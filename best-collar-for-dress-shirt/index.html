Best Best Collar For Dress Shirt For Formal Or Cassual

Written by Luffy Jun 12, 2023 · 4 min read

In today's world, dressing well is not only about looking good but also feeling good. A dress shirt is an essential part of any man's wardrobe, and choosing the right collar adds an extra touch of elegance. The best collar for a dress shirt can make or break the entire look, so it's crucial to choose wisely.

When it comes to choosing the best collar for a dress shirt, there are several pain points to consider. Firstly, it can be challenging to find a collar that suits your face shape and body type. Secondly, the collar's height and spread can affect the overall look of the shirt, making it difficult to choose the right one.

The target of this article is to help you find the best collar for your dress shirt. We will provide you with some tips and tricks to choose the perfect collar that complements your features and enhances your personal style.

In summary, choosing the perfect collar for your dress shirt is essential to make the right impression. It can be a daunting task, but we're here to help. We'll take you through the different types of collars and what to consider when choosing one. Let's get started.

Classic Point Collar

One of the most common collars is the classic point collar. It's a timeless option that suits most face shapes and body types. It has a narrow spread and a small point that sits nicely under a suit jacket. This collar is perfect for formal occasions, but it can also be dressed down for a casual look.

Personally, I prefer the classic point collar as it's versatile and suits my face shape. It's an excellent choice for a job interview or a formal dinner.

Spread Collar

The spread collar has a wider spread than the classic point collar, making it perfect for men with wider faces. It's a popular choice among men who prefer a more modern and stylish look. It's important to note that the spread collar requires a thicker tie knot to fill the space between the collar points.

I have a wider face and prefer the spread collar as it balances my features. It's an excellent choice for a night out or a wedding.

Button-Down Collar

The button-down collar has buttons that fasten the collar to the shirt, making it perfect for a casual look. It's a popular choice among younger men who prefer a more relaxed and laid-back style. It's important to note that the button-down collar should not be worn with a tie.

I prefer the button-down collar for a casual look, such as a day out with friends or a picnic at the park.

How to Choose the Right Collar

When choosing the right collar, it's important to consider the following:

  • Your face shape
  • Your body type
  • The occasion
  • The tie you plan to wear

Once you have considered these factors, you can choose the right collar that enhances your overall look and complements your personal style.

Wingtip Collar

The wingtip collar is a formal option that's commonly worn with a tuxedo. It has small wings that point upwards, making it perfect for a black-tie event. It's important to note that the wingtip collar should only be worn with a bowtie.

I would choose the wingtip collar for a black-tie event, such as a wedding or a gala.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a button-down collar with a tie?

A: No, a button-down collar should not be worn with a tie.

Q: What collar is best for a job interview?

A: A classic point collar is best for a job interview as it's timeless and professional.

Q: Can I wear a spread collar with a skinny tie?

A: Yes, a spread collar can be worn with a skinny tie as long as the knot fills the space between the collar points.

Q: What collar is best for a casual look?

A: A button-down collar is best for a casual look as it's relaxed and laid-back.

Conclusion of Best Collar for Dress Shirt

Choosing the best collar for your dress shirt can be a challenge, but it's essential to create the right impression. By considering your face shape, body type, and the occasion, you can choose the right collar that complements your personal style. Whether you prefer a classic point collar, a spread collar, or a button-down collar, there's a collar out there that's perfect for you.
