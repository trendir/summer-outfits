Menswear Brands In Lahore A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect menswear brands in Lahore? Do you find it difficult to navigate through the sea of options and find the right fit for your style and budget? Look no further! This guide will help you find the best menswear brands in Lahore that cater to all your needs.

Whether you're looking for formal wear, casual wear, or something in between, Lahore has a plethora of options to choose from. However, with so many brands and stores to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the right fit for you.

The target of this guide is to provide an overview of the best menswear brands in Lahore, their specialties, and price ranges, so you can make an informed decision and find the perfect outfit for any occasion.

Menswear Brands in Lahore: Our Top Picks

As a fashion enthusiast with years of experience in the industry, I have personally tried and tested various menswear brands in Lahore. Here are my top picks:

1. Amir Adnan

Amir Adnan is a brand that needs no introduction. It is one of the most popular and well-established menswear brands in Lahore. Known for its high-quality fabric and modern designs, Amir Adnan offers a range of products from formal wear to casual wear.

I personally love their kurtas and waistcoats, which are perfect for any formal occasion. Their prices range from mid to high-end, making it perfect for those who want to invest in quality clothing.

2. Cambridge

Cambridge is another popular menswear brand in Lahore. It is known for its classic designs and affordable prices. Their collection includes formal shirts, pants, and suits, as well as casual wear.

I have personally tried their formal shirts and found them to be of good quality and comfortable to wear. Their prices range from low to mid-end, making it a great option for those on a budget.

3. Bonanza Satrangi

Bonanza Satrangi is a great option for those looking for traditional wear with a modern twist. Their collection includes kurtas, shalwar kameez, and waistcoats in a range of colors and designs.

I recently bought a kurta from Bonanza Satrangi and was impressed with the quality and design. Their prices range from mid to high-end, making it a great option for those who want to invest in quality traditional wear.

What to Consider When Choosing Menswear Brands in Lahore

When choosing the right menswear brand in Lahore, there are several factors to consider:

1. Quality of Fabric

The quality of fabric is crucial when choosing the right menswear brand. Look for brands that use high-quality fabric that is comfortable to wear and lasts longer.

2. Design and Style

Choose a brand that aligns with your personal style and offers a range of designs that cater to your needs.

3. Price Range

Consider your budget and choose a brand that offers quality clothing within your price range.

Where to Find Menswear Brands in Lahore

Menswear brands in Lahore can be found in various shopping centers and malls, including Emporium Mall, Packages Mall, and Fortress Square. You can also find them in various markets, including Liberty Market, Anarkali Bazaar, and Ichra Bazaar.
