High End Designer Furniture Brands A Guide To Luxury Living

Written by Oliver May 25, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for furniture that can elevate your living space to the next level of luxury? High end designer furniture brands may be just what you need. With their exquisite craftsmanship, attention to detail, and use of premium materials, these brands offer a range of products that cater to the most discerning customers.

However, finding the right designer furniture brand can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the one that suits your taste and style.

In this guide, we will introduce you to some of the best high end designer furniture brands in the market and help you understand what makes them stand out. Whether you are looking for a statement piece or a complete home makeover, we have got you covered.

Why Choose High End Designer Furniture Brands?

High end designer furniture brands are not just about aesthetics. They offer a complete package of functionality, durability, and comfort. When you invest in designer furniture, you are not just buying a piece of décor, you are investing in a lifestyle.

Designer furniture brands use only the highest quality materials such as leather, solid wood, metal, and glass to create furniture that is not only visually stunning but also long-lasting. The craftsmanship that goes into each piece is unmatched, ensuring that your furniture will stand the test of time.

Moreover, designer furniture brands offer a wide range of customization options so that you can choose the perfect piece that fits your unique style and taste. From materials to colors to finishes, you have complete control over the final product.

Top High End Designer Furniture Brands

1. Hermès

Known for their luxurious fashion and accessories, Hermès also offers a range of high-quality furniture that reflects their signature style. Their furniture collection is a blend of traditional and modern design, with a focus on natural materials such as leather, wood, and metal. From sofas to armchairs to dining tables, Hermès has it all.

2. B&B Italia

B&B Italia is a leading Italian furniture brand that has been in the business for over 50 years. They are known for their contemporary designs that are both functional and stylish. Their furniture collection includes sofas, chairs, tables, and accessories, all of which are made with premium quality materials.

3. Minotti

Minotti is a family-owned Italian furniture brand that has been creating high-end furniture for over 70 years. Their furniture collection is a perfect blend of comfort and style, with a focus on clean lines and simple designs. From lounge chairs to dining tables to outdoor furniture, Minotti has something for everyone.

Personal Experience with High End Designer Furniture Brands

As a luxury interior designer, I have had the pleasure of working with several high end designer furniture brands. One of my favorite experiences was working with the Italian brand Poltrona Frau. Their leather sofas are not only stunning but also incredibly comfortable. The attention to detail and craftsmanship that goes into each piece is truly remarkable.

I also love working with the French brand Roche Bobois. Their furniture collection is a perfect blend of French elegance and contemporary design. Their modular sofas are a personal favorite of mine, as they offer endless possibilities for customization.

How to Choose the Right High End Designer Furniture Brand

Choosing the right high end designer furniture brand can be a difficult task, but there are a few things that you can keep in mind to make the process easier:

1. Consider your budget: High end designer furniture can be expensive, so it is important to set a budget and stick to it. However, keep in mind that investing in quality furniture can save you money in the long run, as you will not need to replace it as often.

2. Look for quality materials: High end designer furniture brands use only the best materials, so make sure to choose a brand that offers products made with premium quality materials such as leather, wood, and metal.

3. Consider the style: Designer furniture brands offer a wide range of styles, from traditional to modern to contemporary. Choose a brand that aligns with your personal style and taste.

FAQs

Q: Are high end designer furniture brands worth the investment?

A: Yes, high end designer furniture brands are worth the investment. They offer quality, durability, and customization options that are unmatched by other furniture brands.

Q: How can I tell if a high end designer furniture brand is authentic?

A: Look for a certificate of authenticity or a label that indicates the brand name. You can also do some research online to verify the brand's authenticity.

Q: Can I customize high end designer furniture?

A: Yes, most high end designer furniture brands offer customization options for their products. You can choose the materials, colors, and finishes that best suit your style and taste.

Q: How do I care for high end designer furniture?

A: Follow the manufacturer's instructions for care and maintenance. Use only recommended cleaning products and avoid exposing the furniture to direct sunlight or extreme temperatures.

Conclusion of High End Designer Furniture Brands

High end designer furniture brands offer a range of products that cater to the most discerning customers. Whether you are looking for a statement piece or a complete home makeover, there is a designer furniture brand out there that can meet your needs. When investing in high end designer furniture, consider the quality of materials, customization options, and your personal style and taste. With the right designer furniture brand, you can transform your living space into a luxurious oasis.
