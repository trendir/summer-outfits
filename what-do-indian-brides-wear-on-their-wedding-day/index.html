What Do Indian Brides Wear On Their Wedding Day

Written by Robby Oct 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and extravagance, and one of the most significant aspects of an Indian wedding is the attire of the bride. The bride's wedding dress is not just a garment but a symbol of tradition, culture, and values. It is an essential part of the wedding ceremony, and every bride wants to look her best on her wedding day.

Choosing the perfect wedding dress can be a daunting task for any bride, and it is no different for Indian brides. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide what to wear on the big day. From sarees to lehengas, there are various traditional and modern outfits that Indian brides can choose from.

The traditional dress for Indian brides is a saree, but nowadays, many brides prefer to wear a lehenga or a fusion outfit that combines traditional and modern elements. The choice of dress largely depends on the bride's personal preference, the region, and the customs and traditions of the family.

In this article, we will discuss in detail what Indian brides wear on their wedding day, the different types of wedding dresses, and the significance of each attire.

Traditional Indian Bridal Attire

As mentioned earlier, the traditional dress for Indian brides is a saree. A saree is a long piece of cloth that is draped around the body and is usually made of silk or other luxurious fabrics. The saree is accompanied by a blouse and a petticoat. The blouse is worn on the upper body, and the petticoat is worn underneath the saree to hold it in place. The saree is draped in various styles depending on the region, and every style has its unique significance.

Another traditional attire for Indian brides is the lehenga choli. A lehenga is a long skirt that is paired with a blouse and a dupatta. The skirt and blouse are usually heavily embellished with embroidery, sequins, and other decorations, making it a popular choice for brides who want to look glamorous and traditional at the same time.

Modern Indian Bridal Attire

With the changing times, Indian brides are also experimenting with modern wedding dresses that combine traditional and contemporary elements. One such outfit is the Indo-western gown that features a fusion of Indian and western styles. The gown is usually made of luxurious fabrics such as silk or velvet and is designed to accentuate the bride's curves.

Another popular modern outfit is the sharara. A sharara is a long skirt that is paired with a short blouse and a dupatta. The skirt is usually made of flowy fabric, giving it a unique look and feel. The sharara is perfect for brides who want to look modern yet traditional.

Significance of Indian Bridal Attire

The attire of an Indian bride is not just a fashion statement but a symbol of tradition and culture. Each garment has its unique significance and is worn with great pride and honor. The saree, for example, represents the grace and elegance of an Indian woman, while the lehenga choli represents the bride's cultural heritage and tradition. The modern outfits, on the other hand, represent the changing times and the fusion of Indian and western cultures.

How to Choose the Perfect Indian Bridal Attire?

Choosing the perfect Indian bridal attire can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be a memorable experience. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect attire:

  • Consider your personal style and preference
  • Research different types of Indian bridal attire
  • Consult with a designer or stylist
  • Consider the customs and traditions of your family
  • Choose a dress that makes you feel comfortable and confident

Personal Experience

As an Indian bride, I chose to wear a red lehenga choli on my wedding day. Red is considered a lucky color in Indian culture, and it symbolizes love and prosperity. My lehenga was heavily embellished with gold embroidery and sequins, giving it a traditional yet glamorous look. I paired it with a matching dupatta and jewelry, which completed my bridal look.

Conclusion of What Do Indian Brides Wear on Their Wedding Day

Choosing the perfect bridal attire is an essential part of an Indian wedding, and there are various traditional and modern outfits to choose from. The attire of an Indian bride is not just a garment but a symbol of tradition, culture, and values. Whether it is a saree, lehenga choli, or a modern outfit, the bride's dress is worn with great pride and honor, making her feel like a queen on her special day.
