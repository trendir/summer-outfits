1. Muhammad Ali – The Greatest of All Time

When it comes to the most well known boxers in the world, there's no question that Muhammad Ali is at the top of the list. Born Cassius Clay, Ali rose to fame in the 1960s, winning the heavyweight title and becoming a cultural icon. He was known for his quick wit, sharp tongue, and incredible athleticism, making him one of the most beloved athletes of all time.

My personal experience with Muhammad Ali was watching his famous fight against George Foreman in the Rumble in the Jungle. His ability to stay calm under pressure and come back from a defeat is something that has always inspired me.

2. Mike Tyson – The Baddest Man on the Planet

Mike Tyson is another well known boxer who has made a huge impact on the sport. He rose to fame in the 1980s and quickly became known as the "Baddest Man on the Planet." Tyson's incredible punching power and ferocity in the ring made him one of the most feared boxers of all time.

My personal experience with Mike Tyson was watching his fight against Evander Holyfield, where he famously bit off a piece of Holyfield's ear. While this was a controversial moment in his career, Tyson's legacy as one of the most dominant boxers in history cannot be denied.

3. Floyd Mayweather – Undefeated Champion

Floyd Mayweather is a boxer who needs no introduction. He is one of the most successful boxers in history, with an undefeated record of 50-0. Mayweather's incredible defensive skills and counterpunching ability have made him one of the most dominant boxers of his generation.

My personal experience with Floyd Mayweather was watching his fight against Manny Pacquiao. While the fight was not as exciting as many had hoped, Mayweather's precision and skill in the ring were undeniable.

4. Manny Pacquiao – The Fighting Pride of the Philippines

Manny Pacquiao is a Filipino boxer who has become one of the most well known boxers in the world. He has won multiple titles in eight different weight divisions, making him one of the most versatile and accomplished boxers of all time. Pacquiao's incredible speed and power in the ring have made him a fan favorite for many years.

My personal experience with Manny Pacquiao was watching his fight against Juan Manuel Marquez. While Pacquiao was ultimately defeated in this fight, his incredible heart and determination were on full display.
