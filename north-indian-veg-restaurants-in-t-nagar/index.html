Exploring the Best North Indian Veg Restaurants in T Nagar

One of the best North Indian veg restaurants in T Nagar is Saravana Bhavan. This restaurant is famous for its authentic South Indian cuisine but also serves delicious North Indian dishes. The restaurant has a cozy ambiance and friendly staff. Their menu includes a wide range of North Indian delicacies, including paneer butter masala, chana masala, and aloo gobi.

Another popular North Indian veg restaurant in T Nagar is Rajdhani Thali. This restaurant serves a wide range of vegetarian thalis that include roti, sabzi, dal, rice, and dessert. Their thalis are a perfect combination of flavors and are reasonably priced.
