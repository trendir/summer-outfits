What to Look for When Choosing the Best Designer Men's Suits in Delhi?

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What is the average cost of a designer men's suit in Delhi?

A1. The average cost of a designer men's suit in Delhi can range from INR 10,000 to INR 50,000, depending on the brand, fabric, and style.

Q2. Can I get a bespoke suit in Delhi?

A2. Yes, you can get a bespoke suit in Delhi from custom tailors such as Herringbone & Sui, Study by Janak, and many others.

Q3. What color suit should I wear to a wedding?

A3. It's best to wear a navy blue, charcoal gray, or black suit to a wedding. Avoid wearing a white suit or anything too flashy that might steal the spotlight from the groom.

Q4. How should a suit fit?

A4. A suit should fit snugly but not too tight. The jacket should fit your shoulders, chest, and waist, while the trousers should fit your hips, thighs, and legs without being too baggy or too tight.
