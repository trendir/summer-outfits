Discover The Best Places To Shop Vintage In London

Written by Bardi Oct 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a vintage lover, and always on the hunt for unique and one-of-a-kind pieces to add to your wardrobe? Shopping vintage can be a great way to express your personal style, but finding the right places to shop can be overwhelming. With so many options available, it's hard to know where to start. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll share the best places to shop vintage in London, so you can find your next favorite piece without the stress.

If you're looking for vintage clothing in London, you may feel overwhelmed by the number of options available. You might be wondering where to start or what stores to visit. You might also be worried about the quality and authenticity of the vintage clothing you find. These are all valid concerns, but with a little guidance, you can find the perfect vintage pieces to suit your style and budget.

The best places to shop vintage in London are spread out across the city, so it's a good idea to plan your shopping trip in advance. Make a list of the stores you want to visit and plan your route accordingly. You can also use public transportation to get around the city quickly and easily. Now, let's dive into the best places to shop vintage in London.

The East End Thrift Store

Located in Whitechapel, the East End Thrift Store is a must-visit for any vintage lover. This store offers a huge selection of vintage clothing, accessories, and homeware at affordable prices. You can find everything from 80s leather jackets to 60s shift dresses to 70s platform shoes. The store also has a great selection of vintage denim and band t-shirts. The best part? All proceeds go to charity, so you can feel good about your purchases.

Beyond Retro

With several locations across London, Beyond Retro is a vintage powerhouse. This store offers a massive selection of vintage clothing for men and women, as well as a curated selection of vintage homeware and accessories. The store is well-organized, making it easy to find what you're looking for. You can also shop online if you prefer. Beyond Retro is a great place to find statement pieces like faux fur coats, sequin dresses, and cowboy boots.

Portobello Road Market

If you're looking for a more eclectic vintage shopping experience, head to Portobello Road Market in Notting Hill. This famous street market is home to numerous vintage clothing stalls, as well as antique shops and independent boutiques. You can find everything from vintage designer pieces to quirky 70s and 80s clothing. The market is also a great place to find vintage accessories like jewelry, hats, and handbags. Just be prepared to haggle for the best prices.

Cabbages & Roses

If you're looking for vintage-inspired clothing with a modern twist, check out Cabbages & Roses in Chelsea. This boutique offers a selection of vintage-inspired dresses, blouses, and skirts made from high-quality fabrics. While the clothing is not technically vintage, it has a vintage feel and is perfect for those who love the vintage aesthetic but prefer new clothing. The store also offers a selection of homeware and accessories.

Conclusion of Best Places to Shop Vintage in London

London is a vintage lover's paradise, with so many amazing places to shop for unique and one-of-a-kind pieces. Whether you're into 60s mod style, 70s boho chic, or 80s punk, there's a vintage store in London that has what you're looking for. From the East End Thrift Store to Beyond Retro to Portobello Road Market to Cabbages & Roses, there are plenty of options to choose from. So, grab your shopping bag and get ready to discover the best places to shop vintage in London!

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best vintage store in London?

A: This is subjective and depends on personal taste, but some of the best vintage stores in London are the East End Thrift Store, Beyond Retro, and Portobello Road Market.

Q: What can I expect to find at a vintage store?

A: You can expect to find unique and one-of-a-kind pieces of clothing, accessories, and homeware. Vintage stores often have a wide selection of clothing from different eras, including 60s mod, 70s boho, and 80s punk.

Q: Is vintage clothing expensive?

A: Vintage clothing can range in price depending on the item and its condition. However, many vintage stores offer affordable options, and you can often find great deals if you're willing to do some digging.

Q: How do I know if a vintage item is authentic?

A: Authentic vintage items will have certain characteristics that are unique to the era they were made in. Look for labels, zippers, and buttons that are indicative of the time period. You can also do some research online to learn more about vintage clothing and how to spot fakes.
