3 Piece Suit Price In Pakistan 2022 Everything You Need To Know

Written by Petter Jun 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you in the market for a new suit but wondering how much you should expect to spend? Look no further than this guide to 3 piece suit price in Pakistan 2022. With so many options and variables, it can be overwhelming to determine what is a fair price to pay. In this article, we will break down the factors that affect the price of a suit and give you a range of what you can expect to pay.

If you have ever shopped for a suit before, you know that the prices can vary greatly. The quality of the fabric, the brand, and the tailoring all play a role in the final price. Additionally, the season and the location of the store can also affect the cost. It can be frustrating to walk into a store with a budget in mind, only to find that the suits that fit your style and needs are out of your price range.

The good news is that there is a wide range of prices for 3 piece suits in Pakistan. You can find options that range from affordable to luxury. The average price for a 3 piece suit in Pakistan is between PKR 5,000 to PKR 100,000. This range includes options for different budgets, styles, and occasions.

Factors That Affect 3 Piece Suit Price in Pakistan 2022

Before delving into specific prices, it is important to understand what factors affect the price of a 3 piece suit. The following are some of the main factors:

Fabric Quality

The quality of the fabric is one of the biggest factors that affects the price of a suit. Higher quality fabrics, such as wool or cashmere, are more expensive than synthetic materials. Additionally, the thread count and weave type can also impact the price. A suit made from a higher quality fabric will not only look better, but it will also last longer.

Brand

The brand of the suit can also greatly affect the price. Designer brands, such as Armani or Gucci, will be more expensive than lesser-known brands. Keep in mind that paying for a brand name does not necessarily mean that you are getting a better quality suit.

Tailoring

The cost of tailoring can also vary depending on the type of suit and the alterations needed. If you purchase a suit off the rack, you will likely need to have it tailored to fit your body. This can add to the overall cost of the suit.

Season and Location

The time of year and location of the store can also affect the price of a 3 piece suit. During peak wedding season, for example, prices may be higher due to demand. Additionally, stores in more affluent areas may charge more for their suits.

My Personal Experience with 3 Piece Suit Price in Pakistan 2022

As someone who has purchased both affordable and higher-end suits in Pakistan, I can attest to the fact that there are options for every budget. When I was in college and needed a suit for job interviews, I found a great option for PKR 5,000 at a local store. While it wasn't the highest quality fabric, it fit my needs and my budget. On the other hand, when I needed a suit for my brother's wedding, I invested in a higher-end suit for PKR 75,000. The difference in quality was noticeable and worth the extra expense for such a special occasion.

Where to Find Affordable 3 Piece Suits in Pakistan 2022

If you are on a tight budget but still want a suit that looks good and fits well, there are options available. Local stores and markets often have affordable options that are suitable for everyday wear. Additionally, online retailers such as Daraz and Kaymu offer a wide range of options at varying price points. Keep in mind that if you purchase a suit online, you may need to have it tailored to fit properly.

How to Save Money on 3 Piece Suits in Pakistan 2022

If you are looking to save money on a suit, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, consider purchasing a suit during off-peak seasons when prices may be lower. Additionally, look for sales or discounts at local stores or online retailers. Finally, consider purchasing a suit off the rack and having it tailored, rather than purchasing a custom suit.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I find a good quality 3 piece suit for under PKR 10,000?

A: Yes, there are many options for 3 piece suits under PKR 10,000. Keep in mind that the quality of the fabric may not be as high as more expensive options, but you can still find a suit that looks good and fits well.

Q: Should I invest in a higher-end suit for special occasions?

A: It depends on your personal preference and budget. If you have a special occasion coming up and want to look your best, investing in a higher-end suit may be worth it. However, if you are on a tight budget, there are still options available that will look good and fit well.

Q: Is it better to purchase a custom suit or one off the rack?

A: It depends on your body type and budget. If you have a difficult time finding suits that fit well off the rack, a custom suit may be worth the investment. However, if you are on a tight budget, purchasing a suit off the rack and having it tailored can save you money.

Q: Are there any tips for finding a good quality suit for a reasonable price?

A: Look for sales and discounts at local stores and online retailers. Additionally, consider purchasing a suit during off-peak seasons when prices may be lower. Finally, don't be afraid to ask for advice from friends or family members who have purchased suits before.

Conclusion of 3 Piece Suit Price in Pakistan 2022

In conclusion, there are many factors that affect the price of a 3 piece suit in Pakistan. However, with a little research and patience, you can find a suit that fits your budget and looks great. Whether you are in the market for an affordable suit for everyday wear or a higher-end suit for a special occasion, there are options available to suit every need.
