How To Put Beads In Your Laces Step By Step Tutorial

Written by Petter May 17, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of boring laces? Do you want to add some sparkle and personality to your shoes? Adding beads to your laces is an easy and fun way to do just that. However, it can be a bit tricky to figure out how to put beads in your laces without them falling off or getting tangled. In this tutorial, we'll show you the best way to add beads to your laces and make sure they stay put.

To get started with how to put beads in your laces, you'll need the following:

  • Laces (of course!)
  • Beads (make sure the hole is large enough to fit your laces through)
  • Scissors
  • Super glue or clear nail polish (optional)

Now, let's get started with how to put beads in your laces:

Step 1: Measure your laces

Measure the length of your laces and decide how many beads you want to add. Keep in mind that adding too many beads can make your laces too heavy and uncomfortable to wear.

Step 2: Thread the beads onto the lace

Take one end of the lace and thread it through the first bead. Slide the bead down to the middle of the lace. Repeat this process with the other end of the lace and thread it through the same bead. This will create a loop of lace with the bead in the middle.

Step 3: Thread the remaining beads

Thread the remaining beads onto the laces, alternating sides with each bead. This will prevent the beads from sliding to one side or the other. Continue until you have reached the desired number of beads.

Step 4: Tie the ends

Once you have threaded all of the beads onto the laces, tie a knot at each end of the laces to keep the beads in place. Use scissors to trim any excess lace.

Optional: Secure the knots with glue or clear nail polish

If you're worried about the knots coming undone, you can secure them with a small amount of super glue or clear nail polish. Apply the glue or polish to the knots and let it dry completely before wearing your shoes.

FAQs About How to Put Beads in Your Laces:

Q: Can I add beads to any type of lace?

A: Yes, you can add beads to any type of lace as long as the hole in the bead is large enough to fit the lace through.

Q: Can I wash my shoes with the beaded laces?

A: We recommend removing the laces before washing your shoes to prevent damage to the beads.

Q: How do I keep the beads from sliding?

A: Alternate sides with each bead to keep them from sliding to one side or the other.

Q: How many beads should I add?

A: It's up to you! Keep in mind that adding too many beads can make your laces too heavy and uncomfortable to wear.

Conclusion of How to Put Beads in Your Laces:

Adding beads to your laces is a fun and easy way to add some personality to your shoes. With these simple steps, you can learn how to put beads in your laces and make sure they stay put. Just remember to measure your laces, thread the beads onto the lace, tie the ends, and secure the knots with glue or clear nail polish if desired. Happy beading!
