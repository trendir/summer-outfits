When you are stranded on a deserted island with limited resources, basic necessities such as clothing can become a major pain point. Clothing can provide protection from the sun, insects, and other elements. However, wearing the wrong type of clothing can lead to discomfort and even health issues.

One of the main reasons why guys wear underwear on Survivor is to prevent chafing. Chafing occurs when skin rubs against skin or clothing, causing irritation and sometimes even bleeding. In addition to preventing chafing, wearing underwear can also provide support and comfort. When you are constantly on the move and engaging in physical challenges, having proper support is crucial. Underwear can help keep everything in place and prevent discomfort caused by bouncing or shifting.

In conclusion, wearing underwear on Survivor is a practical choice for male contestants. It provides protection from chafing, support, and comfort during physical challenges.

Not all underwear is created equal, especially when it comes to survival situations. Male contestants on Survivor often choose underwear that is lightweight, breathable, and quick-drying. Some popular choices include compression shorts, boxer briefs, and trunks.

How to Choose the Right Underwear for Survival Situations

When choosing underwear for survival situations such as Survivor, it is important to consider factors such as comfort, support, and durability.
