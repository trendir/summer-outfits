Types of Indian Textiles Available

Indian textile shops in Dubai offer a wide range of products to choose from. Some of the most popular textiles include sarees, salwar kameez, lehengas, and kurtis. Each of these has its unique style and can be worn for various occasions.

Sarees are the most popular type of Indian clothing and are a versatile piece that can be worn for weddings, parties, and other events. Salwar kameez is another popular option that consists of a long tunic worn over loose-fitting pants. Lehengas are a type of long skirt that is often worn for weddings and other formal events. Kurtis are a shorter tunic that can be worn with pants or a skirt.
